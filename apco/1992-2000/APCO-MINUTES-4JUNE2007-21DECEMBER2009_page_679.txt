The Board of Supervisors participated in creating a unified vision for the community with
representation from the leadership of the community involved in the discussion.
The Board welcomed Hannah Twaddell who has more than 20 years of experience in regional
and local planning, with an emphasis on transportation, land use, community design, and public
participation. Hannah served for fourteen years as chief for the Thomas Jefferson Planning
District Commission and the Charlottesville-Albemarle Metropolitan Planning Organization in
Virginia.
The visioning began with an introduction of each participant present and they also stated their
personal vision.
There was discussion of a common theme among all present.
Each participant was put in a group to work on an group exercise and then each group presented
their vision for the future of Appomattox County and Town.
