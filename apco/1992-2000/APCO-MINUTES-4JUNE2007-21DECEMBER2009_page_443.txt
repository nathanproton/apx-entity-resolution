On Tuesday, February 17, 2009 at 6:30 p.m. the Appomattox County Board of
Supervisors held their regular scheduled meeting at the Appomattox Community Center,
located at 220 Community Lane, Appomattox Virginia.
PRESENT: Samuel E. Carter - Appomattox Courthouse District
William H. Craft — Appomattox River District
Thomas H. Conrad — Falling River District
Russell H. Moore — Piney Mountain District
Gary W. Tanner — Wreck Island District
ALSO PRESENT: = Aileen T. Ferguson - County Administrator
John K. Spencer — Assistant County Administrator
J.G. Overstreet - County Attorney
Vicky Phelps — Administrative Assistant
Chairman Conrad called the regular scheduled meeting of the Appomattox County Board
of Supervisors to order at 6:30 p.m. and asked Mr. Carter to deliver the invocation which
was then followed by the Pledge of Allegiance to the American Flag.
Chairman Conrad called for the setting of the agenda. Added to the agenda was
Administrative Item #7A — Engineering and Design Services; Administrative Item #7-B —
Oakville School Demolition; and Informational Item #15A — Department Report for
Assistant County Administrator.
Chairman Conrad next called for citizen public comment.
Hearing no public comment, Chairman Conrad presented Administrative Item#1-
Drought Response and Water Conservation Measures and called for comment from the
Board.
Mr. Craft made a motion to adopt the Drought Response and Water Conservation
