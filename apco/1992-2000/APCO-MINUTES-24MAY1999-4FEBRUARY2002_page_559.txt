Mr. Gecker provided each Board member with a handout explaining some of the principles involved
in these tax credits.
There are basically three (3) qualifications for Tax Credits:
1. Structure is on the Historic Register
2. Historic District or contributing character to the district
3. Have the Director of the Department of Historic Resources declare the building to be
eligible without going through the nomination process.
Building does not have to be on the register at the time the rehabilitation starts.
