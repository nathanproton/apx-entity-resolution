Mr. Jamerson next asked the Board to consider renewing the
accidental death and dismembership and secondary accidental
medical insurance for the Recreation Department. Invoices
have been received for extending this coverage. After
discussion the Board by consensus asked Mr. Rice to renew
these policies if money for them had been budgeted and if the
fees for the activities were sufficient to cover these costs.
Mr. Jamerson next asked the Board to consider two line item
transfers for the Summer Youth Program. Mr. Torrence made a
motion seconded by Mrs. Beasley to approve transferring
$462.00 from 8102-1002 and $130.00 from 8102-5401 to
8102-5804 and 8102-5501 respectively and $7.00 from 8102-5201
with $6.00 going to 8102-3007 and $1.00 going to 8102-5411.
Vote: Mr. Jamerson, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile,
aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.
Mr. Jamerson next informed the Board the site of preference
for the James River Park as selected by the Central Virginia
Planning District Commission committee is a combined tract
consisting of the Kirk Dance property in Appomattox County
and the adjoining Appalachian Power Company property in
Campbell County. The site selection committee asks if the
Board of Supervisors would object to this site being a State
Park should it be selected. After discussion, the Board
agreed by consensus to support this site for a State Park and
asked Mr. Torrence, the County’s representative on the
Central Virginia Planning District Commission, to so inform
the Committee at the Central Virginia Planning District
Commission Board meeting next Thursday.
Mr. Jamerson next informed the Board he had received a letter
from the Mayor of Alexandria asking the Board’s help to
defeat the proposed Redskins Football Stadium in Alexandria.
No action was taken.
Mr. Jamerson next asked for an executive session and a motion
made by Mr. Torrence seconded by Mr. Carwile, it was resolved
that the Board of Supervisors in accord with Section 2.1-344
of the Code of Virginia enter into an executive session at
10:47 p.m. to discuss personnel concerning hiring a new
Agriculture Extension Agent. Vote: Mr. Jamerson, aye; Mrs.
Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence,
aye.
