c. Planning Commission: Several appointments are needed for the Planning Commission.
Also, the Commission has recommended that the structure of the commission be altered
as follows: 6 County - one for each district (plus a Board Member); two for the Town of
Appomattox and 2 from the Town of Pamplin, to better accommodate appointments from
the Board. This change would also need to be approved by the Towns of Appomattox and
Pamplin before a public hearing can be held.
Mr. Kendrick made a motion seconded by Mr. Carwile to amend the Joint Planning Commission to
eliminate the requirement of a Board of Supervisor member on the Planning Commission and to
notify the Towns of Appomattox and Pamplin. VOTE: Mr. Kendrick, aye; Mr. Carter, aye; Mr.
Hancock, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; and Mr. Moore, aye.
There was some discussion regarding the new and re-appointments to the Planning Commission as
suggested. Mr. Kendrick recommended Mr. Jerry Hughes to represent the Appomattox River
District with Mr. Carter recommending Mr. Steve Conner from the Courthouse District.
Mr. Kendrick made a motion seconded by Mr. Hancock to accept the Planning Commission
