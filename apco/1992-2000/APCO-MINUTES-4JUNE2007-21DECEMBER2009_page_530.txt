On Monday, May 18, 2009 at 6:00 p.m. the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors held a
public hearing at the Appomattox Community Center, located at 220 Park Lane, Appomattox,
Virginia.

Chairman Conrad called the public hearing to order at 6:00 p.m. and explained that the purpose
of the public hearing would be to receive public comment on the proposed Secondary Six Year
Plan for Fiscal Years 2009-10 through 2014-15 in Appomattox County and on the Secondary
System Construction Budget for Fiscal Year 2009-10.
Chairman Conrad welcomed Mr. Don Austin, VDOT Resident Engineer, to the meeting and
called for him to come and report to the Board.
Mr. Austin came forward and gave copies of the six year plan to those in the audience.
Mr. Austin stated that they are later this year having this hearing due to the budget shortfall of
funding and funding projections from last year to this year.
Mr. Austin stated that the handout given to the Board and the audience, is the proposed plan and
the first year will become the effective budget in July. Mr. Austin stated that the total funds for
FY2010 were $278,119 and then pointed out the proposed budget figures for FY2011-FY2015
