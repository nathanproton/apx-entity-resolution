Mr. Moore called the meeting to order at 6:03 P.M. and asked Mr. Carter to give the invocation.
Mr. Moore then called Ms. Sandra Lindsay, VDOT Resident Engineer to address the Board.
Ms. Lindsay presented the Board with warrants for traffic signals. Mr. Carter had previously
requested a study regarding traffic signals at the entrance to the Shoppes of Appomattox.
Ms. Lindsay then explained that the Board needed to adopt a resolution regarding the replacement
of a bridge on Route 629 0.65 miles east of Route 630.
Mr. Kendrick made a motion seconded by Mr. Carter to adopt the following resolution:
WHEREAS, Route 629, 1.046 km. (0.65 mile) East of Route 630, the existing bridge at Little Cub
Creek, due to deterioration, will be replaced as a result of project 0629-006-199, N501, D627
with a pre-cast concrete bridge with concrete footings and
WHEREAS, the pre-cast concrete bridge will be built on the same location as the old structure and
will require demolition of the old structure prior to construction of th new pre-cast concrete
bridge.
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, that this Board concurs in closing this section of
Route 629 to thru traffic for approximately 4 months (beginning approximately 4/01/01 and
ending approximately 8/01/01) for the construction of the new pre-cast concrete bridge.
VOTE: Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Carter, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Hancock, aye; Mr. Kendrick, aye.
Ms. Lindsay informed the Board that a work session would be held on October 23, 2000 at 6:00 P.M.
at the VDOT Office to consider the 6 Year Plan - Secondary Road System. There would also be a
Public Hearing on this on December 4, 2000.
