Mr. Bosiger called the public hearing to order at 7:00 P.M. and explained the purpose of
the hearing was to receive public comment on several zoning petitions. Mr. Bosiger then
explained the process for addressing comments to the Board.
Mr. Bosiger then called on Mr. Caldwell, Clerk to the Planning Commission, to read the
first petition.
Mr. Caldwell read petition number 99-400 of Evie G. Harris for a conditional use permit
to allow additional dwelling on single lot in A-1 zoning district. Property is Tax Map
No. 53-A-14 and is located on Route 616 between Route 24 and Route 631.
Mr. Bosiger called for comments on this petition.
Ms. Evie G. Harris came forward and gave her address as Route 3 Box 61 Appomattox.
Ms. Harris asked the Board to please approve her petition in order that she could have
some help.
Mr. Bosiger called for other comments.
Hearing none, Mr. Bosiger asked Mr. Caldwell to read the next petition.
Mr. Caldwell read petition number of 99-401 of Zane and Karla Summers for a
conditional use permit to allow additional dwelling on single lot in A-1 zoning district.
Property is part of Tax Map No. 19-A-4B and is located on Route 611 between Route
611 and Route 686.
Mr. Bosiger called for comments on this petition.
Ms, Sara Summers came forward and explained that the reason for this request was so
she could put a doublewide on her property so that her son and daughter-in-law could
move into it to take care of her in the future.
Mr. Bosiger called for other comments.
Hearing none, Mr. Bosiger asked Mr. Caldwell to read the last petition.
Mr. Caldwell read petition number 99-402 for Tyrone Scott for a variance to paragraph
170-80A of the Appomattox County Zoning Ordinance pertaining to rear yard setback,,
Property is Tax Map No. 108-A-118 zoned R-2 andis located on Route 636 between
Route 644 and Route 636.
