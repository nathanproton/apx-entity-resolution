Mr. Rice presented a proposal from Appomattox Cleaning Service, Inc. to
provide cleaning services for the County offices. After discussion the
Board asked Mr. Rice to contact Mr. Wilder and get specifics and report
back at the June meeting.
Mr. Rice next requested the Board waive the review fee for two (2)
plats for the Town of Appomattox. Mr. Torrence made a motion seconded
by Mr. Carwile to waive the plat review fee for the Town of Appomattox
on the two (2) well lots. Vote: Mr. Jamerson, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye;
Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.
The Board next discussed consolidating payrolls for the Schools, Clerk
of Circuit Court, Social Services and other County departments. The
Board requested that Mr. Rice contact the departments involved and seek
their input.
Mr. Jamerson read a request for Entertainment Festival Permit and fee
and bond requirement waver from the Historic Appomattox Railroad
Festival Board. Mr. Carwile made a motion seconded by Mrs. Beasley to
approve the Music Festival scheduled for May 23, 1993 and waive fee and
bond requirements. Vote: Mr. Jamerson, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr.
Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.
