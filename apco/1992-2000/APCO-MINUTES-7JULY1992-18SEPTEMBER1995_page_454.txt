Mrs. Ferguson then gave the board some information concerning the
make-up of the Regional Jail Authority, the effect on local employees
and the effect on the Sheriff’s Department.
REGIONAL JAIL AUTHORITY:
Will be made up of two (2) representatives from each participating
locality. (The Sheriff must be one of these representatives.)
All bonds for construction would be issued by the AUTHORITY. (no
referendum required) .
All decisions concerning operation and construction would be made by
the Authority.
The local jail facilities would have to be deeded to the Authority with
the stipulation that in the event the authority no longer wanted to usé
this facility as a jail, the facility would revert back to the locality
at no cost to the locality.

All maintenance and upkeep of the facility would be the responsibility
of the Authority.
Funding for construction comes from the Department of Treasury. If the
concept is approved the Region would be eligible for 50% construction
reimbursement. The Region would have to borrow the total amount, as
the State will reimburse on an annual basis, over the long term of the
loan, bonds, etc.

SERVICE AGREEMENT:
Each locality will have a Service agreement with the Authority.
EMPLOYEES:
All personnel would be employees of the Regional Authority.
Benefits, salaries, personnel policy, etc. would be decided by the
Authority.
Our jail/dispatch personnel are presently covered by the Law
Enforcement Organization retirement with Virginia Retirement System.
This system allows them some special retirement benefits due to stress
involved with their line of employment. Under the Regional Authority
this would not be available.
Personnel could be shifted from facility to facility for short term
emergencies without creating a problem.
