WHEREAS, the grade crossing on this loop road provides public service to thirteen houses
and two farms,
WHEREAS, the closing of the crossing will create a dead end road with only one access
across the Norfolk Southern Railroad and will increase the potential for delay of police, fire
and emergency services to the residents,
NOW THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED, that the Board of Supervisors of Appomattox
County, for the reasons stated herein, vigorously oppose the closure by the Commonwealth
Transportation Board of the portion of Route 674 at the Norfolk Southern Railroad east grade
crossing in Appomattox County.
Vote: Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Kendrick, aye; Mr. Hancock, aye; Mr. Carter, aye; and Mr.
