RESOLUTION
WHEREAS, the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors, is genuinely
concerned with the safety of all its citizens; and
WHEREAS, the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors is not only
concerned with the safety of local citizens; but also those across our
Commonwealth and those individuals who visit our state and area; and
WHEREAS, to abolish the annual state inspection of vehicles would be
detrimental to all motoring public; and
WHEREAS, it is vital to all our citizens to keep a properly equipped
and maintained vehicle on our highways;
NOW, therefore, be it resolved that the Appomattox County Board of
Supervisors, meeting in regular session on October 3, 1994 and all
members present and voting aye, do hereby adamantly oppose the
elimination of annual state inspections.
Vote: Mr. Torrence, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr
Moore, aye; and Mr. Paulette, aye.
