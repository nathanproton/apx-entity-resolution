BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED THAT the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors
does hereby authorize County Administrator Aileen T. Ferguson to sign and execute all grant-
telated documents on behalf of the Board and that Ms. Ferguson accepts all responsibility for the
accuracy of the application and for the appropriate use of the Tobacco Commission funds, should
they be granted.
Chairman Conrad called for discussion, hearing none; Mr. Moore made a motion to adopt the
resolution to endorse the application for Virginia Tobacco Indemnification and Community
Revitalization Commission Southside Economic Development Grant Program funds to aid in the
development of a Broadband Authority serving Appomattox County. Mr. Tanner seconded Mr.
Moore’s motion. Chairman Conrad called for discussion, hearing none, he called for a vote: Mr.
Carter, aye; Mr. Conrad, aye; Mr. Craft, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Tanner, aye.
Chairman Conrad presented Administrative Item#5 — Special Election and explained that due to
the resignation of the Clerk of Circuit Court with effective date of August 31, 2009, Mr.
Overstreet had prepared a petition and an order related to filling the Clerk of Circuit Court’s
unexpired term of office. Chairman Conrad called for discussion.
Mr. Overstreet stated that he had prepared this document per code requirements and a special
election would have to be held in year 2010. Mr. Overstreet explained that the person elected
would begin immediately until the term ends in 2015.
