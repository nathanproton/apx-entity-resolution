Mr. Bosiger then explained that Mrs. Ferguson was requesting the Board adopt the
Financial Forecast for Fiscal Years 2001 through 2005 that had been prepared by
Robinson Farmer Cox Associates, in order to recognize the document as an official
planning document for the County.
Mr. Carter made a motion seconded by Mr. Moore to accept the Financial Forecast for
Fiscal Years 2001 through 2005 as prepared by Robinson Farmer Cox Associates as the
official planning document for Appomattox County. Vote: Mr. Bosiger, aye; Mr. Carter,
aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.
Mr. Bosiger next asked the Board to adopt the Governor’s Christmas Holiday schedule
for County employees.
Mr. Carwile made a motion seconded by Mr. Carter to adopt the Governor’s Christmas
holiday schedule for County employees. Vote: Mr. Bosiger, aye; Mr. Carter, aye; Mr.
Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.
Mr. Bosiger then explained that there were still several committees that required
appointments and it appeared that no one was able to find a person interested in serving.

from the community, if the Board so desired.
