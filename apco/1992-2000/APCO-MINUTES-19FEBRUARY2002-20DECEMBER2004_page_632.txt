10. Resolution of Support for Tourism Signs: Anne Dixon has been working on
placing tourism signs at various locations throughout the County. VDOT has
agreed to install the signs but require a letter of support from the Board and also
agreement that the Board will own and be responsible for maintenance of the
signs.
Mr. Craft made a motion, seconded by Mr. Moore, to issue a letter to VDOT in support of
placing the tourism signs in the county and also to take responsibility for the
maintenance of these signs. VOTE: Mr. Conrad, aye; Mr. Craft, aye; Mr. Moore, aye;
and Mr. Carter, aye. ( 4 aye votes- 1 absent)
11. Water Options: Since the study done by Wiley & Wilson, another option for
