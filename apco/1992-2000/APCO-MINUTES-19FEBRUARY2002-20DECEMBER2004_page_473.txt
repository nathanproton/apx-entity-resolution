3. Resolution Requesting Secondary Road Paving: The VDOT Resident
Engineer has determined that portions of Route 675 are eligible for paving under
the Paved in Place Program. Unlike the Rural Rustic Program, this program
allows for needed upgrades behind the ditch line. VDOT Resident Engineer has
also provided resolutions to be adopted by the Board that will make this an
official request to VDOT.
Recommendation is that the resolutions be adopted and forwarded to the local
VDOT Resident Engineer.
Mr. Craft made a motion, seconded by Mr. Conrad, to adopt the following resolutions for
Paving of Secondary Roads (Route 675).

re-enacted Section 33.1-70.1 of the Code of Virginia relating to the paving of certain
secondary roads using a pave-in-place option, and

body of any county requesting the Virginia Department of Transportation (VDOT) to
consider the hardsurfacing of any secondary road meeting the criteria prescribed by this
