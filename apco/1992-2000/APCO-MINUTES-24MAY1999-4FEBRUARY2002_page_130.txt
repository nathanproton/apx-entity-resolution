A joint work session of the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors and the
Appomattox Town Council was held on November 6, 1999 at 7:30 A.M. in the Town
Office Building, Linden Street, Appomattox, Virginia.
Present (Board of Supervisors):
Garet K. Bosiger — Chairman — Falling River District

Mr. Spiggle opened the work session and welcomed everyone. Mr. Spiggle explained for
the newly elected member of the Board that the Board and Town Council tried to meet
several times a year in a work session to discuss matters of mutual concern.
Mr. Spiggle then talked about the proposed 460 Water line project with Lynchburg. Mr.
Spiggle explained that the City was waiting for Appomattox Town and County to take
the proposed project to the next level. Mr. Spiggle also explained that Region 2000 had
put $50,000.00 in their FY2000 budget to help with expenses involved in carrying this
project forward.
A lengthy discussion followed with alternatives being presented for consideration.
Mr. Bosiger then asked Mrs. Ferguson to give a report on the work being done by the
Public Safety Committee in regard to Y2K.
