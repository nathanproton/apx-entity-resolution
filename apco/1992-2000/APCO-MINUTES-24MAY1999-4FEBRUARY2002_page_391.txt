The General Assembly has passed legislation that promotes deregulation of the electric companies.
This legislation requires that the Board change the way it collects its utility tax on electric bills.
By consensus the Board will hold a public hearing on October 16, 2000 at 6:00 P.M. to receive
comments on the deregulation of the electric taxes.
The next item is the Library regarding fees collected. At the August 21, 2000 meeting the Board
decided to place monies collected by the Library in the general fund. Ms. Gibbs is requesting that
the Board reconsider this decision. When the Library expenses were being considered in preparation
for the budget, these monies were taken into consideration and budget expenses were modified
accordingly.
There was lengthy discussion regarding library policy and supplemental appropriations.
Mr. Kendrick made a motion seconded by Mr. Moore to revert back to the former policy regarding
these monies until the next Budget process. VOTE: Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Kendrick, aye; Mr.
Hancock, aye; Mr. Carwile, nay; and Mr. Carter, nay.
