3. Planning Commission Recommendations: The Planning Commission has recommended
that the following petitions, heard at the June 18, 2001 public hearing, be approved.
01-473 - Clayton C. Bryant and Thomas M. Page: Re-zone property from B-1 to M-1 with
Conditional Use Permit for welding fabrication shop per §170-51 of the Appomattox County Zoning
Ordinance. Property is located on Route 609 between Route 460 West and Route 608, Tax Map
#60(8)7, and is zoned B-1.
Mr. Kendrick made a motion that for reasons of public necessity, convenience, general welfare, and
good zoning practices, the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors approve the petition of Clayton
C. Bryant and Thomas M. page, to re-zone a parcel of land located at Tax Map #60(8)7 from B-1
to M-1. Motion seconded by Mr. Moore. VOTE: Mr. Kendrick, aye; Mr. Carter, aye; Mr. Hancock,
aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; and Mr. Moore, aye.
On a motion by Mr. Kendrick seconded by Mr. Hancock the following petitions were approved as
recommended by the Planning Commission.
