Mr. Bosiger explained that a letter had been received from a citizen complaining that no
one would take care of a raccoon that she felt was acting strange. Mr. Bosiger continued
that the citizen felt that someone should be designated to take care of these animals as
situations arise.
Discussion followed.
After discussion, and by consensus of the Board, it was decided that Animal Control
would not get involved in handling game animals that were acting strange.
