A public hearing of the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors was held on June 28,
1999 at 6:30 P.M. in the Courthouse, Courthouse Square, North Court Street,
Appomattox, Virginia. \
Present (Board of Supervisors):
Garet K. Bosiger — Chairman — Falling River District
Samuel E. Carter — Appomattox Courthouse District

Samuel Carter

Also Present:
Aileen T. Ferguson — County Administrator

Mr. Carter called the public hearing to order and explained that prior to receiving public
comments the Elementary School Site Committee would provide information on the last
two sites that have been considered for placement of a new elementary school.
Mr. Carter began that after six plus years the Elementary School Site Committee was
ready to make a recommendation to the Board of Supervisors. Mr. Carter continued that
current committee members included Lannis Selz, Kenneth McClenny, Dennis Torrence
and himself. Mr. Carter explained that the committee also enlisted the services of two
professionals, Earl Dickerson of Hurt & Proffitt and Blair Smith of Fauber Architects for
site evaluations and projected cost estimates. Mr. Carter explained that this was a
through and in-depth evaluation of all the potential sites, where nothing was taken for
granted.
