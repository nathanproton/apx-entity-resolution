At 6:16 P.M. Mr. Moore called the meeting to order stating this was a recessed meeting from
November 20, 2000.
The Board of Supervisors waited for the Appomattox Town Council to enter into session. The Town
Council then joined the Board of Supervisors.
At 6:17 P.M. Mr. Ronnie Spiggle called the Town Council meeting to order with a motion by Mr.
Jimmy Mayberry, seconded by Mrs. Joyce Bennett. Council voted to call the meeting to order and
all those present voting aye. Motion carried.
Mr. Moore introduced Ms.Leigh-Ann Jones of the Appomattox County High School Family, Career
and Community Leaders of America.
Ms. Jones madea brief statement about the FCCLA and the “Stop the Violence Week” proclamation.
Ms. Channing Harrison of the FCCLA invited the Board of Supervisors and the Town Council to
a candlelight ceremony at 5:00 P.M. on December 2, 2000 at the Appomattox County Courthouse
Complex.
