Mr. Moore requested Ms. Barbara Bennett, Regional Renaissance to come forward and address the

a presentation for the Board to review. 30% of the people who voted have volunteered to work on

type of people who voted, - men and women and the ethnic backgrounds. 20% of the voters were

projects. Also all of their findings are available on the Website.
Ms. Bennett gave Mr. Moore the report of the Regional Renaissance process from beginning to end,
including specific Appomattox information.
