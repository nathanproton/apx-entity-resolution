Chairman Conrad called the public hearing to order at 6:15 p.m. and explained that the purpose
of the public hearing is to receive public comment on the proposed abandonment of a portion of
State Route 654 that is now in the State Secondary Road System. Chairman Conrad explained
that this abandonment had been requested by Alice Corbin Rollins and Stephen Rollins pursuant
to Section 33.1-151 of the Code of Virginia.
Chairman Conrad called for the petitioners to come forward and speak if they wished to do so.
The petitioners made no comment. Chairman Conrad then called for anyone in the audience who
would like to come and speak in favor or in opposition to this petition.
No comments were heard in favor or opposition of this petition, Chairman Conrad closed the
public hearing atat 6:24 PM.
