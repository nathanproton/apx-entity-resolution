At 6:00 P.M. Mr. Moore called the meeting to order and asked Mr. Kendrick to give the invocation,
Mr. Moore called Mr. Wayne Bowman of the Virginia Department of Forestry to address the Board.
Mr. Bowman presented the Board with a check in the amount of $21,062.73 which represents onequarter of the proceeds from the Appomattox/Buckingham State Forests.
Mr. Bowman also stated that the Forestry Department was still catching up with the sleet damage,which is why the check amount is down somewhat this year. They are also trying to establish somehorseback riding trails, bike riding trails, hiking trails and are trying to make the State Forests more
There have been some problems with timber damaging insects but not as bad as several years ago.
Mr. Moore thanked Mr. Bowman for meeting with the Board and for presenting them with the check.
Mr. Moore explained that an closed Session was necessary under Code Section 2.1-344(A)7 - legalissues and Code Section 2.1-344(A)1 - personnel matters.
