At 7:00 p.m. on Tuesday, January 22TM 2002 the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors and
the Appomattox County Planning Commission held a public hearing at the Appomattox County
Courthouse, Courthouse Square, Court Street, Appomattox, Va. 24522.
PRESENT: Samuel E. Carter, Chairman - Courthouse District
Jesse L. Hancock - Falling River District
Frank H. Carwile - Wreck Island District
Russell H. Moore - Piney Mountain District
Jerry G. Kendrick - Appomattox River District
ALSO PRESENT:
Andy Carroll - County Administrator
At 7:01 p.m. Mr. Carter opened the Public Hearing and stated the purpose of the public hearing
to consider the follow changes to the Appomattox County Code.
Chapter 17 - Erosion & Sediment Control: Update to conform with Code of Virginia
requirements, among other changes.
Mr. Carter called for comments for or against this amendment.
Hearing none, Mr. Carter read the next amendment.
Chapter 170 - Section 97.1 - Joint Board of Zoning Appeals: Update to conform with Code of
Virginia requirements, among other changes.
Mr. Carter called for comments for or against this amendment.
Hearing none Mr. Carter read the next amendment.
