Mr. Jamerson next asked the Board to reconsider the request from the
Town of Appomattox to reimburse the Town 1/4 the cost of refurbishing a
fire truck owned by the Appomattox Volunteer Fire Department. After
reviewing prior commitments and discussion, Mr. Torrence made a motion
seconded by Mrs. Beasley to pay the Town of Appomattox $11,175.16 as
the County share for refurbishing and retrofitting the 1972 Chevrolet
Pumper fire truck owned by the Appomattox Volunteer Fire Department.
The money is to be appropriated from unappropriated surplus. Mr.
Jamerson, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr.
Torrence, aye.
