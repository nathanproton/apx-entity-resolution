ARTICLE XVII
SEMI-ANNUAL COLLECTION OF REAL ESTATE TAXES
Authority: 1950 Code of Virginia, as amended (1993)
147-45 Taxes due and owing to the County assessed for any year on
real estate, shall be due and payable in equal installments
on the 5th day of June and the 5th day of December of the
year the tax is assessed. On all such taxes remaining unpaid
on the day after such tax due date there shall be added a
penalty of ten (10) percent of the installment then due or
ten dollars ($10.00), whichever is greater, which penalty
shall become a part of said tax; together with interest
thereon at a rate of ten (10) percent per annun, which
interest shall begin on the day after such tax is due.
