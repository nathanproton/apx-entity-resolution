On Monday, April 2, 2001 at 6:00 P.M., the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors held it’s
regularly scheduled monthly meeting at the Appomattox County Courthouse, Courthouse Square,
Court Street, Appomattox, Va.
PRESENT: _ Jerry G. Kendrick, Chairman - Appomattox River District
Samuel Carter - Courthouse District
Jesse Hancock - Falling River District
Frank H. Carwile - Wreck Island District
Russell H. Moore - Piney Mountain District
ALSO PRESENT:
Andy Carroll - County Administrator
John Spencer - Assistant County Administrator
Mr. Kendrick opened the meeting at 6:00 P.M. and gave the invocation.
Mr. Kendrick asked Mr. C. T. Moses to come before the Board to discuss the American Legion’s
request to place plaques in the renovated Courthouse in honor of those men and women from
Appomattox County who have served in the armed forces of our Country. The American Legion is
not asking the County for any money towards this project. They are planning to have a plaque for
those who served in each of the different conflicts: World War I; Wold War II; Korean; Vietnam;
and Desert Storm.
