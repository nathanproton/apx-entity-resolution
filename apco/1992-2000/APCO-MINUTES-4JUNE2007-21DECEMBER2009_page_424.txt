On Monday, January 20, 2009 at 6:30 p.m. the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors held
its regular scheduled meeting at the Appomattox Community Center, located on 220 Community
Lane, Appomattox, Virginia.
Present: Thomas H. Conrad -Chairman — Falling River District
Gary W. Tanner-Vice Chairman— Stonewall District
Samuel E. Carter — Appomattox Courthouse District
William H. Craft - Appomattox River District
Russell H. Moore ~ Piney Mountain District
Also Present: Aileen T. Ferguson — County Administrator
John K. Spencer — Assistant County Administrator
J. G. Overstreet - County Attorney
Vicky Phelps — Administrative Assistant
Chairman Conrad called the regular scheduled meeting to order at 6:30 p.m. and called on Mr.
Craft to deliver the invocation. Everyone joined in the Pledge of Allegiance to the American
Flag.
Chairman Conrad called for the setting of the agenda. Ms. Ferguson stated that Mr. Fred Godsey
had been added to the agenda under appearances at 7:30 to present the revised Emergency
Operation Plan and a resolution will need to be adopted to accept the plan as revised. Mrs.
Ferguson stated that item 9-A on the additional items will need to be removed from the agenda.
Mr. Carter questioned additional item 9-A as to why it was being removed. Chairman Conrad
explained that this item did not need to be approved by the Board tonight, the Board would need
to ask someone to serve in this capacity then Central Virginia Area Agency’s Board of Directors
will appoint.
Chairman Conrad next called for public comment.
