A recessed meeting of the Appomatto County Board of
Supervisors was called to order by Chairman Jamerson in the
Court House, Appomattox, Virginia at 7:05 P.M. August 18,
1992s
Present
W. E. Jamerson, Chairman, Courthouse District
Dennis Torrence, Vice Chairman, Appomattox River District
Frank Carwile, Wreck Island District
Russell Moore, Piney Mountain District
Nina Beasley, Falling River District
Thomas W. Lawson, County Attorney
Gordon L. Rice, County Administrator
Mr. Jamerson recognized Mr. Torrence who asked the Board to
reconsider participating in a feasibility study regarding a
regional landfill in cooperation with Nelson and Buckingham
Counties. After discussion, Mr. Torrence made a motion
seconded by Mr. Moore to participate in the study for a fee
not to exceed $1500.00 providing Nelson and Buckingham
counties also agree to participate. Vote: Mr. Jamerson,
aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore,aye; Mr.
Torrence, aye.
The Board then reviewed several County ordinances and the
comments on each by the consultant hired to help update the
County Code. No actions were taken.
