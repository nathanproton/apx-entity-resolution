Mr. Carroll informed the Board that he has talked with Davenport & Co. LLC out of Richmond
about coming in and looking at the Budget, Finances and Debt Service for the future years, and give
us a fluid number. They could work with the Commissionér of Revenue, the Treasurer and also the
School to try to figure out where we are on some things and maybe make some decisions on how to

Board to give this some consideration.

to look at the County’s financial situation at a price not to exceed $7,500. VOTE: Mr. Moore, aye;
Mr. Kendrick, aye; Mr. Hancock, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye and Mr. Carter, aye.
