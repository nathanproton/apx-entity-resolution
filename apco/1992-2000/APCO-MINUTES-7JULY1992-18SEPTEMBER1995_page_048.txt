Mr. Rice informed the Board of the Virginia Association of
Counties Annual Meeting November 8 - 10, 1992. No Board
members were interested in attending.
Mr. Jamerson informed the Board he had received a letter from
John Purcell, a member of the Board of Supervisors from
Louisa County asking for support to the VACO Board of
Directors from Region IV. After discussion, Mr. Torrence
made a motion seconded by Mr. Carwile to adopt a resolution
supporting Mr. John Purcell for the ACO Board of Directors
from Region IV. The resolution being:
A RESOLUTION IN SUPPORT OF JOHN J. PURCELL, JR. FOR THE
REGION FOUR REPRESENTATIVE ON THE VIRGINIA ASSOCIATION OF
COUNTIES BOARD OF DIRECTORS
WHEREAS, the Virginia Association of Counties has
redistricted the Twelve Regions for the election of the
twenty-two Virginia Association of Counties; and
WHEREAS, John J. Purcell, Jr., Chairman, Louisa County
Board of Supervisors, has served six years on the Virginia
Association of Counties Board of Directors representing
Region Two; and
WHEREAS, Louisa County has been moved from Region Two to
Region Four; and
WHEREAS, John J. Purcell, Jr. served all the counties
within Region Two with distinction during his tenure on the
Virginia Association of Counties Board of Directors; and
WHEREAS, John J. Purcell, Jr. is eligible to serve two
more years on the Virginia Association of Counties Board of
Directors.
NOW, THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED by the Appomattox County
Board of Supervisors on this the 14th day of September 1992,
that the Board of Supervisors does hereby unanimously endorse
and support John J. Purcell, Jr, for the Virginia Association
of Counties Board of Directors representing Region Four.
Vote: Mr. Jamerson, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile,
aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.
