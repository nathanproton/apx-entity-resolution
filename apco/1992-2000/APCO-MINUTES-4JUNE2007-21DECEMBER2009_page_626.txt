VIRGINIA: IN THE CIRCUIT COURT OF APPOMATTOX COUNTY
RE: COUNTY OF APPOMATTOX, VIRGINIA,
PETITION
TO THE HONORABLE RICHARD S. BLANTON, JUDGE
This day comes the County of Appomattox, Virginia by and through its County
Attorney and petitions the Circuit Court of Appomattox County, Virginia for a special
election, pursuant to Section 24.2-228.1 of the Virginia Code and in support of said
motion says as follows:
That Barbara R. Williams is the duly elected Clerk of Court for the Circuit Court
of Appomattox County.
That the aforesaid Barbara R. Williams has retired from the Circuit Court of
Appomattox County effective the 31* day of August 2009.
That the Board of Supervisors, pursuant to Sec. 24.2-228.1 of the Code of
Virginia must petition the Circuit Court of Appomattox County for the issuance
of a writ of election to fill the aforesaid vacancy.
That the Board of Supervisors of Appomattox County at its meeting duly held on
the 8" day of September, 2009 passed a resolution authorizing the County
Attorney of Appomattox County to submit a petition to the Circuit Court of
Appomattox County.
That Sec. 24.2-228.1A Code of Virginia provides that “. . .a vacancy in any
elected constitutional office, ..., shall be filled by special election”; and that the
date shall be ‘determined pursuant to Sec. 24.2-682.” and
