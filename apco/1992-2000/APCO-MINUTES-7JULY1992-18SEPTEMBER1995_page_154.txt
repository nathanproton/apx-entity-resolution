Mr. Jamerson, presiding, called the meeting to order at 7:00 P.M.,
recognized Sheriff Richardson, and asked the Sheriff to address the
Board regarding his proposed FY94 budget.
Mr. Richardson’s budget requests and justifications were reviewed and
discussed at length.
Mr. Richardson also reviewed the status of a proposed addition and
alterations to the County jail and preserited cost estimates and
preliminary sketches for review and consideration. A needs assessment
and preliminary study was estimated to cost $5,000.00. After the
Sheriff had answered several questions from Board members, Mr. Jamerson
thanked and excused the Sheriff and invited Mrs. Janet Belter, County
Treasurer, to address the Board.
