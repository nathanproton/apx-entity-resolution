A public hearing of the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors was held on
November 15, 1999 at 6:00 P.M. in the Courthouse, Courthouse Square, North Court
Street, Appomattox, Virginia.

Also Present:
Aileen T. Ferguson — County Administrator
Andy Carroll — Assistant County Administrator

Mr. Bosiger called the public hearing to order at 6:00 P.M. and explained that the
purpose of the hearing was to receive public comment on a proposed ordinance. Mr.
Bosiger then read the ordinance as follows:
There is hereby levied upon every corporation or other entity providing heat, light and
power within the county, for the privilege of doing business within the county, a license
tax equal to one half of 1 percent of the gross receipts accruing from sales to the ultimate
consumer in the county.
