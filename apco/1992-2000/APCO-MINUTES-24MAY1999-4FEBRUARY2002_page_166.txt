Mr. Bosiger called the public hearing to order at 7:15 P.M. explained the purpose of the hearing
was to receive public comment on a zoning petition and gave the procedure for addressing
comments. Mr. Bosiger then asked Mr. Caldwell to read the petition.
Mr. Caldwell read petition number 99-405 of Dolly F. Sowell to rezone property from A-1 to B-1
with conditional use permit for Auto Sales, Fitness center and Hair Salon. Property is part of Tax
Map No. 62-A-158 located on Route 646 between Route 460 and Route 708.
Mr. Bosiger called for comments on this petition.
Mr. Terry Ramsey came forward gave his address as Route 3 Box 276, Appomattox, Virginia.
