Ms. Mary Lou Spiggle rose and stated that she lived on Pine Street and

Ms. Barbara Shelton came forward and stated that she agrees with what
the others have said and she is also opposed to the rezoning request.
She sees no reason to change this area from R-1.
Mr. Steve Conner came forward and stated that he did not request the
rezoning. He stated that the request was from Pilgrim Land Development
who has owned the property since 1976, he is only acting as their
agent. Mr. Conner stated that in 1972 some of the restrictions were
lifted on these lots. Mr. Conner stated that he is unaware if the Town
can do a conditional zoning to allow townhouses and not apartments but
Pilgrim Land Development would agree to this concession.
Mr. Conner stated that the developer would be interested in closing
Pine Street to avoid the problem of through traffic. They are also
willing to sell all the lots they own for $10,000.00 each. Mr. Conner
felt that it will be difficult to sell property for single family
dwellings that close to the lagoon. Mr. Conner stated that there is
the potential for building 17 single family dwellings on their lots.
Mr. Conner stated if anyone had questions or wished to see the plans he
would be happy to discuss this with them.
