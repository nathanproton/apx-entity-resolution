Mr. Fred Godsey, Emergency Services Coordinator was asked to address the Board.
Mr. Godsey presented an overview of the Appomattox County Emergency Operations Plan. Mr.
Godsey noted there were several additions to the Plan: 7 annexes and 2 Hazard Specific appendixes,
which need to be adopted by the Board of Supervisors. This Emergency Operations Manual (Plan)
has to be updated annually and has to be approved by the Board every five (5) years. The Plan is
geared toward Appomattox County, what we can do and would do in some type of disaster or an
emergency situation.
Mr. Carter stated he would like to commend Mr. Godsey on his fine efforts.
Mr. Kendrick thanked and excused Mr. Godsey.
Mr. Carwile made a motion seconded by Mr. Kendrick to adopt the updated Appomattox County
Emergency Operations Plan as submitted by Mr. Godsey. VOTE: Mr. Kendrick, aye; Mr. Carter,
aye; Mr. Hancock, aye;, Mr. Carwile, aye; and Mr. Moore, aye.
