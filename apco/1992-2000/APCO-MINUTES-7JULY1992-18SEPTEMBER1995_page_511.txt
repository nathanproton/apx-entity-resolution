Mrs. Ferguson requested permission to attend the Virginia Municipal
League Conference at Norfolk October 16-18, 1994.
Mr. Paulette made a motion seconded by Mrs. Beasley to approve Mrs.
Ferguson to attend the Virginia Municipal League Conference in
Norfolk. Vote: Mr. Torrence, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye;
Mr. Moore, aye; and Mr. Paulette, aye.
Mrs. Ferguson next presented a request from Monte Mays, Commissioner of
Revenue, for him and one deputy to attend the Annual Commissioners of
the Revenue Association Convention.
After discussion, Mrs. Beasley made a motion seconded by Mr. Paulette
to approve expenses for Mr. Mays to attend the Commissioner of the
Revenue Association Convention. Vote: Mr. Torrence, aye; Mrs. Beasley,
aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; and Mr. Paulette, aye.
Mrs. Ferguson presented a request for an appropriation to the
Comprehensive Services budget.
Mr. Paulette made a motion seconded by Mrs. Beasley to appropriate
$5,000.00 to the Comprehensive Services budget. Vote: Mr. Torrence,
aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; and Mr.
Paulette, aye.
Mrs. Ferguson next asked the Board of Supervisors for their support in
writing a letter to Disney offering Appomattox as a site for their new
development.
After discussion, the Board by consensus, requested Mrs. Ferguson to
proceed with the letter to Disney concerning an Appomattox site.
Mr. Torrence asked the Board write a letter of appreciation to the
landfill employees for a job well done.
After discussion and by consensus, the Board agreed to send such a
letter to all landfill employees.
Being no further business, the meeting was adjourned at 9:02 P.M.
