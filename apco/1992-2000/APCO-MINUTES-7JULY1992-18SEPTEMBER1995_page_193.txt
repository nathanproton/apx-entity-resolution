A special recessed meeting and budget work session was held by th
Appomattox County Board of Supervisors May 13, 1993 following the
Public Hearing on the proposed 1994 Fiscal Year operations’ budget for
Appomattox County Government. The meeting was held in the courtroom
of the Appomattox County Courthouse, Courthouse Square, North Court
Street, Appomattox, Virginia beginning at 8:15 P.M.
~~
Present:
Dennis Torrence, Vice Chairman, Appomattox River District
Nina Beasley, Falling River District
Frank Carwile, Wreck Island District
Russell Moore, Piney Mountain District
Absent:
W. E. "Bill" Jamerson, Courthouse District
Also Present:
Gordon L. Rice, County Administrator
Vice Chairman Torrence presiding called the meeting to order at 8:15
P.M. The School Board’s proposed budget and the comments from the
recently concluded public hearing were discussed at length; however, no
action was taken pending Mr. Jamerson’s return.
Mr. Torrence informed the Board all nominees for the Piedmont Privat
Industry Council need recommendation of the Chamber of Commerce sin
the search is for a business or industry representative.
Mr. Torrence next asked the Board to consider Zoning Ordinance Z-93-
and noted that at their meeting of March 11, 1993, the Board resolv
to rezone the Landfill to Agricultural (A-1), but did not adopt
ordinance as suggested by Attorney Tribble. After discussion, Mrs
Beasley made a motion seconded by Mr. Moore to adopt the following
Ordinance:
ORDINANCE Z 93-2
BE IT ORDAINED by the Board of Supervisors of Appomattox County
that the zoning ordinance and zoning district map of this County is
amended by the rezoning of property described as Tax Map Parcel 66(A) 29
from EH Environmental Hazard District to A-1 Agricultural District.
