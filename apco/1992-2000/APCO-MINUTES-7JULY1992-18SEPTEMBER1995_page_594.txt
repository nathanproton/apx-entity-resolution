Falling River District:
Mr. Ronnie Cyrus came forward and stated that he lived at the
intersection of Route 700 and Route 675, and that he is requesting
Route 675 be paved. Mr. Cyrus continued by saying that in the past few
years, three new residences have been constructed on Route 675 and that
there were five families there now, within about one mile or less.
Mr. Jimmy Bollinger came forward next and explained that his request is
also for Route 675 to be paved. He continued by stating that he has a
seven year old child that was asthmatic and the dust from the road
caused her lots of problems.
Piney Mountain District:
Ms. Dianne Miller came forward and stated that she lived on Route 677
and was not sure what district that was in. Ms. Miller explained that
she lived on the new access road and had understood when the road was
done, that two additional coats of surface treatment would be put on
this road, which has not been done. Ms. Miller is requesting that all
of her end of Route 677 be resurfaced.
Wreck Island District:
No requests.
Mr. Austin stated that he wanted to verify there were no requests for
new additions.
Hearing no further requests, Mr. Paulette closed the public hearing at
7:02 P.M.
