On Monday, April 28, 2008 at 6:30 p.m. the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors
held a Public Hearing on the FY09 Proposed Tax Rate at the Appomattox Community
Center, located on 220 Community Lane, Appomattox, Virginia.
Present: William H. Craft - Appomattox River District
Samuel E. Carter — Courthouse District
Thomas H. Conrad — Falling River District
Russell H. Moore - Piney Mountain District
Gary W. Tanner — Wreck Island District
Also Present: J.G. Overstreet- County Attorney
Aileen T. Ferguson — County Administrator
John K. Spencer — Assistant County Administrator
Johnnie Roark - Planner
Vicky Phelps — Administrative Assistant
Chairman Craft called the Public Hearing to order at 6:30 p.m. and everyone joined in the
pledge of allegiance to the American Flag,
Chairman Craft explained that the purpose of the Public Hearing would be to receive
public comment on the Proposed FY09 Proposed Tax Rate. Chairman Craft explained
the procedure for addressing the Board during the public hearing.
Chairman Craft next called on Mr. Tom Schmutz to come and address the Board.
Mr. Tom Schmutz came forward and gave his address at 1656 Beckham Road, Concord,
Virginia. Mr. Schmutz stated that he feels the tax rate is going to cause a hardship ona
lot of people this year. Mr. Schmutz then stated that he would like to commend the
Board for considering land use for the next year.
