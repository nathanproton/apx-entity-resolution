Mr. Torrence called the meeting to order at 6:40 P.M. and explained the
purpose of the hearing was to receive public comment on two (2) zoning
petitions.
Mr. Torrence then asked Mr. Caldwell, Clerk to the Planning Commission,
to read the first petition.
Mr. Caldwell read petition number 94-230 of Ronald E. Franklin for
Conditional Use Permit to allow installation of two mobile homes.
Property is located on Route T-1111 between Route T-1103 and Route
T-1112. Property is zoned V-1.
Mr. Torrence asked Mr. Franklin if he wished to make a statement
concerning his request.
Mr. Franklin rose, stated his address as Pamplin and gave a brief
description of the location of his property. Mr. Franklin stated that
the reason for the second mobile home to be placed on the property was
so that someone could help care for a neighbor.
Mr. Torrence asked if anyone else wished to comment on this petition.
Hearing no one, Mr. Torrence asked Mr. Caldwell to read the second
petition.
Mr. Caldwell read petition number 94-231 of Robert T. Card, Jr. fora
Conditional Use Permit for Beauty Shop in existing structure. Building
is located on Route 460 between Route 620 and Route 633. Property is
zoned A-1.
Mr. Torrence called for comments on petition 94-231.
Hearing none, and being no further petitions, Mr. Torrence closed the
public hearing at 6:49 P.M.
