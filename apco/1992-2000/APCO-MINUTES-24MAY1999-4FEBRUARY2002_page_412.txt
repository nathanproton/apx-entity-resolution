On October 23, 2000 at 6:00 the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors met with the Virginia
Department of Transportation at the VDOT Residency Office on Ferguson Street, Appomattox, Va.
The purpose of this meeting was to discuss the Six-Year Secondary Road Plan.
PRESENT: Russell H. Moore, Chairman - Piney Mountain District
Jerry Kendrick - Appomattox River District
Jesse Hancock - Falling River District
Frank H. Carwile - Wreck Island District
Samuel Carter - Courthouse District
ALSO PRESENT: = Andy Carroll - County Administrator
Sandra Lindsay - VDOT Resident Engineer
Don Austin - VDOT
Cathy Arrington - VDOT
At 6:10 P. M. Mr. Moore called the meeting to order.
There was some preliminary discussion on the Six-Year Plan.
Mr. Moore stated that the Board of Supervisors had a couple of items that needed to be discussed
and acted upon.
Mr. Moore called for comments and approval of the bills for the month.
Mr. Carter made a motion seconded by Mr. Hancock to approve the bills as presented. VOTE: Mr.
Moore, aye; Mr. Kendrick, aye; Mr. Hancock, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; and Mr. Carter, aye.
Mr. Carroll informed the Board that Region 2000 will be hosting several project managers from
VDEP in Richmond October 30-November 1. These project managers will be visiting several
industrial parks and companies in our area. Mr. Carroll would like to host a lunch at the industrial
park for these managers, some local industry personnel and the Board of Supervisors. The cost of
which would be approximately $250.00.
There was some discussion regarding the VEDP visit and the Industrial Park.
