THE FOLLOWING GUIDELINES MUST BE FOLLOWED:
Written application must be made by one or more persons who will
sign this agreement form. This person(s) will then be our main
contact in the event of problems.
No alcoholic beverages allowed on grounds at any time.
Applicant(s) should complete an application for the use of the
ball field and return to the Recreation Department prior to your
event.
All games must terminate by 11:00 P.M. and lights turned off by
11:30 P.M.
In the event of inclement weather (wet field/grounds) the
Recreation Department should be contacted to determine whether or
not the ball field is playable. (Coaches may decide for the high
school or the Youth Association Teams.)

approval is given. The county reserves the right to know why a
fee is necessary and what the money collected will be used for.
Appomattox County will furnish and maintain field, lights, and
major repairs; subject to Board of Supervisors approval.
It is extremely important that applicant(s), League Officials,
and Coaches enforce the following:
A. Park in designated areas only.
B. Trash should be picked up nightly and placed in barrels.
c. Infield, pitchers mound, and batters box should be left in
good condition as found.
D. Field and grounds should be policed nightly.
