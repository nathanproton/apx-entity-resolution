Mr. Austin then explained the procedure that would be followed when
requests were made for new additions or improvements to the secondary
road system. First, the request is made. Second the Board of Viewers go
out with the Department of Transportation to look at the roads in
question. Third, the Board of Supervisors must pass a resolution
requesting Virginia Department of Transportation pursue taking a road
into the system. Fourth, Virginia Department of Transportation review
and approves or rejects the request.
