On Monday, October 22, 2007, the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors held a joint
meeting with the Appomattox County School Board at 7:00 p.m. in the School
Administration Building located on Court Street, Appomattox, VA.

At 7:00 p.m. Mr. Waddell calied the School Board to order and Mr. Carter called the
Board of Supervisors to order.
Mr. Waddell welcomed everyone present and said that they would entertain questions as
the meeting went along. He also expressed their appreciation for the support the Board
of Supervisors has shown over the years.
Mr. Carter, on behalf of the Board of Supervisors, thanked the School Board for their
hospitality and that the two bodies could meet together and continue the open
communication lines that exists between them.
Dr. Aldridge Boone, Superintendent of Schools, stated he takes this meeting in the spirit
for which it is intended. It is always his goal to work on relationships, not on to build
them, but to maintain them.
Dr. Boone, presented a slide presentation of the State of the Schools entitled
“Relationships Matter”. This presentation covered topics such as Student Demographic
Data; Graduates Information on the Class of 2007; Teacher Demographic Data; County
School Accomplishments; Initiatives for 2007-08 and Challenges for 2007-08.
