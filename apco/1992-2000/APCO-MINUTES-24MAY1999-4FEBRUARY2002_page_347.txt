Mr. Carter made a motion seconded by Mr. Kendrick to appoint Ms. Mary Ann Freshwater as ProjectDirector for the Victim/Witness Advocate Program effective immediately subject to her approval,in lieu of her prior position. Mrs. Freshwater will report to the Project Administrator (CountyAdministrator) and be paid back wages for her previous position. VOTE: Mr. Moore, aye; Mr.Kendrick, aye; Mr. Hancock, abstain; Mr. Carwile, nay; and Mr. Carter, aye.
Mr. Kendrick made a motion seconded by Mr. Carter to have Mr. Carroll, Acting CountyAdministrator send a letter to all persons employed under a Grant, stating that they are consideredas County Employees. Along with this letter he is to a copy of the County Personnel Policy andasking the employee to sign the letter, agreeing to follow the County Personnel Policy, and returnit to the County Administrator’s Office, if they do not, the County will retract the Grant. VOTE: Mr.Moore, aye; Mr. Kendrick, aye; Mr. Hancock, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; and Mr. Carter, aye.
At 11:05 p.m. Mr. Carter made a motion seconded by Mr. Kendrick to recess until 6:00 p.m. June20, 2000. VOTE: Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Kendrick, aye; Mr. Hancock, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; and Mr.Carter, aye.
Russell H. Moore, Chairman
