On Monday, December 17, 2001 at 6:00 P.M. the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors held its
regularly scheduled meeting at the Appomattox County Courthouse, Courthouse Square, Court
Street, Appomattox, Va.
PRESENT: _ Jerry G. Kendrick, Chairman - Appomattox River District
Samuel E. Carter - Courthouse District
Jesse L. Hancock - Falling River District
Frank H. Carwile - Wreck Island District
Russell H. Moore - Piney Mountain District
ALSO PRESENT:
Andy Carroll - County Administrator
Brandon Stidham - County Planner
Mr. Kendrick opened the meeting at 6:00 p.m. and asked Mr. Hancock to give the invocation.
Mr. Kendrick then invited Mr. Danny Jobe, NESBE Cable General Manager to inform the Board of
the improvements NESBE was making in order to better serve the residents of Appomattox.
Mr. Jobe stated the work on the west side of the county had already been completed and things were
running a little behind schedule due to the couple of rainy days last week but everything would be
completed on schedule. He also stated he would be glad to answer questions anyone may have.
Mr. Carter wanted to know if the new scheduled line-up would be the same as Campbell County
with the basic channels (2-56) being the nominal rate of $36.95. Also if you wished to add other
options such as the Music Choice 900 Series, how many of these would you get for the additional
$8.95.
Mr. Jobe stated the nominal rate of $36.95 would get you the basic channels 2-56 and for the $8.95
additional fee for Music Choice you would get all of them in addition you get the digital basic and
access to the pay-per-view.
