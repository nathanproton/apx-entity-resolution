Mr. Moore called the meeting to order at 6:00 P.M. and asked Mr. Hancock to deliver the
invocation.
Mr. Moore then called for approval of the bills for payment January 18, 2000.
Mr. Carter made a motion seconded by Mr. Carwile to approve the bills for payment dated
January 18, 2000 as submitted. Vote: Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Carter, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr.
Hancock, aye; Mr. Kendrick, aye.
Mr. Moore next welcomed the members of the Appomattox County Planning Commission and
explained that the Board of Supervisors had received a copy of the proposed changes to the
zoning ordinance at the last meeting and therefore had wanted to meet with the Planning
Commission to see if there were any areas that the Board of Supervisors felt needed explanation.
A lengthy discussion followed concerning the proposed changes addressing intensive livestock,
dairy, and poultry facilities.
