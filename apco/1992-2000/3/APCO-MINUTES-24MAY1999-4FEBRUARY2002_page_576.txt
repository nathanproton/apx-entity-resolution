12. Employee Assistance Program: This program was approved by the Board at it’s
meeting on August 6", 2001. All Constitutional Officers, Library, Department of Social
Services and county employees can be covered under the same contract for only a
marginal cost which will be paid by the Department of Social Services and the Library.
Approval of the Consent Agenda will adopt the EAP policy as submitted and amend the
Board’s previous decision to include constitutional officers, Department of Social
Services, Library staff and county employees under one contract for an additional cost of
$337.50.
