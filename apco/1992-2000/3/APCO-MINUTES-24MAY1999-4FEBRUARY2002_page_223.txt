Mr. Moore called the public hearing to order and stated that the purpose of the hearing was to
receive public comment on several zoning petitions. Mr. Moore then stated the procedure for
addressing comments.
Mr. Moore called on Mr. Caldwell to read the first petition.
Mr. Caldwell read petition number 00-410 of Appomattox County to rezone property from A-1
to R-2. Property is Tax Map Parcel No. 64-A-157, 64A4-1-E-11 and 64A4-15-E-45 and is
located on Lee-Grant Avenue and Route 691.
Mr. Moore called for comments on this petition.
Hearing none, Mr. Moore asked Mr. Caldwell to read the next petition.
Mr. Caldwell read petition number 00-411 of CFW Intelos for a conditional use permit to allow
195-foot tower on property located on Route 732 between Route 460 and dead end. Property is
zoned B-1 and is Tax Map Parcel No. 62-5-C.
Mr. Moore called for comments on this petition.
Ms. Paula Figgatt came forward and gave her address as Waynesboro. Ms. Figgatt explained that
she was employed by CFW Intelos and that this application was for a 195 foot tower on property
owned by Fred Jones along the 460 corridor bypass. Ms. Figgatt stated that they needed the
placement of this tower to connect with the existing facilities to provide better service. Ms.
Figgatt then provided pictures of the location for the Board to review.
Mr. Moore called for comments on this petition.
Hearing none, Mr. Moore asked Mr. Caldwell to read the next petition.

190 foot tower on property located on Route 460 between Route 648 and Route 667. Property is
zoned A-1 and is Tax Map Parcel No. 73-A-6A.
