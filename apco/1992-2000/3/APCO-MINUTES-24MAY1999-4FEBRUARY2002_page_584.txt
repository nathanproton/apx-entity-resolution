PROCLAMATION TO RECOGNIZE THE ACHIEVEMENTS
OF THE APPOMATTOX MINOR LEAGUE ALL-STAR TEAM
WHEREAS, good citizenship and sportsmanship are molded through the values of cooperation
and competition fostered within youth sports in Appomattox County; and
WHEREAS, the Appomattox Minor League All-Star Team makes their home at the Woodmen
of the World Ball Field; and
WHEREAS, the Appomattox Minor League All-Star Team has prevailed as AAA Virginia State
Champions and have distinguished themselves through performance in the Dixie Youth World
Series; and
WHEREAS, the players and coaches have served as outstanding representatives of our County,
both on and off the field; and
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT PROCLAIMED this 4" Day of September 2001 that the
Appomattox County Board of Supervisors congratulates the Appomattox Minor League All-Star
Team for exemplary achievement in completing a remarkable and memorable 2001 season.
