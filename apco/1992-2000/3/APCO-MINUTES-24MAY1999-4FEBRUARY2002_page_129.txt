Mr. Bosiger then invited Mr. Hal Craddock to give his presentation to the Board
regarding the expansion at the Sheriff's Office. Mr. Craddock thanked the Board for its
time and gave a presentation to the Board using several displays. After the presentation
the Board and Mr. Craddock discussed the plans and if the plans would meet the needs of
