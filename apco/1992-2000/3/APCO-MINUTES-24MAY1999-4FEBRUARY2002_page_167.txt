Mr. Ramsey stated that he was the one making the petition on behalf of his mother-in-law and
that he and his wife would be operating the businesses if the zoning were approved. Mr. Ramsey
stated that he had been asked a lot of questions concerning what he proposed to do and he had
tried to answer these questions as best he could.
Mr. Bosiger called for other comments.
Mr. Mickey Martin came forward and gave his address as Route 1 Box 3460, Spout Spring,
Virginia. Mr. Martin stated that he had lived in this neighborhood for quite a few years and that
Mrs. Sowell had been a good neighbor. Mr. Martin continued that he was concerned with this
operation being constructed across from his brick home and he was concerned with the traffic
involved due to the businesses. Mr. Martin stated that he was opposed to the petition.
Mr. Bosiger called for other comments.
Mrs. Nannie Mae Davidson came forward and gave her address as Route 2, Spout Spring,
Virginia. Mrs. Davidson continued that she was opposed to the rezoning because she lived right
down the street and she knew this was going to create a lot of traffic.
Mr. Bosiger called for other comments.
Hearing none and being no further petitions at 7:20 P.M. Mr. Bosiger declared the public hearing
closed.
