assurance. Mr. Carroll stated that there was a draft bill 5844 which would require the escrow
funds for closure and post-closure care cost. Mr. Carroll continued that this entire bill was
specifically homing in on localities that have landfills. In particular those that use the financial test
as financial assurance. Mr. Carrroll continued with an explanation of the proposed bill.
A lengthy discussion followed.
Mr. Carroll then presented information concerning the need for hiring someone to haul cover
material from the borrow area at the landfill to be placed at the present cell. Mr. Carroll explained
that he had received several quotes for hauling 10,000 cubic yards of cover material.

yards of soil from the borrow area to the working face and appropriate $15,000.00 to cover this
expense. Vote: Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Carter, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Kendrick, aye.
