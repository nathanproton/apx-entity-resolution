Mr. Moore thanked Ms. Lindsay for her comments and excused her. Mr. Moore then asked Lee
Hood Capps, Executive Director of the Central Virginia Planning District Commission
(CVPDC), to appear before the Board.
Mr. Capps approached and stated that he had a folder for each Board member containing
information on the CVPDC and that he wanted to go over this information to remind former
Board members of the services provided and to introduce the new Board members to the CVPDC
services. Mr. Capps explained the role the CVPDC had played in several projects involving
Appomattox County, the Town of Appomattox, and the Town of Pamplin. These projects ranged
from grant development & support to 2000 Census preparation. Mr. Capps concluded his
comments and asked if there were any questions from the Board. Mr. Kendrick asked a question
regarding the Foreign Trade Zone listed in the packet. Mr. Capps explained the difference
between a Foreign Trade Zone and a Free Trade Zone and the benefits that a Foreign Trade Zone
can have for businesses. Mr. Moore thanked Mr. Capps for his appearance and the information
he had provided.
