7. Board Appointments:
a. Industrial Development Authority: Two members of the IDA terms will expire on
December 31, 2001. Mr. Bill Slagle is willing to serve another term if re-
appointed. However, Mr. Leon Turner does not wish to be re-appointed. The IDA
wishes to offer up Mr. Wayne Lewis for consideration and Mr. Bill Slagle for re-
appointment. Both of these are 4-year terms which will expire on December 31,
2005.
On a motion by Mr. Moore, seconded by Mr. Hancock, Mr. Bill Slagle and Mr. Wayne Lewis
were appointed to the Industrial Development Authority for a four-year term to expire on
December 31, 2005. VOTE: Mr. Kendrick, aye; Mr. Carter, aye; Mr. Hancock, aye; Mr. Carwile,
aye; and Mr. Moore, aye.

Carter, aye; Mr. Hancock, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; and Mr. Moore, aye.

Carwile, aye; and Mr. Moore, aye.
