7B. “Children At Play” signs: We have received two (2) requests for “Children At
Play” signs to be installed for safety purposes at two different locations in the
County. VDOT has done the preliminary evaluation and find that all is in order.
The individuals making the request will be purchasing the signs.
WHEREAS, the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors acknowledges the need to provide a safe
environment for our children, and
WHEREAS, Mr. Eddie Gunter has approached the Board of Supervisors with concern for the need for
WATCH FOR CHILDREN signs on Route 610; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Gunter has agreed to bear the cost for the signs and installation,
NOW THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED, that the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors is
requesting Virginia Department of Transportation to pursue the installation of WATCH FOR CHILDREN
signs on secondary Route 610.
Adopted July 16, 2007 by the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors.
