2. Possible Workforce Investment Area Consolidations: We have received a
letter from Gov. Kaine seeking comments on possible Workforce Investment Area
realignments in order to reduce program costs. The Region 2000 Workforce
Investment Board serves Appomattox, Amherst, Bedford and Campbell counties
and the City of Bedford & Lynchburg. The agency has worked hard to become a
model WIB and has provided us a list of accomplishments to justify why its
geographic service boundaries should not be changed.
Recommendation is to write a letter to Governor Kaine to keep the boundaries of
the Region 2000 Workforce Investment Board as they now exist.
Mr. Conrad made a motion, seconded by Mr. Craft, to write a letter to Governor Kaine
requesting that the Region 2000 Workforce Investment Board boundaries remain as
they are and should not be changed. VOTE: Mr. Armbrust, aye; Mr. Conrad, aye; Mr.
Craft, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; and Mr. Carter, aye. (5 aye votes)
