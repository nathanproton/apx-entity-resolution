Mr. Torrence stated that he would like for the Board to agree to make a decision at the
next meeting. Mr. Torrence continued that the Board was never going to please
everyone.
A lengthy discussion followed. After discussion it was agreed that the Board would wait
until the next meeting to make the decision on the elementary school in order to gather
more information.
Mr. Carter then gave a report from the Appomattox Town Council meeting. Mr. Carter
explained the Town drilled for water on the Central Virginia Electric Co-op site and were
unsuccessful. Mr. Carter also stated that the Main Street Project was currently on hold
and would not begin until sometime next year.
Mr. Bosiger then informed the other members of the Board that Marvin “Bo” House has
agreed to serve on the Regional Renaissance Board of Directors representing
Appomattox County.
Mr. Bosiger next explained that there were several supplemental appropriations that were
necessary.
