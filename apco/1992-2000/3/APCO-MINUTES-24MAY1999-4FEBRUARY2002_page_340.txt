Since Mr. Hal Craddock was not present and was expected to be a little late in arriving, Mr. Moore
called Mr. John Gascoyne to appear before the Board.
Mr. Gascoyne came forward and stated he was going to read a prepared statement. Mr. Gascoyne is
asking the Board to repeal the restricted voter district system and institute the at-large process for
selecting Board of Supervisors in the same manner as the other governmental officers; the Sheriff,
the Commissioner of Revenue, etc. Mr. Gascoyne had a petition containing 15 signatures of people
who join him in this petition.
Mr. Moore thanked and excused Mr. Gascoyne.
Mr. Moore called for approval of the bills.
Mr. Carter questioned 10 items regarding the appropriate suffix being charged. They have all been
corrected.
Mr. Carter then made a motion seconded by Mr. Kendrick to approve the bills for payment as
presented. VOTE: Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Kendrick, aye, Mr. Hancock, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; and Mr.
Carter, aye.
