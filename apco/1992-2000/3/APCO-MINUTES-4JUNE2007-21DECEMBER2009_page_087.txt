At 6:15 p.m. Mr. Carter opened the Public Hearing and stated the purpose of this public
hearing was to consider Proposed Amendments to the Zoning Ordinance and to the
Land Applications of Biosolids Ordinance.
He then turned the meeting over to Mr. Johnnie Roark, County Planner.
Mr. Roark stated the first amendment was to Section 170-34 — Conditional Uses, in
the Agricultural District, A-1;:
ADD: Automotive Repair Garages, Mechanical and Body Shops to the list of
conditional uses.
The origin of this request was through a petition received.
