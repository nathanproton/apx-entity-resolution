22. Portraits for Courthouse: We have received a request to place portraits of Samuel
Daniel McDearmon (1815-1871) and Zacchheus Elam Cheatham in the Courthouse. Both
of these men were representatives in the General Assembly. Mr. McDearmon was the
county’s first delegate and was later state senator for Appomattox, Campbell and
Lynchburg. Mr. Cheatham succeeded him in the House of Delegates. Based on Board
policy, the Courthouse is the accepted place for these portraits. The family has requested
a formal ceremony be held on October 27" to present these portraits.
At 8:00 P.M. Mr. Carter made a motion seconded by Mr. Moore to recess the meeting to 6:30
P.M. on August 23", 2001 for a joint meeting with the Appomattox Town Council. Meeting to
be held at the Town Office. VOTE: Mr. Kendrick, aye; Mr. Carter, aye; Mr. Hancock, aye; Mr.
Carwile, aye; and Mr. Moore, aye.
