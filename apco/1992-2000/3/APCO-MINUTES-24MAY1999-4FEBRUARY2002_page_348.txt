At 7:02 A.M. Mr. Moore opened the Public Hearing and stated the purpose of the Public Hearingwas to receive comments on petitions submitted for Variances to the Zoning Ordinance and statedthe procedure for addressing comments.
Mr. Moore asked Mr. Caldwell to read the first petition.
Petition No. 00-429 - Walter Litchford - Variance to paragraph 170-80A of the AppomattoxCounty Zoning Ordinance pertaining to side yard setback. Property is Tax Map Parcel No. 37-A-24A zoned A-1 and is located on Route 660 between Route 661 and Route 26.
Mr. Moore asked for comments in favor of this petition.
Mr. Litchford came forward and spoke in favor of this petition and stated he would answer anyquestions anyone had regarding his petition.
Mr. Moore asked for comments against this petition.
Hearing none, he asked Mr. Caldwell to read the next petition.
Petition No. 00-430 - Pearl & Edward Walker - Conditional Use Permit to allow HomeOccupation of tire mounting and balancing. Property is Tax Map No. 102-A-18 and is located onRoute 460 between Route 628 and Route 620. Property is zoned A-1.
Mr. Moore called for comments for this petition.
Mr. & Mrs. Walker came forward and stated they would be glad to answer any questions pertainingto this petition.
Mr. Moore called for comments against the petition.
