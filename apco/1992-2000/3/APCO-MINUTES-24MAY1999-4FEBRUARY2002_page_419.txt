On November 16, 2000 at 6:00 P.M. the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors met at the
Appomattox County Courthouse, Courthouse Square, Court Street, Appomattox, Va.
PRESENT: Russell H. Moore, Chairman - Piney Mountain District
Jerry Kendrick - Appomattox River District
Jesse Hancock - Falling River District
Frank H. Carwile - Wreck Island District
Samuel Carter - Courthouse District
ALSO PRESENT: = Andy Carroll - County Administrator
At 6:00 P.M. Mr. Moore called the meeting to order and stated that the purpose of this meeting
was to interview architects for the Courthouse Renovation.
Mr. Moore explained that a closed session was necessary in accordance with the Code of
Virginia, 1950 as amended, Code Section 2.1-344(A)1 for the purpose of interviewing architects
for the Courthouse Renovation Project.
At 6:05 P.M. Mr. Carter made a motion seconded by Mr. Carwile to enter into closed session to
discuss a legal and/or personnel matter. VOTE: Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Kendrick, aye; Mr.
Hancock, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; and Mr. Carter, aye.
On a motion by Mr. Carter at 9:00 P.M. seconded by Mr. Carwile it was resolved the Board of
Supervisors return to regular session. VOTE: Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Kendrick, aye; Mr. Hancock,
aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; and Mr. Carter, aye.
