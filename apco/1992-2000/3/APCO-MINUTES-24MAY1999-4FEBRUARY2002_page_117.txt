Mr. Bosiger called the meeting to order at 6:00 P.M. and asked Mr. Torrence to deliver
the invocation.

18, 1999?

1999. Vote: Mr. Bosiger, aye; Mr. Carter, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye, Mr. Moore, aye; Mr.
