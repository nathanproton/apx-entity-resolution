A public hearing of the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors was held on February 28, 2000
at 6:00 P.M. at Appomattox County High School in the cafeteria, Route 727, Appomattox,
Virginia.
Present:
Russell H. Moore - Chairman - Piney Mountain District
Samuel E. Carter - Appomattox Courthouse District
Frank H. Carwile - Wreck Island District
Jesse L. Hancock - Falling River District
Jerry G. Kendrick - Appomattox River District
Also Present:
Aileen T. Ferguson - County Administrator
Mr. Caldwell - Clerk - Planning Commission
Wayne Lewis - Planning Commission
Al Sears - Planning Commission
Mr. Moore called the meeting to order and asked Mr. Carter to deliver the invocation.
Mr. Moore then introduced Mr. Wayne Lewis, a member of the Appomattox County Planning
Commission, and stated that Mr. Lewis would present a brief overview of the proposed
ordinance. Mr. Moore continued that after Mr. Lewis’ presentation there would be a five minute
recess in order to allow citizens time to review the overlays that Mr. Lewis had prepared. Mr.
Moore then stated that each individual would be given an opportunity to address comments to the
Board and explained the procedure for addressing comments.
Mr. Moore then asked Mr. Lewis to proceed with his presentation.
Mr. Lewis began by saying that the proposed ordinance had been published in it’s entirety in the
newspaper and that copies were available at the door for those that wished to receive them. Mr.
Lewis then read the intent of the proposed ordinance and gave a very informative presentation
explaining the highlights of the ordinance as he saw them.
Mr. Moore then called for a brief recess in order to allow citizens time to review the materials
provided by Mr. Lewis.
