1. Redistricting: Since there was no opposition to the Redistricting Plan from the public hearing,
Mr. Kendrick stated the Board should act on the Redistricting Plan as presented on May 7, 2001.
Mr. Carter stated that with the time and effort spent by the Redistricting Committee and the plan that
was presented to the citizens of Appomattox County, he makes a motion that the Board adopt Plan
7 as so stipulated. Mr. Moore seconded the motion. VOTE: Mr. Kendrick, aye; Mr. Carter, aye; Mr.
Hancock, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; and Mr. Moore, aye.
At 7:24 P.M. Mr. Kendrick made a motion seconded by Mr. Hancock to adjourn. VOTE: Mr.
Kendrick, aye; Mr. Carter, aye; Mr. Hancock, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; and Mr. Moore, aye.
