On Monday, December 3, 2001 the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors held a joint PublicHearing with the Virginia Department of Transportation at the Appomattox County Courthouse,
Courthouse Square, Court Street, Appomattox, VA.
PRESENT: _ Jerry G. Kendrick, Chairman - Appomattox River District
Samuel E. Carter - Courthouse District
Jesse L. Hancock - Falling River District
Frank H. Carwile - Wreck Island District
Russell H. Moore - Piney Mountain District
ALSO PRESENT:
Andy Carroll - County Administrator
Brandon Stidham - County Planner
At 6:01 p.m. Mr. Kendrick opened the Public Hearing on the Annual VDOT6 Year Plan and
Secondary Road System. Mr. Kendrick then turned the meeting over to Mr. Don Austin,
Resident Engineer, Appomattox Residency Office, VDOT.
Mr. Austin began by introducing his staff:
Milton Thacker - Assistant Resident Engineer
Don Harvey - Maintenance Manager
Cathy Arrington - Contract Administrator
Mr. Austin began by stating this would be a combined hearing of the Six Year Plan and also for
the budget for the upcoming year 2002-2003.
Mr. Austin briefly described the Six Year Plan and the unpaved road priority list. This list is
developed with the assistance of the Board of Supervisors. Mr. Austin discussed the estimated
allocations summary. This is just an estimate of allocated funds. There will probably be some
cuts in the allocation funds due to the shortfall of revenues expected.
Mr. Austin also explained the secondary system construction program, and how to read the charts
presented.
