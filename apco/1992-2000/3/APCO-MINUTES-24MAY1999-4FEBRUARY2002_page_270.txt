Mr. Pugh began by giving his address as Route 1, Spout Spring. Mr. Pugh stated that he had a great

Mr. Moore then called on Mr. Raymond Mullins to appear before the Board.
Mr. Mullins gave his address as Route 1, Appomattox, Virginia and continued that he hunts with
County Line Hunt Club. Mr. Mullins stated that a lot of the time they hunt in Campbell County
where the rifle is legal. Mr. Mullins continued that he felt that people have the impression that
hunters hunt in subdivisions and that this is not the case. Mr. Mullins stated that mostly hunters hunt
in large tracts of timber with no houses within one half a mile. Mr. Mullins stated that he felt that
it was safe to hunt with rifles in Appomattox County and encourage the Board to change the
ordinance accordingly.
