After discussion, Mr. Carter made a motion seconded by Mr. Torrence to deny the
request for a special exception to the subdivision ordinance for Vivian Seay (C. B. Bryant
property located on Tower Hill) and abide by our existing ordinance. Vote: Mr. Bosiger,
Mr. Carter, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.
Mr. Bosiger next explained that Robinson Farmer Cox was not ready to present the five
year financial forecast at this time but would be ready by July 22, 1999.
Mr. Bosiger then asked the other members of the Board to turn their attention to the
landfill report. Mr. Carroll reported on a meeting that he attended concerning with the
fate of 1205 landfills.
After a lengthy discussion, by consensus of the Board, it was agreed to invite Senator
Holland and Delegate Abbitt to a Board meeting to discuss our concerns with legislation
concerning 1205 landfills.
Mr. Bosiger next told the Board that the Upper Appomattox Watershed Project had been
successfully implemented and action is required by the Board to close out this project.
Mr. Carter made a motion seconded by Mr. Moore to close out the Upper Appomattox
Watershed Project since it has been successfully implemented. Vote: Mr. Bosiger, aye;
Mr. Carter, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.
Mr. Bosiger then presented supplemental appropriation requests from various
departments.
Mr. Carwile made a motion seconded by Mr. Torrence to appropriate the following funds
from the I.D.A. bond:
$40,000.00 to the Appomattox County Rescue Squad Building Fund
$10,000.00 to the Concord Rescue Squad on their crash truck
$10,000.00 to the Concord Fire Department Building Fund
Vote: Mr. Bosiger, aye; Mr. Carter, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr.
Torrence, aye.
Mr. Bosiger then asked the Board to appropriate funds for the Court Security and
Resource Officer positions at the Sheriff's Department.
Mr. Torrence made a motion seconded by Mr. Carwile to appropriate the following funds
for the two court security positions at the Sheriff's Department:

3,402.00 to 3102-2001
6,212.00 to 3102-2002
4,300.00 to 3102-2005
25.00 to 3102-2009
Vote: Mr. Bosiger, aye; Mr. Carter, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr.
Torrence, aye.
