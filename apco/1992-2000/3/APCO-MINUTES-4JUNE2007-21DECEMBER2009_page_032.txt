At 6:15 p.m. Mr. Carter opened the public hearing and read the following statement that
had been published in the Times-Virginian newspaper:
PUBLIC HEARING
Notice is hereby given that the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors will conduct a
public hearing on Monday, July 2, 2007 at 6:15 p.m. at the Appomattox Community
Center located on Community Lane, Appomattox, VA.
The purpose of this hearing will be to consider the following amendment to Article
XVIII — Section 147-64.1 Assessment of Conviction Fees — to increase conviction
fees to fund additional courthouse security personnel.
Changes are shown in /ta/ics. The amendment is as follows:
Article XVIII — Ordinance on Conviction Fees to Fund Courthouse Security Personnel.
Section 147-61. Assessment of Conviction Fees
A. Fee Amount. A fee in the amount of five fen dollars (45:08) ($20.00) per
criminal or traffic case shall be imposed upon any individual convicted of
violation of any stature or ordinance in either the General District or Circuit
Court of Appomattox.
