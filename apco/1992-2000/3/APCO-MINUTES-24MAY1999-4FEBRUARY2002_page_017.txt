Mr. Bosiger called the public hearing to order stated the purpose of the hearing was to
receive public comment on several zoning petitions. Mr. Bosiger then gave the
procedure for addressing comments.
Mr. Bosiger called on Mr. Caldwell to read the first petition.
Mr. Caldwell read petition number 99-381 of Tammy Franklin Chandler for a conditional
use permit to allow mobile home in Village District. Property is Tax Map No. 41-(A)-46
located on Route 616 between Route 655 and Route 24.
Mr. Bosiger called for comments on this petition.
Hearing none, Mr. Bosiger asked Mr. Caldwell to read the next petition.
Mr. Caldwell read petition number 99-382 of Obie Fred Chandler and Timothy S.
Chandler to rezone property from R-1 to A-1. Property is Tax Map Nos. 41-5-3 and 41-
5-2 and is located on Route 697 between Route 24 and Route 24.
Mr. Bosiger called for comments on this petition.
Mr. Obie Chandler came forward and gave his address as Route 3 Box 431, Appomattox,
Virginia. Mr. Chandler explained that he needed to put a doublewide mobile home on
this property for his son and his son’s four children.
Mr. Bosiger called for other comments.
Hearing none, Mr. Bosiger asked Mr. Caldwell to read the next petition.
