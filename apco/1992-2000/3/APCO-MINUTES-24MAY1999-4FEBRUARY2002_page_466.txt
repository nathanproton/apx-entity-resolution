At 7:00 P.M. Mr. Kendrick called the Public Hearing to order and stated the purpose of the
Public Hearing was to receive comments regarding several petitions for exceptions to the
Appomattox County Zoning Ordinance. Mr. Kendrick also stated the guidelines for comments.
Questions and discussion will be heard by the Planning Commission following the close of the
Public Hearing.
Mr. Kendrick asked Mr. Stidham to read the first petition:
01-451 - Michael T. Powers: Conditional Use Permit to place self storage business on lot in B-1
district per Section 170-47 of the Appomattox County Zoning Ordinance. Property is located on
Route 460 West between Route 609 and Route 608, Tax Map No. 60-(8)-11 and is zoned B-1.
Mr. Michael Powers of 1549 Cedar Bluff Trail, Marietta, Ga 30062 came froward and stated he
would like to place a 194 unit self-storage facility with one (1) residential unit for on site security
on this property. This would be an all paved, gated facility with little impact on utilities and no
impact on schools. It would also provide a community service.
Mr. Stidham then read the next petition.
01-452 - Tracy M. Campbell: Variance to Section 170-80 of the Appomattox County Zoning
Ordinance pertaining to side yard setback requirements. Property is located on Route 608
between Route 26 and Route 712. Tax Map No. 38(A)103A and is zoned A-1.
Mr. Randy Campbell of Route 3, Box 318, Appomattox, came forward and stated that his side
yard is not consistent with the Code. Somehow the mobile home was put to close to the line.
Mr. Stidham read the next petition.
