Mr. Moore called the Public Hearing to order and stated the purpose of the hearing was to receive
comments regarding several petitions for exceptions to the Appomattox County Zoning Ordinance.
Mr. Moore also asked that comments be limited to those in favor of or against the petitions be made
during the hearing. Other questions and discussion will be heard by the Planning Commission on
this meeting which will follow the public hearing.
Mr. Moore asked Mr. Stidham to read the first petition.
Petition #00-448 - O. H. Tolley, Jr. - Conditional Use Permit for self-storage building per Section
170-47 of the Appomattox County Zoning Ordinance. Property is located on a double-frontage lot
fronting on Route 460 and Route 628, Tax Map No. 102-(A)81, and is zoned B-1.
Mr. Moore called for comments for or against this petition.
Hearing none, Mr. Moore asked Mr. Stidham to read the next petition.
Petition #00-449 - William E. Jamerson - Conditional Use Permit to place a second dwelling on
lot in A-1 district per Section 170-23 of the Appomattox County Zoning Ordinance. Property is
located on Route 727 between Route 604 and Route 642, Tax Map No. 76-(6)B, and is zoned A-1.
Mr. Moore called for comments.
Hearing none, Mr. Moore called for the next petition to be read.
Petition #00-450 - George Manson - Conditional Use Permit to place second dwelling on lot in A-1
district per Section 170-23 of the Appomattox County Zoning Ordinance. Property is located on
Route 644 between Route 649 and Route 716, Tax Map #113(1)4A and is zoned A-1.
Mr. Moore called for comments for this petition.
Mrs. Dolly Manson, HCR Box 1980, Red House, Va. spoke on behalf of the petition. Mrs. Manson
stated they had began this project in the summer of last year and had received a stop work order in
November. They had already put in the well and septic system. The electric connections had been
put in and new siding on the single wide in anticipation of providing a home for their son.
