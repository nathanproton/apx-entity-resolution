Section 170-113-Permitted Uses
(A) Co-locations in the following zoning districts: A-1, B-1, M-1, M-2, and 4-J/P provided that the
height of the existing structure is not increased more than 20 feet of its original permitted height.
(Cc) New construction towers that do not exceed 199 feet in height that are proposed for siting in a B-1,
M-1, M-2, and M-/P zoning district, provided the site does not border a lot zoned H-1, R-1, R-2,
or V-1.
Section 170-114 Conditional Uses
(A) New construction towers that do not exceed 199 feet in height that are proposed for siting in an A-
1, B-1, M-1, M-2, or M-/P zoning district bordering an H-1, R-1, R-2, or V-1 district.
(B) New construction towers that exceed 199 feet in height that are proposed for siting in an A-1, B-1,
M-1,
M-2, or M-IP zoning district.
