At 7:00 p.m. Mr. Carter called for the APPEARANCES:
Jillian Mitchel: Ms. Mitchel has requested a time to discuss the lease for the old
Courthouse with the Board. Ms. Mitchel is moving from the area and will no longer be
able to lease the old courthouse for the theater. There is another group that is
interested in taking over the lease and continuing to use the facility for a theater.
Ms. Mitchel stated she had enjoyed working with the Board in getting the Appomattox
Courthouse Theater open. She explained that her family would be moving from
Appomattox in the fall. She feels she has been blessed in that she has found the right
people to continue operating The Appomattox Gallery and Tea Room, the Bed and
Breakfast, as well as the Theater as she has in the past.
Mr. Carter stated that Ms. Mitchel has been an asset to our community and thanked her
for all that she has done to provide cultural arts to our citizens.
Each of the Board Members expressed their thanks to Ms. Mitchel for being such a vital
part of our community and wished her well in her future endeavors.
Ms. Mitchel informed the Board that five committed and talented local citizens would
love to continue the non profit community theater and wish to assume direction of the
Appomattox Courthouse Theatre, non-profit, and lease the old courthouse from the
County for the same purpose.
