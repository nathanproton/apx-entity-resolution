Mr. Bosiger called the meeting to order at 7:26 P.M. and delivered the invocation.
Mr. Bosiger then called for additions or corrections to the minutes.
Mr. Torrence made a motion seconded by Mr. Carter to approve the minutes of the joint
public hearing of July 1999 as presented. Vote: Mr. Bosiger, aye; Mr. Carter, aye; Mr.
Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.
Mr. Bosiger next called for approval of the bills.
Mr. Carter made a motion seconded by Mr. Torrence to approve the bills of August 16,
1999 for payment. Vote: Mr. Bosiger, aye; Mr. Carter, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr.
Torrence, aye.
Mr. Bosiger then began with the Administrative Items.
Mr. Bosiger explained that a resolution was necessary for Country Haven Estates to
proceed with paving another portion of Main Street in order for it to be taken into the
State highway system. Mr. Bosiger continued that Country Haven Estates Homeowners
Association had deposited $40,575.67 with the County to cover their portion of the
construction cost and that this would need to be appropriated for payment to the State.
Mr. Torrence made a motion seconded by Mr. Carter to adopt the following resolution
and appropriate $40,575.67 to pay for construction to a portion of Main Street in Country
Haven Estates.
WHEREAS, the property owners in Country Haven Subdivision located on Route 750,
0.37 Mile West of Route 611 in Stonewall Magisterial District have requested the
Virginia Department of Transportation to provide an estimate for the construction of 0.63
miles of Main Street from Route 750 (0.37 mile west of Route 611) to 0.63 mile west
Route 750 to the requirements of Virginia Department of Transportation and
WHEREAS, the Virginia Department of Transportation has provided an estimate for the
cost for said subdivision street and the affected property owners have donated throughout
the county $40,575.67 fifty (50) percent of the estimate.
NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, that the Virginia Department of
Transportation hereby is requested to add to the Appomattox County Secondary Road
System for construction and maintenance the following section of road in accordance
with Section 33.1-72.1, paragraph C, of the Code of Virginia 1950, as amended.
MAINSTREET, Beginning at the existing end of state maintenance of Route 750 (0.37
mile west Route 611) and running northwest for a distance of 0.63 mile.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that this Board does guarantee a minimum fifty (50)
foot right of way with additional widths as may be necessary for cuts, fills and drainage,
together with easements for drainage on adjacent properties.
