the following conditions:
(1) From a point near the southeast corner along Phoebe Pond Road and

shall remain alive and in good condition in perpetuity. Planting should occur

(2) All area to be used for the display of and/or storage of automobiles and all
Customer parking areas shall be paved within one (1) year of the issuance of
