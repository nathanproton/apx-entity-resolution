On Monday, March 17, 2008 at 6:30 p.m. the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors
held their regular scheduled meeting at the Appomattox Community Center, located on
220 Community Lane, Appomattox, Virginia,
Present: William H. Craft — Appomattox River District
Samuel E. Carter — Courthouse District
Thomas H. Conrad ~ Falling River District
Russell H. Moore — Piney Mountain District
Gary W. Tanner — Wreck Island District
Also Present: Aileen T, Ferguson — County Administrator
John K. Spencer — Assistant County Administrator
J. G. Overstreet - County Attorney
Johnnie Roark — Planner
Vicky Phelps — Administrative Assistant
Chairman Craft called the meeting to order at 6:30 p.m. and called on Mr. Tanner to
deliver the invocation. Everyone joined in the Pledge of Allegiance to the American Flag.
Chairman Craft welcomed everyone to the meeting and called for the setting of the
agenda. Ms. Ferguson provided two additional items to be added to the consent agenda
for consideration.
Chairman Craft next called for citizen public comments.
Chairman Craft called on Mr. Ray Perkins to speak.
Mr. Perkins stated that he was speaking to the Board on behalf of the Appomattox Youth
Sports and various Community sport activities involving the youth.
Mr. Perkins stated that he has been an active volunteer at the new community park since
1991. He stated that he feels very passionate about this Community Park and the youth
of the community.
Mr. Perkins stated his main reason for speaking with the Board tonight is asking for
money to get this park finished. He stated that this park is a gold mine. In his traveling
to other counties for sport events there is a lot of money spent not only at the park where
the games are being played, but within the towns also.
Mr. Perkins stated he is requesting a minimum of $75,000 in the budget to help with
power, water and sewer at the Community Park.
