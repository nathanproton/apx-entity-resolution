The School Board is requesting to be allowed to cruise the timber on the new school site and receive
sealed bids for the approximately 30 acres that need to be cleared. The proceeds from the sale of this
timber would go toward the development of the new elementary school.
Mr. Kendrick stated he had no problem with the sale of this timber but feels the proceeds should go
into the General Revenue Fund because of the $9 million dollar cap on the elementary school.
Mr. Carwile made a motion seconded by Mr. Carter to allow the School Board to have the timber
cruised and proceeds to be placed in the General Revenue Fund. VOTE: Mr. Kendrick, aye; Mr.
Hancock, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; and Mr. Carter, aye.
Virginia Region 2000 is opposing the merger of United Airlines and U. S. Airways because they feel
this would have a detrimental effect on airline customers in the Central Virginia area. They are
requesting that the Board send a letter to the U. S. Department of Justice opposing this merger.
Mr. Carwile made a motion seconded by Mr. Carter that the Board of Supervisors send a letter to
the Department of Justice opposing the merger of United Airlines and U. S. Airways. VOTE: Mr.
Kendrick, aye; Mr. Hancock, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; and Mr. Carter, aye.
