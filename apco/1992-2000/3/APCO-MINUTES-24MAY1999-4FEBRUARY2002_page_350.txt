On June 29, 2000 the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors held a joint meeting with the
Appomattox County School Board at the Appomattox County Courthouse, Courthouse Square,
Court Street, Appomattox, VA at 6:00 P.M.

Mr. Moore called the meeting to order at 6:15 P. M. and stated the purpose of the meeting was to
discuss the FY 2001 Budget.
Mr. Moore then invited the School Board Members to join the Board in discussing the FY2001
School Budget.
Mr. Moore explained to the School Board that the Board of Supervisors would like to hear from
them regarding where they stood on the revisions to their Budget.
There was much discussion regarding the minimum amount of funding from the locality and projects
involving the school. Also discussed were the State matching funds.
There was a lot of discussion about how both Boards could work together to be able to obtain all the
things we need to have done, like the elementary school, the Courthouse and the new landfill cell.
