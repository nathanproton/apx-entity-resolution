Mr. Bosiger then called on Mrs. Renee Hoffman to appear before the Board.
Mrs. Hoffman began that she has come before the Board many times over the last few
years with her concerns for the children. Mrs. Hoffman continued that two of her
children have attended this elementary school. Mrs. Hoffman expressed her concerns
with the present building and site. Mrs. Hoffman explained that she is not in favor of
renovating the elementary school. Mrs. Hoffman continued that she felt there were a lot
of uses for the present building that would benefit the community. Mrs. Hoffman asked
the Board to look to the future and not try to piecemeal these buildings.
Mr. Bosiger next called on Shana Canada to appear before the Board.
Mrs. Canada gave her address as Chestnut Drive, Appomattox. Mrs. Canada explained
that she has two little girls that will be attending school in Appomattox and she is
concerned with the school atmosphere. Mrs. Canada expressed that with the cost as close
as they are between renovation and new construction she did not understand why the
Board was still considering renovation. Mrs. Canada expressed that safety issues were a
real concern for her. In closing Mrs. Canada asked the Board to be sensitive to all the
needs and listen to God and prayerfully make the decision.
