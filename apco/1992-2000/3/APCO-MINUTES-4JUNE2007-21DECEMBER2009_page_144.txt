9. Resolution for Centra Health Bond Financing: Centra Health has
approached the EDA requesting the issuance of $7,500,000 in bank-qualified
bonds. With the assurance of bond counsel that neither the EDA nor
Appomattox County would be liable for this debt and after an advertised public
hearing in front of the EDA, the EDA passed a resolution on October 22, 2007
approving this request. According to the IRS code, the Board must approve the
bond package followed by approval from Lynchburg City Council because the
bond proceeds will be used for facility improvement within the City of Lynchburg.
Centra Health paid an application fee of $500 to the EDA and will pay a yearly
administrative fee based on the total outstanding debt.
