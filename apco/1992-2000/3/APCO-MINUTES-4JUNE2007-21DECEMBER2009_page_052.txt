On Monday, August 6, 2007 the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors conducted a
Public Hearing at 6:15 p.m. at the Appomattox Community Center located on
Community Lane, Appomattox.
PRESENT: Samuel E. Carter, Chairman — Courthouse District
Thomas H. Conrad — Falling River District
William H. Craft — Appomattox River District
Russell H. Moore — Piney Mountain District
ABSENT: Shawn A. Armbrust — Wreck Island District
ALSO PRESENT:
Aileen T. Ferguson — County Administrator
John K. Spencer, Jr. — Assistant County Administrator
John G. Overstreet — County Attorney
Johnnie Roark — County Planner
At 6:15 p.m. Mr. Carter opened the meeting and stated the purpose of the Public
Hearing was to consider the following amendment to Article XVII-Section 146-64.A
Assessment of Conviction Fees — to add Juvenile and Domestic Relations Court.
Changes are shown in italics. The amendment is as follows:
Article XVII-Ordinance on Conviction Fees to Fund Courthouse Security Personnel.
Section — 147-64 Assessment of Conviction Fees
A. Fee Amount: A fee in the amount of ten dollars ($10.00) per criminal or
traffic case shall be imposed upon any individual convicted of violation of
any stature or ordinance in either the General District, Circuit Court of
Juvenile and Domestic Relations Court of Appomattox County.
Ms. Ferguson explained that when the Board passed the change to this ordinance on
July 16, 2007, it came to their attention that the Juvenile and Domestic Relations Court
was not included. Therefore, it is necessary to amend this ordinance to include the
Juvenile and Domestic Relations Court.
Mr. Carter then called for comments on this amendment.
Hearing none, Mr. Carter closed the Public Hearing at 6:20 p.m.
