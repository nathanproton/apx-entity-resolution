3: Landfill Collection Policy: In the past we have not had any formal guidelines for collection
of fees owed to the County for landfill use and the proposed policy should alleviate some
problems that are currently being experienced. Approval for the purchase of new
software/hardware to better implement this policy is also being requested.
Costs for this program will be approximately $10,000 and we are currently owed past due
fees of over $17,000.
After some discussion, Mr. Carwile made a motion seconded by Mr. Moore in support of this policy
and the purchase of software/hardware as proposed. VOTE: Mr. Kendrick, aye; Mr. Hancock, aye;
Mr. Carwile, aye; and Mr. Moore, aye.
