A recessed meeting and joint work session of the Appomattox County Board of
Supervisors and the Appomattox County School Board was held on August 10, 1999 in
the School Board Conference Room, North Court Street, Appomattox, Virginia.

Mr. Bosiger called the meeting to order at 7:00 P.M. and welcomed everyone in
attendance. Mr. Bosiger next delivered the invocation. Mr. Bosiger then asked that each
Supervisor introduce his citizen representative on the elementary school committee.
Mr. Bosiger then provided a brief background of the elementary school project and stated
that the purpose of the meeting was to try to answer any questions that members of the
committee may have.
