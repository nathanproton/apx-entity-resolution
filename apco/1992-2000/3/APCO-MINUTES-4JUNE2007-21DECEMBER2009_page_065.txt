Mr. Conrad stated he thought that based on the problems we have at some of the
dumpster sites with trash being thrown here and there and everywhere, that we would
like to go with the manned sites certainly to stop the amount of trash that is thrown
everywhere and certainly encourage recycling more which will be mandated more and
more in the future. Based on looking at the map, he is going to support Mr. Craft on
this because if you remove the 620 site, as well as the Hixburg site, he does not feel we
would be doing the citizens in that area justice. He thinks that in the long haul with
manning the sites, it will cost us some money with the less costs being transporting
trash.
Mr. Carter stated that from the discussion from a couple of weeks ago, it would cause
some hardship on people in the Pamplin/Hixburg area as they would have to travel
further to a dumpster site.
Ms. Ferguson said she, Ms. Rockefeller and Mr. Craft have discussed the Route 620 site
and if we could get a little bit more area, it might work better. There will be more cost
involved in getting this site ready than some of the other sites. Ms. Rockefeller will
begin looking more closely on how to handle this site.
Mr. Conrad made a motion, seconded by Mr. Carter, to close two (2) dumpster sites -
Hixburg (Route 618) and Ida Campbell's site (Routes 667 & 668). ROLL CALL VOTE:
Mr. Armbrust, aye; Mr. Conrad, aye; Mr. Craft, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; and Mr. Carter, aye.
(5 aye votes)
