Personnel Policy: At the last meeting Mr. Carroll presented a draft of the proposed Personnel Policy
for County Employees. Does the Board have any comments on this policy at this time?
There was a general discussion regarding this policy draft. Mr. Kendrick has some suggestions and
Mr. Carwile would like to see the policy condensed.
On the issue of Salary Scales the Board by consensus approved for Mr. Carroll to proceed with
getting prices on having this work done.

Financing of New Elementary School: Mr. Carroll is requesting the Board allow him to request
bids from financial institutions for interim financing for the New Elementary School project. He has

Mr. Carwile, aye. Mr. Hancock still in Planning Commission meeting.
