Board Appointments:
5. a. Library Board: The Library Board is in need of an appointment from the
Appomattox River District. This item was tabled from the last meeting.
Mr. Kendrick stated he has not made any contacts at this time and would like to table this item until
a future meeting.
b. Workforce Investment Board Appointment: Mr. George Taylor, President of
Process Pipe and Welding has agreed to serve on this Board as a representative from
the private sector if appointed.
Mr. Carter made a motion seconded by Mr. Moore to appoint Mr. George Taylor as a representative
from the private sector on the Workforce Investment Board. VOTE: Mr. Kendrick, aye; Mr. Carter,
aye; Mr. Hancock, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; and Mr. Moore, aye.
6. Board of Equalization: The Board of Supervisors has received a letter indicating that a
Board of Equalization needs to be appointed so they can receive adequate training.
There was some discussion as to whether we wanted to elect a three (3) or (5) member Board.
Mr. Kendrick stated that it was the consensus of the Board to elect five (5) members to this Board
with maybe two (2) extras. The Board will accept suggestions at the next meeting.
2. VPSA Bond Resolution: After having had a Public Hearing to receive comments on this
Resolution, the Board needs to adopt the VPSA Bond Resolution for financing the new
elementary school.

follows:
