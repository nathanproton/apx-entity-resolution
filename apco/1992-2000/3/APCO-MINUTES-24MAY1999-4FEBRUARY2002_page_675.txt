The Organizational Meeting of the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors was held on
Monday, January 7, 2002 at 6:00 P.M. at the Appomattox County Courthouse, Courthouse
Square, Court Street, Appomattox, VA.
PRESENT: _ Jerry G. Kendrick - Appomattox River District
Samuel E. Carter - Courthouse District
Jesse L. Hancock - Falling River District
Frank H. Carwile - Wreck Island District
Russell H. Moore - Piney Mountain District
ALSO PRESENT:
Andy Carroll - County Administrator
This being the Organizational Meeting, the County Administrator called the meeting to order at6:00 P.M. and asked Mr. Carter to deliver the invocation.
Mr Carroll called for nominations for Chairman of the Board for 2002, stating there was a
procedure outline for nominations on page 1 of the Board Book.
Mr. Hancock nominated Mr. Carter for the position of Chairman for the upcoming year.
Hearing no other nominations for Chairman, Mr. Carroll called for a vote on the nomination ofMr. Carter for Chairman. VOTE: Mr. Kendrick, aye; Mr. Hancock, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr.Moore, aye, and Mr. Carter, aye.
Mr. Carroll stated that on a vote of 5 to 0, Mr. Carter was elected the Chairman for the upcoming
year.
Mr. Carroll then turned the meeting over the Mr. Carter.
