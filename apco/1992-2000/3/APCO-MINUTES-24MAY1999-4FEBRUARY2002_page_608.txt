At 7:02 P.M. Mr. Kendrick opened the Public Hearing and stated to those in attendance that the
Public Hearing is not the appropriate forum to engage in discussion on the petitions. Discussion
will take place during the Planning Commission meeting that will immediately follow the public
meeting in the Jury Room off to the right. We will begin a hearing of each petition with
comments by the petitioner, followed by comments from individuals to speak for the petition and
finally by individuals wishing to speak against the petition. As you approach the microphone,
please state your name, address and whether you are for or against the petition.
Mr. Kendrick then turned the meeting over to Mr. Brandon Stidham, County Planner.
Mr. Stidham read the first petition:
01-483 - Michael D. Whitmer - Variance to §170-81(D) of the Appomattox County Zoning
Ordinance pertaining to minimum rear yard setback distances for accessory buildings.
Property is located on Rt. 703 between U. S. 460 and Rt. 685, Tax Map #62(A)5, and is
zoned R-1.
Mr. Stidham called for comments for or against this petition.
Mr. Michael Whitmer, of Route 1, Box 3165, Spout Spring, Va.. Mr. Whitmer stated there had
been an oversight in the construction process and urged the Board to approve this petition
because of costs and the hardship of removing this building.
Mr. Stidham called for comments against this petition.
