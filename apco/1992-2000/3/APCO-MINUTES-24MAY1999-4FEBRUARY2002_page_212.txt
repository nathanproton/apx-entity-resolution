A recessed meeting of the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors was held on February 15,
2000 at 6:30 P.M. in the Courthouse, Courthouse Square, North Court Street, Appomattox,
Virginia.
Present:
Russell H. Moore - Chairman - Piney Mountain District
Samuel E. Carter - Appomattox Courthouse District
Frank H. Carwile - Wreck Island District
Jesse L. Hancock - Falling River District
Jerry G. Kendrick - Appomattox River District
Also Present:
Aileen T. Ferguson - County Administrator
Andy Carroll - Assistant County Administrator
Mr. Moore called the meeting to order at 6:38 P.M. and asked Mr. Carter to deliver the
invocation.
Mr. Moore then explained that the purpose of the meeting was for Mr. Hal Craddock to review
the scenario for the Courthouse project in order to bring the new members of the Board up to
date.
Mr. Craddock then provided handouts with advantages and disadvantages of new construction
and renovation. Mr. Craddock also, provided handouts of cost projections for both options.
After Mr. Craddock’s presentation he answered questions of the members of the Board.
A lengthy discussion followed.
After discussion, Mr. Hancock made a motion seconded by Mr. Carter to adjourn at 7:43 P.M.
