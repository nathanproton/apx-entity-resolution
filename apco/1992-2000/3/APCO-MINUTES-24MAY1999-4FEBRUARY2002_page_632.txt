A public hearing on the Six Year Secondary Road System was scheduled for December 5, 2001
at 6:00 p.m. at the Appomattox County Courthouse.
There was a lengthy discussion regarding a traffic light at the Shoppes of Appomattox on Route
460 and/or an access road, with Mr. Austin stating this needs to be addressed in the
Transportation Plan in the Comprehensive Plan for the County.
Mr. Austin stated that there is a drainage problem on Atwood Street that may need to be
addressed. At the present time, there is no definite plan on what this will cost.
Mr. Carter questioned who was responsible for the drainage ditch off Oakleigh Avenue just off
Business Route 460. The grass and weeds are in need of cutting and some residents are
complaining of flooding during heavy rainfalls. The Town contends that this is a problem for
VDOT.
Mr. Austin stated VDOT was only responsible for problems on State acquired right of ways or if
a problem of this type caused problems for them.
At 7:00 p.m. Mr. Carter made a motion seconded by Mr. Carwile to adjourn. VOTE: Mr.
Kendrick, aye; Mr. Carter, aye; Mr. Hancock, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; and Mr. Moore, aye.
