At 6:15 P.M. Mr. Kendrick called the Visioning meeting to order with the Planning Commission,
the Board of Zoning Appeals, I.D.A. and the Town Councils opening their meetings.
Mr. John Spencer began the meeting by stating the purpose for the meeting and introduced the
speaker, Dr. Michael Chandler, Professor at Virginia Tech.
With the aid of hand-outs and flip charts, Dr. Chandler presented those present with some issues
and challenges that will be of importance to the County in developing a Comprehensive Plan in
the near future.
Mr. Kendrick excused himself at approximately 9:15 p.m.
At 9:20 P.M. on a motion by Mr. Carter seconded by Mr. Carwile the meeting was adjourned.
VOTE: Mr. Carter, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; and Mr. Moore, aye.
