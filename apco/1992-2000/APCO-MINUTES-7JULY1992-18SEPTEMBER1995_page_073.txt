The public hearing having been duly advertised was called to
order at 7:30 P.M. by W. E. "Bill" Jamerson, presiding. Mr.
Jamerson stated the purpose of the hearing and explained the
procedure to be followed.
Mr. Jamerson then asked Mr. Caldwell to read the first
Petition to be considered. Mr. Caldwell read application
92-179 of GTE Virginia, Inc. requesting a variance to
paragraph 904 of the Appomattox County Zoning Ordinance
pertaining to Front Yard Setback. The property is located on
Route 656 between Route 706 and Route 657 and is zoned A-1.
Mr. Jamerson asked to hear from the petitioner. Mr. Fred
Nicely from Copper and Associates spoke as the engineer for
GTE. Mr. Nicely stated he was present to answer any
questions and presented photographs of similar installations.
Some of the photographs pertained to Petition 92-179 and some
to Petition 92-180. Mr. Jamerson noted the applicant for
both petitions was GTE and asked Mr. Caldwell to read
Petition 92-180 so it could be considered jointly with
petition 92-179.
Mr. Caldwell read application 92-180 of GTE Virginia. GTE is
requesting a Conditional Use Permit to allow installation of
a remote communication switching facility on a property zoned
M-1 located on Route 460 between Route 1045 and Route 613.
Mr. Caldwell pointed out the equipment to be installed was
Similar to that proposed under petition 92-179.
Mr. Jamerson asked Mr. Nicely to distribute the photographs
to the Planning Commission as well as the Board of
Supervisors. After the photos had been reviewed, Mr.
Jamerson asked if there were any questions of Mr. Nicely.
Hearing none, Mr. Jamerson asked if there was anyone else who
would like to speak regarding petition 92-179 and/or 92-180.
Hearing none, Mr. Jamerson asked Mr. Caldwell to read the
next petition to be considered.
