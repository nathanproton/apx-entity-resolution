WHEREAS, the new Code Section 15.2-2286.1 B, that requires each locality to

County, Virginia that the Governor and General Assembly of the State of Virginia are
strongly urged to:
I. Make cluster development an optional land use tool for local governments.
2. Allow for the approval of cluster developments through a special use, special
exemption or conditional use process.
