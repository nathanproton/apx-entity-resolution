Mr. Moore called the Public Hearing to order at 7:00 P.M.
Mr. Moore then stated the purpose of the hearing was to receive comments on petitions for variance
to the Appomattox County Zoning Code. Mr. Moore also explained the procedure for receiving
comments.
Mr. Moore also stated for the record that no questions be asked from the floor during the hearing
process. If you have questions, you may present them to the Planning Commission at it’s meeting
following the public hearing.
Mr. Moore then asked Mr. Stidham to read the first petition:
Petition #00-442 - George F. Creamer, III - Variance to Section 170-81(C)(3) pertaining to
maximum height restrictions on accessory buildings. Property is part of Tax Map #67-A-3, zoned
A-1, and is located on Route 616, 0.2 miles from Route 614.
Mr. Moore asked if anyone wished to speak for this petition.
Mr. Creamer came forward and stated he lived in a mobile home only 8 feet high. He would like to
construct a building for storage, housing and maintaining farm equipment which requires a height
of approximately 18 feet.
