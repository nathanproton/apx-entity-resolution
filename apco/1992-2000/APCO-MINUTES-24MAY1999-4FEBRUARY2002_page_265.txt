Mr. Moore next asked if anyone had any other business for the Board?
Mr. Kendrick explained that he would like to request that the Educational Advisory Committee
be changed to an Educational Finance Committee comprised of two members of the Board of
Supervisors, two members of the School Board, Mr. Perrow and Mrs. F erguson.
After discussion and by consensus of the Board it was agreed that Mr. Moore and Mr. Kendrick
would serve on this committee representing the Board of Supervisors.
Mr. Kendrick made a motion to revise the Educational Advisory Committee to an Educational
Finance Committee with two members of the Board of Supervisors, two members of the School
Board, Mr. Perrow and Mrs. Ferguson serving on this committee for the sole purpose of coming
to a financial agreement between the two Boards. Vote: all aye.
Being no further business, at 8:40 P.M., Mr. Kendrick made a motion seconded by Mr. Carter to
recess until March 28, 2000 at 6:00 P.M. in the Courthouse. Vote: Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Carter,
ra aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Kendrick, aye.
Russell H. Moore, Chairman
