Mr. Kendrick next called Mr. Bill Coalson of Tri-County Appraisals to give an update on the process
of the reassessment.
Mr. Coalson gave a little background of himself and his Company. He stated the reassessment is on
schedule per contract. Mr. Coalson also stated some of the criteria he uses in assessing property,
such as market value where a property would sell higher to an outsider than to a family member per
se. The purpose of reassessment is for equalization (people to pay their fair share). To make sure
that every one is taxed fairly. 100% of market value and equalized. There are two (2) things to
evaluate when doing reassessment: (1) Condition and (2) Quality of construction.
