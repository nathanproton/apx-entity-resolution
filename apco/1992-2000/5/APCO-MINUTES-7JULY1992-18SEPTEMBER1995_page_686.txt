Mr. Paulette next directed the Board’s attention to a letter from Bob
Bradner, Director, Court Services Unit to handle funds allocated to the
County under the Virginia Juvenile Community Crime Control Act.
After discussion, Mr. Carwile made a motion seconded by Mr. Moore to
ask Mr. Bob Brander, Director, Court Services Unit, to apply for
Appomattox County’s VJCCCA funding in the amount of $2,494.00. Vote:
Mr. Paulette, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye
and Mr. Torrence, aye.
Mr. Paulette next asked the Board if they wished to sign the agreement
for the Southside Economic Development Grant.
After discussion, this matter was held over for the October 1995
meeting.
Mr. Paulette next directed the Board’s attention to a letter from Dr.
Thomas, requesting the Board change their earlier decision to stay out
of the Athletic fields, at the Middle School, with the new addition.
After discussion and by consensus of the Board, it was agreed to stay
with the original decision.
Mr. Paulette read a request from Central Virginia Area Agency on Aging
to eliminate landfill user fees for their Emergency Home Repair and
Weatherization programs.
After discussion and by consensus of the Board, Mrs. Ferguson was
instructed to notify Mr. John Bodtmann that the Board would consider
his request on a case by case basis.
