Mr. Torrence stated that since no one had submitted an application for
the School Board position, for Appomattox River District, he had asked
Mr. Sonny Tolley if he would continue to serve until December 31,
1995. Mr. Torrence continued by stating that Mr. Tolley had agreed to
serve, if the Board so desired.
Mr. Torrence made a motion seconded by Mrs. Beasley to reappoint Mr. O.
H. "Sonny" Tolley to the School Board, serving the Appomattox River
District, for a term from July 1, 1995 to December 31, 1995. Vote: Mr.
Paulette, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; and
Mr. Torrence, aye.
Mr. Paulette explained there were two qualified applications for School
Board serving Courthouse District, Mrs. Terri Conrad and Dr. Richard
Poe. Mr. Paulette continued by stating he had received a number of
telephone calls, concerning this appointment, and they were all in
support of Richard Poe.
Mr. Torrence made a motion seconded by Mrs. Beasley to reappoint Dr.
Richard Poe to the School Board serving Courthouse District, for a term
from July 1, 1995 to December 31, 1996. Vote: Mr. Paulette, aye; Mrs.
Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; and Mr. Torrence, aye.
