A recessed meeting of the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors was
held on July 17, 1995 at 7:25 p.m. in the Courthouse, Courthouse
Square, North Court Street, Appomattox, Virginia.
Present:
Nina B. Beasley - Falling River District
Frank H. Carwile - Wreck Island District
Dennis W. Torrence - Appomattox River District (Presiding)
Absent:
Philip E. Paulette - Courthouse District
Russell H. Moore - Piney Mountain District
Also Present:
Aileen T. Ferguson - County Administrator
Mr. Torrence, presiding, called the meeting to order and explained
there were several items that needed action by the Board.
Mr. Torrence continued by presenting request from Monte Mays, Sara
Wooten and Janet Belter, for their expenses to be paid to Local
Government Officials Conference.
After discussion, Mrs. Beasley made a motion seconded by Mr. Carwile tq
pay expenses for Local Government Officials Conference from 1101-5504
for Monte Mays, Sara Wooten, and Janet Belter. Vote: Mr. Torrence,
aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; and Mr. Carwile, aye.
Mr. Torrence then gave a report of the cost of publishing delinquent
tax list.
After discussion, Mrs. Beasley made a motion seconded by Mr. Carwile tq
publish the delinquent real estate and personal property tax list for
one (1) week at a cost of $650.00. Vote: Mr. Torrence, aye; Mrs.
Beasley, aye; and Mr. Carwile, aye.
