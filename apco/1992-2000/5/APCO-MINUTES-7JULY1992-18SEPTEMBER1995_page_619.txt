A public hearing of the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors was held
on May 15, 1995 at 6:30 P.M. in the School Board Conference Room, North
Court Street, Appomattox, VA.
Present:
Mr. Philip E. Paulette - Chairman - Courthouse District
Mr. Frank H. Carwile - Wreck Island District
Mr. Russell H. Moore - Piney Mountain District
Mr. Dennis W. Torrence - Appomattox River District
Absent:
Ms. Nina B. Beasley - Falling River District
Also Present:
Ms. Aileen T. Ferguson, County Administrator
Mr. Paulette called the public hearing to order and stated the purpose
of the hearing was to receive public comments on proposed changes to
the Restaurant Ordinance Section #129-4.
Mr. Paulette continued by saying the section presently reads:
Sec. 129-4 Enforcement:
"This Article shall be enforced by the Appomattox County Health
Department Agent or his duly authorized representative"; and the
proposed change would read:
"This Article shall be enforced by the Virginia Department of
Agriculture & Consumer Services."
Mr. Paulette called for public comments.
Hearing none, the public hearing was closed at 6:37 P.M.
