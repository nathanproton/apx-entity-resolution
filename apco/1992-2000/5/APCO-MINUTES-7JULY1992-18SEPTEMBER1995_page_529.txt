A recessed meeting of the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors was
held on November 8, 1994 at 1:00 P.M. at the container site at the
intersection of route 460 and route 648, Spout Spring, Virginia.
Present:
Mr. Dennis W. Torrence, Chairman -Appomattox River District
Mrs. Nina B. Beasley - Falling River District
Mr. Frank H. Carwile - Wreck Island District
Mr. Russell H. Moore - Piney Mountain District
Mr. Philip "Gus" Paulette - Courthouse District
Also Present:
Mrs. Aileen T. Ferguson - County Administrator
Mr. Charlie Peterson - Solid Waste Administrator
Mr. Torrence called the meeting to order at 1:00 P.M. and stated the
purpose of the meeting was to review this site for a possible "super
site".
Mr. Peterson explained how he felt the "super site" could be designed
utilizing the available space.
After a lengthy discussion, and by consensus of the Board of
Supervisor’s, Mrs. Ferguson was requested to contact the owner of the
property to see if the County could obtain a parcel for a new "super
site".
Being no further business, the meeting was recessed until November 15,
1994 at 7:30 P.M.
