A joint public hearing of the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors,
the Appomattox County Planning Commission and the Appomattox County
Board of Zoning Appeals was held on August 21, 1995 at 7:00 P.M. in the
Courthouse, Courthouse Square, North Court Street, Appomattox, Virginia.

Mr. Paulette opened the public hearing at 7:00 P.M., explained the
purpose of the hearing was to receive comments on several zoning
petitions and explained the procedure for addressing comments.
Mr. Paulette then asked Bill Caldwell, Clerk to the Planning
Commission, to read the first petition.
Mr. Caldwell read Petition #95-258 - James R. Guill - Rezone property
from A-1 to R-1. Property is located on Route 719 between Route 1034
and Route 641.
