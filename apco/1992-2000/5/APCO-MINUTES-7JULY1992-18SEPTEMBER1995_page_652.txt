A joint public hearing of the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors
and the Appomattox County Planning Commission was held on June 19,
1995, at 7:00 P.M. in the School Board conference room, North Court
Street, Appomattox, Virginia.

Philip E. Paulette, Chairman - Courthouse District (late)

Mr. Moore called the public hearing to order and stated the purpose of
the hearing was to receive comments on several zoning petitions. Mr.
Moore then stated the procedure for addressing the Board.
Mr. Moore asked Mrs. Ferguson to read the first petition.
Mr. Paulette arrived.
Mrs. Ferguson read petition 95-254 of John H. Ranson for a Conditional
Use Permit to operate restaurant in Evergreen General Store. Property
is zoned V-1 and is located on Route 630 between Route 633 and Route
