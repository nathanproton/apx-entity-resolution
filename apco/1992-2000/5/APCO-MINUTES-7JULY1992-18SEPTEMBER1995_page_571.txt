Mr. Paulette called the meeting to order and stated the purpose of the
meeting was to receive public comment on the proposed "Election of
School Board Members" ordinance.
Mr. Paulette then asked Mr. Lawson, County Attorney, to give a brief
overview of the ordinance.
Mr. Lawson reviewed the background of elected school boards and
explained that this ordinance is a follow-up to the referendum on
electing school board members.
The proposed ordinance is as follows:
ELECTION OF SCHOOL BOARD MEMBERS
ORDINANCE
ARTICLE 1
TITLE AND PURPOSE
Section 1. Title
This ordinance shall hereafter be known, cited and referred to as
the "Election of School Board Members Ordinance of Appomattox County,
Virginia."
Section 2. Purpose
This ordinance is adopted for the following purpose:
al To provide for the procedure for the lawful election of
school board members of Appomattox County, Virginia.
2. To provide for the composition and term limits of the school
board members of Appomattox County, Virginia.
3. To provide for the manner in which vacant seats on the school
board of Appomattox County, Virginia, are to be filled following the
initial school board elections.
ARTICLE 2
JURISDICTION AND AUTHORITY
Section 1. Jurisdiction
The Election of School Board Members Ordinance of Appomattox
County, Virginia, shall apply to the election of school board members
in Appomattox County, Virginia.
Section 2. Authority
The Board of Supervisors has authority to adopt this ordinance
pursuant to a referendum held on November 3, 1992, pursuant to Section
22.1-57.2 of the Code of Virginia 1950, as amended; and, after
publication of the intent to pass an ordinance entitled ELECTION OF
