Mrs. Ferguson requested the Board of Supervisors adopt a policy on
hanging portraits in County owned buildings.
After discussion, Mrs. Beasley made a motion seconded by Mr. Paulette
to adopt the following policy:
POLICY ON PLACEMENT OF PORTRAITS
The Appomattox County Board of Supervisors, recognizing the
importance of preserving our County history, has formed the following
policy in reference to portraits donated to the County.
Portraits of local Judges will be displayed in the Circuit
Courtroom.
Local, Federal and State Representatives portraits will be
displayed in the Circuit Courtroom.
Local, Constitutional Officers portraits will be displayed in
their respective offices.
Vote: Mr. Torrence, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr.
Moore, aye; and Mr. Paulette, aye.
