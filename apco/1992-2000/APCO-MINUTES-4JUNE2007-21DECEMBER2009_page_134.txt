9A. Purchase of Loader for Landfill:
Request for Bids for a new compact rubber tire articulated loader were
advertised on September 23 and September 30”, 2007. The deadline for
submitting these bids was October 12, 2007 at 2:00 p.m.
On October 12, 2007, the bids were opened and read aloud at the Office of the
County Administrator, located on Morton Lane, Appomattox, Va. Only one (1)
bid was received: Carter Machinery Company, Inc., P. O. Box 3096, Salem, Va.
24153 in the amount of $48, 986.00.
After some discussion, Mr. Craft made a motion, seconded by Mr. Armbrust to accept
the bid of $48,986.00 from Carter Machinery Company, Inc. for the purchase of a
compact rubber tire articulated loader for use at the Appomattox County Landfill.
ROLL CALL VOTE: Mr. Armbrust, aye; Mr. Conrad, aye; Mr. Craft, aye; Mr. Moore, aye;
and Mr. Carter, aye. (5 aye votes)
