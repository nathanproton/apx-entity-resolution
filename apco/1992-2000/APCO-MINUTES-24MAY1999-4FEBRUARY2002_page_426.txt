Mr. Moore called the Public Hearing to order and stated the purpose of the public hearing was to
hear comments on three (3) petitions requesting changes to the Appomattox County Zoning
Ordinance and also to present an Amendment to the Zoning Ordinance.
Mr. Moore asked Mr. Stidham to read the first petition.
00-445 - Samuel Dickerson - Variance to Section 170-81(A) of the Appomattox County Zoning
Ordinance, provision restricting accessory buildings to rear yard in R-1 district. Property is located
on Rt. 1048 (Walton Drive), is Tax Map No. 75A(3)5E and is zoned R-1.
Mr. Moore called for comments for or against this petition.
Hearing none, Mr. Moore asked Mr. Stidham to read the next petition.
00-446 - Joel Dawson - Conditional Use Permit to place second dwelling on lot in A-1 district per
Section 170-23 of the Appomattox County Zoning Ordinance. Property is located on Route 669
between Route 608 and Route 608, Tax Map No. 47(A)57A, and is zoned A-1.
Mr. Moore called for comments for or against this petition.
Hearing none, Mr. Moore asked Mr. Stidham to read the next petition.
00-447 - Charlene Wheeler - Variance to Section 170-80 of the Appomattox County Zoning
Ordinance pertaining to side yard setback requirements. Property is located on Route 675 between
Route 700 and Route 644 and is part of Tax Map No. 99(1)7, and is zoned A-1.
Mr. Moore Called for comments for or against this petition.
Hearing none, Mr. Moore asked Mr. Stidham to read the next petition.
Zoning Ordinance Amendment - Amend Table 2 of Section 170-80, Maximum Lot Coverage and
Height Restrictions/Minimum Yard Requirements, to clarify applicability of front yard setback to
private rights-of-way.
Mr. Moore asked for comments for or against this petition.
Hearing none and there being no further petitions, Mr. Moore declared the Public Hearing closed at
7:02 P.M. Om, Alen
