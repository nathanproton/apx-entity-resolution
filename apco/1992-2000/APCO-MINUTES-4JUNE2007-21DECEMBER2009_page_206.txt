Virginia Association of Counties Group Self Insurance Risk Pool” which creates a group

joining the Group, and we acknowledge we have received a copy of the pertinent Plan
and supporting documents.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that Aileen Ferguson is authorized to execute the
member agreement to join the Virginia Association of Counties Group Self Insurance
Risk Pool and to act on behalf of Appomattox County in any other matter relative to the
Group.
