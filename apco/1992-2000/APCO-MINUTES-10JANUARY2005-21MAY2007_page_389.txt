On Tuesday, May 30, 2006 the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors and the
Appomattox Town Council held a joint meeting at 6:00 p.m. at the Appomattox County
Community Center located on Community Lane in Appomattox, Va.

Carter asked Mr. Spiggle to give the invocation, after which everyone was asked to join
in the Pledge of Allegiance to the American Flag.
