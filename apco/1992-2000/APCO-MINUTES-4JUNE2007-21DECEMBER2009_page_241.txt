Chairman Craft called the Public Hearing to order at 6:15 p.m.
Chairman Craft explained that the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors will be
holding a public hearing on the proposed FY2009 budget. Chairman Craft explained the
procedure for addressing comments to the Board during this public hearing.
