A recessed meeting of the Board of Supervisors was held Tuesday, August
3, 1993, following the Public Hearing, in the Courthouse, Courthouse
Square, North Court Street, Appomattox, VA
\
Present:
W. E. Jamerson, Courthouse District
Nina B. Beasley, Falling River District
Frank H. Carwile, Wreck Island District
Dennis W. Torrence, Appomattox River District
Also present: Aileen T. Ferguson, Interim County Administrator
Absent:
Russell H. Moore, Piney Mountain District
Mr. Jamerson, presiding, called the meeting to order at 7:50 P.M.
Mr. Torrence informed the Board that he had received a bid to remove
the pecan tree located behind the School Board Office. After
discussion Mr. Carwile volunteered to seek another bid and bring to the
Board at a later date for consideration.
Mrs. Ferguson brought a request to the Board from the Jail to
appropriate $360.00 to the Inmate Telephone Fund to pay for cable T.V.
for the inmates. This money is reimbursed to the County by the
telephone company. Mr. Torrence made a motion seconded by Mr. Carwile
to appropriate $360.00 to the Inmate Telephone Fund to pay for cable
T.V.for the Jail. Vote: Mr. Jamerson, aye; Mrs. Beasley, nay; Mr.
Carwile, aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.
Being no further business Mr. Jamerson with agreement of the other
members, recessed the meeting until Monday, nous 1993.
