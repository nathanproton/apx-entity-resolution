Mr. Roark came forward and explained that the Board would be hearing petition # 09-001 aconditional use permit for Gleaning for the World requesting an off premise sign which has beenon the property for approximately five years. Mr. Roark explained that this petition is being
heard due to VDOT requiring a permit from this organization and to obtain the proper permit
from VDOT, they must go through our office and have approved as a conditional use.
Mr. Roark stated that he had requested Gleaning for the World to cover this sign due to it being
set in concrete which would make it difficult and expensive to take down while waiting for the
Board to make a decision on this petition.
Mr. Roark explained that the Planning Commission has recommended approval of this petition
with one condition and that one condition being the sign would be removed if Gleaning for the
World was to relocate.
Chairman Conrad explained the procedure for addressing the Board during the public hearing.
Chairman Conrad called for comments from citizens that would like to speak in favor or against
this petition.
Rev. Ron Davidson came forward and stated that he was the founder of Gleaning for the World
and stated that he was not aware that he needed a permit for this sign and apologized for not
going through the proper channels for this sign,
Rev. Davidson gave a brief presentation on what Gleaning for the World has accomplished and
done for our fellow man all over the world.
Rev. Davidson stated that the sign is very important to help the truckers to find their
organization.
Again, Mr. Davidson apologized for not following the proper procedure for their sign and asked
the board to consider approving their request.
Chairman Conrad called for other comments, hearing none, the Public Hearing closed at 7:10
