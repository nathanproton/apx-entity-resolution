VIRGINIA: IN THE CIRCUIT COURT FOR APPOMATTOX COUNTY
RE: THE COUNTY OF APPOMATTOX
ORDER
This day came the County of Appomattox, Virginia by and through the County
Attorney, upon its petition that the Circuit Court of Appomattox County order a special
election.
And it appearing to the court that :
1, That Barbara R. Williams is the duly elected Clerk of the Court for the
Circuit Court of Appomattox County.
That the aforesaid Barbara R. Williams has retired as Clerk of the Circuit
Court of Appomattox County effective the 31* day of August 2009.
That the Board of Supervisors, pursuant to Sec. 24.2-228.1 of the Code
of Virginia must petition the Circuit Court of Appomattox County for the
issuance of a writ of election to fill the aforesaid vacancy.
That the Board of Supervisors of Appomattox County at its meeting duly
held on the 8" day of September, 2009 passed a resolution authorizing
the County attorney of Appomattox County to submit a petition to the
Circuit Court of Appomattox County.
That Sec. 24.2-228.1A Code of Virginia provides that “ .. . a vacancy in
any elected constitutional office, . . . , shall be filled by special election”;
and that the date shall be “determined pursuant to Sec. 24.2-682." and
