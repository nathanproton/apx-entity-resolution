Chairman Conrad presented Administrative Item #6- By-Laws for the Local Emergency
Planning Committee of Appomattox County and explained that we are required by the State to
organize a Local Emergency Planning Committee (LEPC) to ensure that all acts required by
SARA Title III are complied with and also, the committee will, serve in an advisory capacity to
Appomattox County Emergency Management personnel in planning for emergencies. Chairman
Conrad called for discussion.
Ms. Ferguson stated that the County has had this committee and it has been a long standing
committee, however, it is has not been functional, Ms. Ferguson stated we will need specific
people on this committee.
Mr. Craft made a motion to approve the by-laws for the Local Emergency Planning Committee
of Appomattox County. Mr. Carter seconded Mr. Craft’s motion. Hearing no discussion,
Chairman Conrad called for a vote: Mr. Carter, aye; Mr. Conrad, aye; Mr. Craft, aye; Mr. Moore,
aye; Mr. Tanner, aye.
