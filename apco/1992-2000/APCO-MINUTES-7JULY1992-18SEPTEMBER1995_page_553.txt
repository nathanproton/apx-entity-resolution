Mrs. Ferguson next presented a request from Spout Spring Ruritan Club
that in the event inclement weather would cause them to be unable to
hold Bingo on Friday night, would it be permissible to hold the game on
Saturday night?
After discussion, and by consensus of the Board, it was agreed that
Spout Spring Ruritan Club could hold Bingo games on Saturday night, if
Friday night game was cancelled due to weather conditions.
