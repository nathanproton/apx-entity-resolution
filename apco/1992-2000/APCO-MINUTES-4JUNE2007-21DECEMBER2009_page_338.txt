Mr. Andrew Mundy came forward and stated his address as 202 Chandler Lane. Mr. Mundy
stated that he is in favor of this petition because his property was bought so that he and his
brother could get farming experience and he wanted it to stay that way.
Chairman Craft next called for citizens who wished to speak against this petition.
Ms. Paige Lovings came forward and gave her address as 377 Chandlers Lane. Ms. Lovings
stated that she was not really against this petition, but she did have some questions about the
changes and would like to get them answered.
Mr. Neal Chandler came forward and gave his address as 185 Chandlers Lane. Mr. Chandler
stated that it was already sheep and goats on these properties before the zoning has been changed
and he feels it is going to bring the value of his home down.
Chairman Craft called for other citizens who would like to speak against this petition. Hearing
no other comments, Chairman Craft explained that the Planning Commission would now meet in
the next room and the meeting will be open to anyone who wished to attend.
Chairman Craft closed the public hearing at 7:10 p.m.
