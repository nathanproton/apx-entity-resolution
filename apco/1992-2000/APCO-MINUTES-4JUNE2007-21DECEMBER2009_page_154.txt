At 6:56 p.m. Mr. Carter called the joint meeting to order and Mr. Dan Cousin, Chairman
of the Extension Leadership Council introduced Mr. Bruce Jones, Appomattox County
Agriculture Extension Agent.
Mr. Jones then gave a presentation on the Value Added Beef Initiative Pro gram.
Appomattox County along with three other counties have received money from the
Tobacco Commission to improve beef cattle stock in the county, by genetic
improvement, improved handling facilities and weaning lots.
Mr. Jones also discussed freeze branding as an alternative to ear tags and also, sheep as a
lower investment animal.
The Pesticide Disposal Program collected 8,000 pounds of pesticides from Appomattox,
Charlotte and Campbell Counties. Department of Agriculture will implement a container
recycling program if asked.
Drought concerns were discussed.
Mrs. Wanda Evans —Adult- EFNEP Program, discussed the super pantry which works
with about 200 families a year. The main focus of this program is on exercise as well as
nutrition.
