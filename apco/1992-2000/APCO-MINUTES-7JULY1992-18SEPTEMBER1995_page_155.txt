again and discussed. Mr. Carwile stated he had reviewed the

Because the evening was getting late, Mr. Jamerson reminded the Board
of the Public Hearing scheduled for tomorrow night April 6, 1993 at
7:30 P.M. and asked the Board if they would consider continuing the
budget work session after the Public Hearing tomorrow night. The Board
agreed.
