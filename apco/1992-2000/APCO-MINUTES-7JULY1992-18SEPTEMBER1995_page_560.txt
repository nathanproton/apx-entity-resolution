Mayor Spiggle, presiding, called the meeting to order and gave the
invocation.
Mr. Spiggle noted the purpose of the meeting was to review the plans
for a proposed industrial site, and began by reviewing the Utilities
Improvements report provided by Bernard C. Proctor, P. E. (Report on
file in February 1995 Board File.)
A lengthy review of the utilities report was provided by Mayor Spiggle
and Vice Mayor Bass with final conclusion being that considerable
upgrades would be required in order to provide utilities to an
industrial site.
Discussion followed as to the employment problems in the County and
what type business it would benefit the County to recruit.
