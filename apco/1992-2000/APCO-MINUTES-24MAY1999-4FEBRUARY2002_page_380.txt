Mr. Moore then asked Mr. Phillip Jones to appear before the Board.
Mr. Jones stated he was there on behalf of the resident of Country Haven Estates Sub-Division.
Mr. Jones thanked the Board for their help on the first sections of the road that have been completed.
There are approximately 1.6 miles left on the main road to complete. At the present time there is a
proposal to complete .8 of a mile with money on hand for this portion. This leaves an additional .6
of a mile to complete. They would like to have this road completed if at all possible.
There was some discussion on this matter.
Mr. Moore thanked Mr. Jones for his appearance.
