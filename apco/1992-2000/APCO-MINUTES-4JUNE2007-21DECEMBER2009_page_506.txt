Mr. Roark explained that he needed direction from the Board as far as staff is concerned as to
whether the Board is heading in the direction of forming a PSA or forming a Utility Department
under the supervision of the County Administrator. Mr. Roark stated that this is a decision that
needs to be made, so that we can organize and shift some personnel around, as this project is
dominating most of his time.
Mr. Carter stated that we still have not heard from the Town in response to our letter and they
may want to take part which would make us go to a PSA.
Ms. Ferguson stated that we must have a course of action as we cannot wait on a reply from the
Town.
There was in-depth discussion on creating a PSA verses a County Utility Department.
Mr. Tanner made a motion to enter into the creation and formalization of a PSA. Mr. Carter
seconded Mr. Tanner’s motion. Chairman Conrad called f or discussion, hearing none,
Chairman Conrad called for a vote: Mr. Carter, aye; Mr. Craft, aye; Mr. Conrad, aye; Mr. Moore,
aye; Mr. Tanner, aye.
Mr. Carter made a motion to adjourn the meeting at 8:30 P.M. Mr. Craft seconded Mr. Carter’s
motion. Hearing no discussion, Chairman Conrad called for a vote: Mr. Carter, aye; Mr. Craft,
aye; Mr. Conrad, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Tanner, aye.
The meeting adjourned at 8:30 p.m.
