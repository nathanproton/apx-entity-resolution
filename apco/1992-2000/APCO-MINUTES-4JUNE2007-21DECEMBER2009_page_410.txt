MEMORANDUM OF AGREEMENT
between the
COUNTY OF APPOMATTOX
and the
VIRGINIA’S REGION 2000 LOCAL GOVERNMENT COUNCIL
for
ADMINISTRATION OF THE APPOMATTOX COUNTY
TRANSPORTATION ENHANCEMENT GRANT
FOR THE DEVELOPMENT OF THE
APPOMATTOX HERITAGE AND RECREATIONAL TRAIL
I. PURPOSE
The County of Appomattox (herein referred to as the “County”) and the Virginia’s Region 2000
Local Government Council (herein referred to as the “Local Government Council”) are entering
into this Memorandum of Agreement (herein referred to as “Agreement” for the purpose of
administering the Virginia Transportation Enhancement Grant awarded to the County in June 2008
by way of signed contract from the Virginia Department of Transportation (VDOT). The
Enhancement Grant Funds are for the design, engineering, and bid document development for the
eventual construction of the Appomattox Heritage and Recreational Trail, a multiuse trail.
