Mr. Jamerson next recognized Mr. T. W. Lawson, County Attorney. Mr.
Lawson asked the second half of his FY93 County Attorney’s fee
($6,000.00) be paid. Mr. Carwile made a motion seconded by Mrs.
Beasley to pay Mr. Lawson $6,000.00 for attorney fee from January 1,
1993 to June 30, 1993. Vote: Mr. Jamersion, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye;
Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.
Mr. Lawson also asked permission to attend a conference. Mr. Torrence
made a motion seconded by Mrs. Beasley to pay expenses estimated at
$200.00 for Mr. Lawson to attend the Commonwealth Attorneys Association
training session in Williamsburg during the last week of March. Vote:
Mr. Jamerson, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye;
Mr. Torrence, aye.
And finally, Mr. Lawson asked the County to pay for two rape tests
needed to collect evidence for a court case. After discussion, Mr.
Carwile made a motion seconded by Mr. Moore to pay $198.25 to Lynchburg
General Hospital and Lynchburg Emergency Physicians for testing for a
rape case. Vote: Mr. Jamerson, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile,
aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.

Vote: Mr. Jamerson, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr.
Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.
