6. Piedmont Geriatric Association: The Piedmont Geriatric Association is requesting that the
Board adopt a resolution of support to the Virginia General Assembly for continued operation of the
Piedmont Geriatric Hospital in Nottoway County. They are also requesting financial support for
lobbying efforts to keep this facility open.
There was some discussion regarding what previous Boards have done regarding this facility. The
Board decided to adopt the resolution of support but did not offer any financial support at this time.
