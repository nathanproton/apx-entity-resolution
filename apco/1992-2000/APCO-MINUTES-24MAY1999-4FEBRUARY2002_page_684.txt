Section 2.2-3711.A-1 - Personnel Matters. VOTE: Mr. Carter, aye; Mr. Hancock, aye; Mr. Carwile,
aye; Mr. Moore, aye; and Mr. Kendrick, aye.

Kendrick, aye.
