with the Appomattox County School Board at the School Board Administration Building, Court
Street, Appomattox, Va.

At 6:03 p.m. Mr. Carter and Mr. Pickett opened their respective meetings.
The purpose of the meeting was to meet with representatives of HRH, Cindy Bartolacci and
Maria Gravely, regarding the health insurance coverage for both the School employees and the
County employees. Ms. Bartolacci and Ms. Gravely gave a presentation on both the medical and
dental benefits.
There were two (2)changes to the prescription drug designs: 1) required mandatory generic; and
2) mail order for 90 day supply (high utilization for prescriptions).
