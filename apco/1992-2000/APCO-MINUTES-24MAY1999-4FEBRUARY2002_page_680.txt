Mr. Carter next called for discussion on Consent Agenda #8 - Registrar’s Request:
The Code of Virginia allows the Board to set salaries for the assistant registrar position. The
Registrar, is requesting permission to grant a raise retroactively to December for her current
assistant registrar and is also requesting the authority to grant future raises as the budget allows.
Mr. Carwile stated that he was aware that the Code of Virginia allows for one assistant.
However, our registrar uses two, alternating the work days and they are being paid $49.00 per
day. Recent changes have created an increased workload. He has no objections to granting the
raise retroactive to December but he has a problem with giving the authority to grant raises in the
future.
Mr. Carwile made a motion seconded by Mr. Kendrick to grant raise for assistant registrar
position, retroactive to December 1, 2000 but not granting permission for registrar to grant pay
increases in the future but to continue with the Code. VOTE: Mr. Carter, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye;
Mr. Moore, aye; and Mr. Kendrick, aye.
