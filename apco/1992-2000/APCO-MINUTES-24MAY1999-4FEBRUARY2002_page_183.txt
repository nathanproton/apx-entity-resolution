WHEREAS, bona fide but ineffectual attempts have been made by the Board of

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the County Attorney is instructed to file a
condemnation petition against Kathleen G. Stevens, et als, in accordance with the
provisions of Article of Chapter 7 of Title 15.2 of the Code of Virginia, 1950 as amended,
and Title 25 of the Code of Virginia, 1950 as amended, to acquire the fee simple title to
.06 acre and a 10 foot temporary construction easement across the property of Kathleen
G. Stevens, et als beginning 751.90 feet from its intersection with State Route 1029 and
continuing in a northerly direction 479.39 feet along the norther portion Route 26, as
shown on a certain plat of survey dated May 20, 1999 by John R. Bradner, L.S., entitled
“Industrial Development Authority of Appomattox County”.
That in the opinion of the Board of Supervisors, a public necessity exists for the
acquisition of said easement.
That in order to provide the total estimated necessary funds to compensate the owners of
the property for the easement to be acquired across the same, and for any damages to the
residue, the sum of $1,000.00 has been heretofore appropriated and shall be paid into the
Court as compensation and damages, if any, to the property and stating that the date of
entry shall be February 2, 2000.
