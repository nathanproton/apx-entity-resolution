A regular meeting of the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors was held on
November 15, 1999 at 6:11 P.M. in the Courthouse, Courthouse Square, North Court
Street, Appomattox, Virginia.
Present:
Garet K. Bosiger — Chairman — Falling River District
Samuel E. Carter — Appomattox Courthouse District
Frank H. Carwile — Wreck Island District
Russell H. Moore — Piney Mountain District
Dennis W. Torrence — Appomattox River District
Also Present:
Aileen T. Ferguson — County Administrator
Andy Carroll — Assistant County Administrator
Thomas W. Lawson — County Attorney
Mr. Bosiger called the meeting to order and asked Mr. Carter to deliver the invocation.
Mr. Bosiger then called for additions or corrections to the minutes.
Mr. Carter made a motion seconded by Mr. Torrence to approve the minutes of the
regular meeting of October 18, 1999, and a work session of October 25, 1999. Vote: Mr.
Bosiger, aye; Mr. Carter, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.
Mr. Bosiger then called for approval of the bills for November 15, 1999.
Mr. Carter asked a question concerning the inmate telephone fund.
Mr. Torrence made a motion seconded by Mr. Carter to approve the bills for November
15, 1999. Vote: Mr. Bosiger, aye; Mr. Carter, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye, Mr. Moore, aye;
Mr. Torrence, aye.
Mr. Bosiger then called on Mr. Steve Jacobs of Robinson Farmer Cox to appear before
the Board and recessed the meeting at 6:20 P.M. to allow Mr. Jacobs to set-up for his
presentation.
Mr. Bosiger reconvened the meeting at 6:23 P.M. and explained that Mr. Jacobs would
present the five year financial forecast for the County.
Mr. Jacobs gave a very informative presentation concerning the financial outlook for the
County for the next five years. (Copy of Documents on file in the County
Administrator’s Office.)
After Mr. Jacob’s presentation he answered questions of the members of the Board.
