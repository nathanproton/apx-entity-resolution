18. Administrator’s Update:
Mr. Carter commented on Central Purchasing. He feels that the County can benefit from a
central purchasing system and he feels this needs to be looked into more extensively for the County,
including Constitutional Officers.
Also some discussion regarding State Contracts, specific Bid items, etc.
Discussion followed regarding a study be done on
- what and how we buy,
- what would be the cost of centralized purchasing,
- what would be the cost of employing a Purchasing Agent
The Board by consensus instructed Mr. Carroll to perform further study on this issue and report the
findings at a future meeting.
