A regular meeting of the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors was held on April 3, 2000 at
6:00 P.M. in the Appomattox County Courthouse, Courthouse Square, North Court Street,
Appomattox, Virginia.
Present:
Russell H. Moore, Chairman - Piney Mountain District
Jerry Kendrick - Appomattox River District
Jesse Hancock - Falling River District
Samuel Carter - Courthouse District
Frank H. Carwile - Wreck Island District
Also Present:
Aileen T. Ferguson, County Administrator
Andy Carroll - Assistant County Administrator
Darrel Puckett - County Attorney
Mr. Moore called the meeting to order at 6:48 P.M. after the public hearing and delivered the
invocation.
Mr. Moore then called for additions or corrections to the minutes.
Mr. Carter noted several corrections to the minutes of February 23, 2000 and the minutes of February
28, 2000.
Mr. Carter then made a motion seconded by Mr. Kendrick to approve the minutes of the February
23, 2000 joint meeting and the March 6, 2000 regular meeting as corrected, the public hearings of
February 28, 2000 and March 20, 2000, and the joint public hearing of March 20, 2000. Vote: Mr.
Moore, Aye; Mr. Kendrick, aye; Mr. Hancock, aye; Mr. Carter, aye; and Mr. Carwile, aye.
Mr. Moore then called for approval of the bills for April 3, 2000.
Mr. Carter made a motion seconded by Mr. Kendrick to approve the bills for April 3, 2000. Vote:
Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Kendrick, aye; Mr. Hancock, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; and Mr. Carter, aye.
Mr. Moore then explained that a closed session was necessary under section 2.1-344(A)(7) legal and
section 2.1-344(A)(1) personnel matters.
At 6:48 P.M. Mr. Carwile made a motion seconded by Mr. Carter to enter into closed session to
discuss a legal matter (Section 2.1-344(A)(7) and a personnel matter (Section 2.1-344(A)(1). Vote:
Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Kendrick, aye; Mr. Hancock, aye; Mr. Carter, aye; and Mr. Carwile, aye.
