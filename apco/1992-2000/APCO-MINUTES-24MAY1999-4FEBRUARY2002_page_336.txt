VA.

Jerry Kendrick - Appomattox River District

Also Present: Aileen T. Ferguson - County Administrator

Mr. Moore called the meeting to order at 6:00 P.M. and asked Mr. Hancock to give the invocation.
Mr. Moore explained that an executive session was necessary under Code Section 2.1-344(A)7 -
legal issues and Code Section 2.1-344(A)1 - personnel matters.
At 6:05 p.m. Mr. Carter made a motion seconded by Mr. Kendrick to enter into executive session
to discuss a legal and/or personnel matter. VOTE: Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Kendrick, aye; Mr.
Hancock, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; and Mr. Carter, aye.
At 7:52 P.M. ona motion by Mr. Carter, seconded by Mr. Kendrick it was resolved the Board of
Supervisors return to regular session. VOTE: Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Kendrick, aye; Mr. Hancock, aye;
Mr. Carwile, aye; and Mr. Carter, aye.
