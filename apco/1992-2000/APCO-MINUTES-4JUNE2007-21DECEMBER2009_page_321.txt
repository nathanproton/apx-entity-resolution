William H. Craft, Chairman
On Monday, August 4, 2008 at 6:30 p.m. the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors
held its regular scheduled meeting at the Appomattox County Administration Building,
located at 153A Morton Lane, Appomattox, Virginia.
Present: William H. Craft - Appomattox River District
Samuel E. Carter — Courthouse District
Thomas H. Conrad — Falling River District
Russell H. Moore ~ Piney Mountain District
Gary W. Tanner — Wreck Island District
Also Present: Aileen T. Ferguson- County Administrator
John K. Spencer ~ Assistant County Administrator
J.G. Overstreet — County Attorney
Vicky Phelps — Administrative Assistant
Chairman Craft called the regular scheduled meeting to order at 6:30 p.m. and called on
Mr. Tanner to deliver the invocation. Everyone joined in the Pledge of Allegiance to the
American Flag.
Chairman Craft welcomed everyone to the meeting and then called for setting of the
agenda.
With no changes to the agenda, Chairman Craft proceeded to the Public Comment
Session, hearing none; Chairman Craft next presented Administrative Item #1- VDOT
Resolution and called for discussion.
At the regular meeting of the Board of Supervisors of Appomattox County, Virginia held
on the 4" day of August 2008, it was duly moved and seconded that the following
Resolution be adopted:
NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, that the Virginia Department of
Transportation is hereby requested to add the street description below and on the attached
additions Form AM-4.3 to the Appomattox County Secondary Road System for
maintenance. The following subdivision road which has been constructed by the sub
divider as a Class “A” subdivision in accordance with the requirements of Section 33.1-
229, of the Code of Virginia, 1952, and The Virginia Department of Transportation’s
Subdivision Street Requirements, 1996, TO WIT:
JONESFIELD SUBDIVISION, SECTION 1
