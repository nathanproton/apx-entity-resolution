Mr. Peterson next brought up the subject of the plans for the proposed
transfer site at Vera.

place the containers in this general area. Vote: Mr. Torrence, aye;
Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; and Mr. Paulette, aye.
Mr. Peterson will rework this proposal site drawing and bring back to
the Board for approval.
Mr. Torrence mentioned that David Nash wants the containers moved from
his property located on route 24. Mr. Torrence feels that research
should be done to locate a site somewhere near Town, possibly at the
ball park.

Compactor for the landfill.

Moore to purchase the 816B Compactor, and pay off the loan on the
trade-in from landfill bond account. Vote: Mr. Torrence, aye; Ms.
Beasley, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; and Mr. Paulette, aye.
