A recessed meeting and work session of the Appomattox County
Board of Supervisors was called to order at 7:00 P.M.
November 5, 1992 at the Courthouse, North Church Street,
Appomattox, Virginia.
Present:
W. E. Jamerson, Chairman, Courthouse District
Dennis Torrence, Vice Chairman, Appomattox River Dist.
Nina Beasley, Falling River District
Frank Carwile, Wreck Island District
Russell Moore, Piney Mt. District
Gordon L. Rice, County Administrator
Mr. Jamerson, presiding, called the meeting to order at 7:00
P.M. Mr. Jamerson stated the purpose of the meeting was to
review the Building Construction, Subdivision, and Taxation
Ordinances. The Board reviewed each ordinance considering
relevant State Code updates and recommendations from the
review committees, County Administrator, and County Attorney.
After discussion, Mr. Torrence made a motion seconded by Mr.
Carwile to appoint Mr. Gordon Rice as Appomattox County
Building Official. Vote: Mr. Jamerson, aye; Mrs. Beasley,
aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.
After discussion, Mr. Torrence made a motion seconded by Mrs.
Beasley to appoint the County Administrator as the
Subdivision Agent and Zoning Administrator. Vote: Mr.
Jamerson, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr.
Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.
