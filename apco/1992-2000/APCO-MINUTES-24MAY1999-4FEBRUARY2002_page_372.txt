BE IT RESOLVED, this Board does hereby accept the Board of Viewers report and will request
the Virginia Department of Transportation to add the following sections to the Secondary Road
System of Appomattox County when the necessary unrestricted fifty (50) feet of right of way is
obtained, required utility adjustments completed and funding is available.

acceptance under Section 33.1-72.1 Paragraph “C”of the Code of Virginia.
Chestnut Mountain Drive- Beginning at the end of maintenance of Route 750 and running to the

Campbell County Line for a distance of 1.09 miles.
(50/50) Estimated Cost - $200,000
6" Street - Beginning at its intersection with Route 750 and running 0.09 miles
to a dead end.
(50/50) Estimated Cost - $15,000
Ash Lane - Beginning at the intersection of Route 750 and running 0.15 miles to
a dead end.
(50/50) Estimated Cost - $23,000
Pine Crest Lane - Beginning at the intersection of Chestnut Mountain Drive and

(50/50) Estimated Cost - $60,000
