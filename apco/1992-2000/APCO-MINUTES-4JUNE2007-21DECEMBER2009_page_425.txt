Mrs. Nina Beasley came before the Board and stated her address as 4905 Pumping Station Road,
Appomattox, Virginia 24522.
Ms. Beasley stated that she was here to speak in support of the Board of Supervisors proposal to
hire a full time Economic Development Director. Ms. Beasley stated that with the recent layoff
at Thomasville Furniture, now is the time more than ever to hire this person. Ms. Beasley stated
that Appomattox County needs to enhance their tax base and this cannot be done if we are losing
businesses. Ms. Beasley stated that there are three things that go hand in hand which is, number
one, the county needs to attract businesses for the industrial park, second we need a reliable
water source to benefit citizens, business and industry and third the County needs a full time
Economic Development Director to enhance the tax base by attracting new business and
industry. Ms. Beasley stated that this ultimately will benefit every citizen in Appomattox County
by lowering taxes. Ms. Beasley stated that the time for decisive action is now. Ms. Beasley
thanked the Board for allowing her to speak.
Chairman Conrad thanked Ms. Beasley and called for other public comments.
