On May 8, 2000 at 6:00 P.M. the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors held a Budget WorkShop at the Appomattox County Courthouse, Courthouse Square, Court Street, Appomattox, Va.The purpose of the meeting was to discuss the upcoming FY2001 Budget.
Present: Russell H. Moore, Chairman - Piney Mountain District
Jerry Kendrick - Appomattox River District
Samuel Carter - Courthouse District
Absent: Frank H. Carwile - Wreck Island District
Jesse Hancock - Falling River District
Also Present: Aileen T. Ferguson - County Administrator
Mrs. Ferguson presented each Board Member with a printout of the increases in the FY2001 Budgetbroken down by department. She also explained each increase and answered any questions that theBoard had regarding this document.
