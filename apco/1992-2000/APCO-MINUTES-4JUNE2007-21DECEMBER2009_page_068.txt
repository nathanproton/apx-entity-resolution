5B. Children Play Signs: Mr. Moore has been approached by Randy Seay
concerning placing a “WATCH FOR CHILDREN” sign on Route 686 “Bear Branch
Road”. Mr. Moore has contacted VDOT and was informed that they needed a
resolution from the Board asking that VDOT install these signs.
Mr. Armbrust made a motion, seconded by Mr, Conrad, to adopt the following resolution
authorizing VDOT to install a “WATCH FOR CHILDREN” sign on Route 686 - Bear Brach
Road.
WHEREAS, the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors acknowledges the need to provide a
safe environment for our children, and
WHEREAS, Mr. Randy Seay has approached the Board of Supervisors with concern for the need
for WATCH FOR CHILDREN signs on Route 686; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Seay has agreed to bear the cost for the signs and installation,
NOW THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED, that the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors is
requesting Virginia Department of Transportation to pursue the installation of WATCH FOR
CHILDREN signs on secondary Route 686.
VOTE: Mr. Armbrust, aye; Mr. Conrad, aye; Mr. Craft, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; and Mr.
Carter, aye. (5 aye votes)
