Mr. Carwile made a motion seconded by Mr. Moore to appropriate
$60,000.00 in the FY94 budget to purchase Jamerson property at the
intersection of Route 727 and Evergreen Avenue. Vote: Mr. Jamerson,
abstain; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr.
Torrence, aye.
Mr. Jamerson next brought up the surface water testing at the
landfill. After discussion, Mr. Carwile made a motion seconded by Mr.
Moore to add $8,200.00 to the landfill budget to cover the cost of
surface water testing. Vote: Mr. Jamerson, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye;
Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.
