On August 3, 2000 at 6:00 P.M., the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors held a meeting with
Davenport and Company, LLC of Richmond at the Appomattox County Courthouse, Courthouse
Square, Court Street, Appomattox, VA.
PRESENT: Russell H. Moore, Chairman - Piney Mountain District
Jerry Kendrick - Appomattox River District
Samuel Carter - Courthouse District
ABSENT: Frank H. Carwile - Wreck Island District
Jesse Hancock - Falling River District
ALSO PRESENT: Andy Carroll - Acting County Administrator
David Rose - Davenport and Company, LLC
At 6:02 p.m., Mr. Moore opened the meeting and explained that Mr. Carwile and Mr. Hancock
would not be in attendance.
Mr. Moore then turned the meeting over to Mr. Rose. Mr. Rose began his presentation regarding
the financial report.
Mr. Rose started his presentation by stating the objectives upon which their report was based.
= Prepare a Capital Plan that meets the County’s objectives and determines
the future fiscal stress on the County.
- Minimize the County’s Reliance on Debt.
- Strategically use Cash Available to assist in Funding Capital Projects.
- Provide a Capital Funding Strategy that minimizes or avoids future tax increases.
Mr. Rose explained the Historic and Projected Population Growth; Revenue Growth; Expenditure
Growth; and Assessed Value Growth of the County.
Mr. Rose then explained the funds available by decline in the existing debt service.
Mr. Rose then listed the Financing Goals of the County as he understood them:
= Meet County’s Timetable Making Funds Available as Needed.
- Minimize the Level of Debt Related Issuance in Terms of Amount and Duration.
- Provide the Lowest All-In-Cost of funds to Maximize Cash Flow.
