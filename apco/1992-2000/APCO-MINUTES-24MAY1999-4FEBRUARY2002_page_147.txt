Mr. Bosiger called the public hearing to order at 7:00 P.M. explained the purpose of the
hearing was to receive public comment on several zoning petitions and gave the
procedure for addressing comments to the Board.
Mr. Bosiger then asked Mr. Caldwell, Clerk to the Planning Commission to read the first
petition.
Mr. Caldwell read petition number 99-403 of Lester H . Pannell to conditional zone
property from V-1 to B-1 with conditional use permit for Automotive Repair Garage.
Property is Tax Map No. 87-A-91 located on Route 604 between Route 679 and Route
645.
Mr. Bosiger called for comments on this petition.
Mr. Lester H. Pannell came forward and gave his address as Route 1 Box 207,
Appomattox, Virginia. Mr. Pannell began that his reason for this request was so that he
could operate an automotive repair shop on a part-time basis only. Mr. Pannell stated
that he just wanted to be legal.
Mr. Bosiger called for other comments on this petition.
