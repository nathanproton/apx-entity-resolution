At 7:55 P.M., the IDA concluded its presentation. Mr. Slagle then asked for questions
and comments from the public regarding the IDA’s presentation.
Several citizens took this opportunity to ask questions concerning the proposed industrial
park. Other citizens voiced their opinions pro and con regarding the proposed industrial
park.
At 9:45 P.M., Mr. Slagle thanked the public for its comments and interest and then made
some closing remarks before turning the meeting over to Mr. Bosiger.
Mr. Bosiger expressed appreciation for everyone coming to the meeting. Mr. Bosiger
then thanked the IDA and their guests for the presentation. Mr. Bosiger stated that the
Board would vote on July 20, 1998 regarding the industrial park.
At 9:50 P.M., Mr. Bosiger declared the public comment session adjourned.
Garet K. Bosiger, Chaitman
