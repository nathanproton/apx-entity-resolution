Section 21.7.Entities of County EMS/Rescue Division responsible to the
a)
b)
Department of Public Safety.
Each of the organizations and groups mentioned in subsection (b) and (c)
of 21.6 shall coordinate their operations and activities with the
Appomattox County Department of Public Safety and will carry out their
assigned tasks to the best of their ability.
Each EMS/rescue organization mentioned in 21.6(b) shall appoint a
captain who shall be responsible for the overall direction and control of
EMS/rescue service activities within his organization’s respective primary
response area. Additionally, the captain shall insure that his organization
complies with all of the provisions of applicable laws, ordinances, and
standard operation procedures and shall coordinate his organization’s
operations and activities with the Director of Public Safety or his designee.

All officially recognized members of the organizations and groups mentioned
above shall perform their respective duties, as outlined in standard operating
procedures and applicable laws and ordinances, to the best of their ability.
All officially recognized members of the organizations and groups mentioned
