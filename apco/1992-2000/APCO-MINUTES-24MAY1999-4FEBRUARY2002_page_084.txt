Mr. Bosiger then asked Mr. Earl Dickerson of Hurt & Proffitt if he would present his
scenarios concerning the present school site.
Mr. Dickerson began by first presenting guidelines from the Department of Education for
an elementary school site. Mr. Dickerson continued that an 800-student school would
require 12 acres of land to meet the minimum site requirement. Mr. Dickerson then
presented five different scenarios for improvements to the present site in order to meet
State requirements. Mr. Dickerson provided cost estimates for each scenario.
Questions and answers followed.
Comments were taken from all present concerning their individual views with the
information provided.
After discussion and by consensus of the Board it was agreed that the Board would tour
the Middle and Elementary Schools on September 3, 1999 beginning at 1:00 P.M. at the
Middle School.
At 9:00 P.M. Mr. Moore made a motion seconded by Mr. Carter to adjourn until August
30, 1999 at 6:00 P.M. in the Courthouse. Vote: Mr. Bosiger, aye; Mr. Carter, aye; Mr.
Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.
