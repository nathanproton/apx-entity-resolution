Mr. Torrence called the meeting to order at 7:30 P.M. and introduced
Mr. Robert Huff and Mr. Steve Jacobs of Robinson, Farmer, Cox. Mr.
Torrence then asked Mr. Huff to present the draft of the five year
capital improvement program for the County.
Mr. Huff began by giving their credentials and explaining the
background of this report.
Mr. Huff continued by discussing the nature of the project and
explained that this project is very much like a capitol improvements
plan that most localities in Virginia have done, to show how special
capitol projects will effect their budgets in the future.
Mr. Huff then asked Mr. Jacobs to hand out the charts and explained
that he had added a 10% operating cost factor into the plan to cover a
fiscal fund balance for the County. Mr. Huff explained that historical]
data on expenditure and revenues, proposed projects, etc. was how they
arrived at their projections for the future.
