At 6:00 p.m. Mr. Carter opened the Public Hearing, welcomed everyone in attendance
and stated the purpose of this hearing was to received comments on the Proposed
VDOT Secondary Six-Year Transportation Plan for the fiscal years 2007/08 through
2012/13 for Appomattox County.
Mr. Carter then turned the meeting over to Mr. Austin.
Mr. Austin then introduced Mr. Harvey and Mr. Thomas.
Mr. Austin explained the purpose of the Road Hearing. Each year they project some
allocations over a six-year period. The Board and VDOT then tries to determine the
priority of which roads need to be worked on.
