Mr. Sears read petition number 00-414 of Raymond Noble for a variance to paragraph 170-80-A
of the Appomattox County Zoning Ordinance pertaining to rear yard setback. Property is Tax

Mr. Ray Noble came forward and gave his address as Route 1 Box 636, Appomattox, Virginia.
Mr. Noble began that all he wanted was a carport that was portable. Mr. Noble explained that he
had looked at several in the County that were installed exactly in the location that he wanted to
put his. Mr. Noble stated that he had tried to buy property that joined him in order to be able to
comply with the zoning requirements but was unable to do so.
Mrs. Noble stated that the topography of their lot did not allow them to place this structure in the
location that was required by the zoning ordinance.
Mr. Moore called for other comments.
Hearing none, Mr. Moore asked Mr. Sears to read the next petition.
Mr. Sears read petition number 00-415 of Charles and Dorothy Bub to rezone property from V-1
to B-1. Property is Tax Map No. 41 (6) C and is located on Route 24 between Route 701 and
