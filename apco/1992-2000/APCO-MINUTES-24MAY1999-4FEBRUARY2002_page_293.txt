Mr. Moore called the public hearing to order at 7:05 P.M. and explained that the purpose of this
hearing to receive public comments on several zoning petitions. Mr. Moore then explained the
procedure for addressing comment to the Board. Mr. Moore next asked Mr. Caldwell, Clerk to the
Planning Commission, to read the first petition.
Mr. Caldwell read petition #00-420 - Timothy and Jeanette Moore request Conditional Use Permit
to allow two family dwelling in A-1 zoned district. Property is part of Tax Map No. 36-A-22B and
is located on Route 653 between Route 608 and Route 721.
Mrs. Moore came forward and stated that they wished to build a 3800 sq .ft. home with two (2)
kitchens to provide an apartment for her mother-in-law who is 72 years old. They have talked with
their neighbors and to their knowledge there are no objections to this.
Mr. Moore asked if anyone wished to speak against this petition.
Hearing none, Mr. Moore asked Mr. Caldwell to read the next petition.
Mr. Caldwell read petition #00-421 - Clayton Bryant, Sr. requests to rezone property from A-1 to
B-1. Property is part of Tax Map No. 60-A-53 and 54 and is located on Route 460 between Route
609 and Route 608.
Mr. Moore asked if anyone wished to speak for or against this petition.
Hearing none, Mr. Moore asked Mr. Caldwell to read the next petition.
Mr. Caldwell read petition #00-422 - Willie A. Coleman requests to rezone property from B-1 to M-
1 with Conditional Use Permit to allow Truck and Heavy Machinery Repairs. Property is part of Tax
Map Parcel No. 60-A-53 (Concord Business Park, Lot 10) and is located on Route 460 between
Route 609 and Route 608.
Mr. Coleman came forward and stated he would like to construct a 40 ft x 60 ft. shop to work on
trucks and heavy machinery. Building to be approximately 550 ft. from Route 460 and have a
parking area of approximately 80 ft. x 80 ft. in front of building.
Mr. Moore asked if anyone wished to speak against this petition.
Hearing none, Mr. Moore asked Mr. Caldwell to read the next petition.
Mr. Caldwell read petition #00-423 of John & Laura Roberts who are requesting a Conditional Use
Permit to allow additional dwelling on single lot. Property is Tax Map Parcel #778-1-2 and is zoned
A-1. Property is located on Route 632 between Route 634 and Route 705.
Mr. Moore called for comments against this petition.
