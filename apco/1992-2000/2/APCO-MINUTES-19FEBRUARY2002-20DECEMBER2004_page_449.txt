At 6:30 p.m. called the meeting to order and asked Mr. Craft to deliver the invocation.
Mr. Carter stated there was a new item on the Agenda that should help the Board
throughout the new year entitled “Setting of Agenda”.
Mr. Carter also asked if any member wished to add anything to the agenda that was not
included.

please add to Item 4-A — Elementary School Committee: Ms. Anne Dixon and Item 4-E.
— Community Center Board: Mr. Shawn Armbrust.
