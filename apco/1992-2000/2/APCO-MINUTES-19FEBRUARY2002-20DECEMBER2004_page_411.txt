4. Recreational Access Funding Agreement: The Board has received the
agreement between the County and VDOT regarding the funding to build the
recreation access road and bike path for the recreational park on Route 613.
Staff recommends the Chairman be authorized to execute this agreement.
Mr. Kendrick made a motion seconded by Mr. Moore to approve the Recreational Access
Funding Agreement as written and to authorize Mr. Carter, Chairman to execute this
document.
