Mr. Conrad made a motion, seconded by Mr. Craft, to approve the following
ordinance:
ORDINANCE FOR REPEAL OF
CHAPTER 87 HUNTING :
OF THE APPOMATTOX COUNTY CODE.
Be it Ordained that:
a) Chapter 87, HUNTING, of the Appomattox County Code is hereby repealed:
b) This Ordinance is adopted pursuant to Sections 15.2-1426 and 1427 of the
Code of Virginia;
c) This ordinance shall be effective immediately.
AppOrd

Mr. Carter, nay. (3 aye votes, 2 nay)
