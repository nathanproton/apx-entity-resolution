At 6:36 p.m. Mr. Carter opened the meeting and gave the invocation.
Mr. Carter welcomed everyone to the meeting and stated the first item on the
agenda was:
1. Courthouse Financing and possible refinancing of the 199¢ IDA
Bond.
At 6:38p.m., Bill Slagle, Chairman of the IDA, stated that the IDA would reopen
their recessed meeting at this time.
