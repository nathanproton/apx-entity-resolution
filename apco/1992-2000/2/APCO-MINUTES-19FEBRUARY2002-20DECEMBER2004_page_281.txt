Supervisors held its regularly scheduled meeting, a Planning
Commission Public Hearing and a Public Hearing on the FY04
Budget in the Council Chambers located at the Appomattox
Town Office Building on Linden Street, Appomattox, Va.

invocation.
Mr. Carter next called for Citizen Public Comments: This time is
provided to allow citizens to appear to discuss issues that are of
importance or concern to them.
Rich Guild came forward. Mr. Guild wanted to review a report he
handed to the Board members. He is speaking as the Webmaster
for the County website. He pointed out that tourism is the world’s
largest industry. It the third largest employer in Virginia and
