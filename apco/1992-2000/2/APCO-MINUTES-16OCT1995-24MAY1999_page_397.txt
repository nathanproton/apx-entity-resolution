Mr. Bosiger called the public hearing to order at 7:00 P.M. stated the purpose of the
hearing was to receive public comment on several zoning petitions and stated the
procedure for addressing comments.
Mr. Bosiger then asked Mr. Caldwell, Clerk to the Planning Commission to read the first
petition.
Mr. Caldwell read petition number 98-337 of Robert Bryant for a conditional use permit
to allow mobile home on 4.0 acres with existing dwelling. Property is Tax Map Parcel
#41 (A) 45 and is located on Route 616 between Route 24 and Route 655. Property is
zoned V-1.
Mr. Bosiger called for comments on this petition.
Mrs. Dale Bryant came forward and gave her address as Route 3 Box 416, Appomattox,
Virginia. Mrs. Bryant asked the Board of Supervisors to grant her request to put a mobile
home on her property for her mother and that she be allowed to keep the mobile home
there in the event that her mother passes away.
Mr. Bosiger called for other comments on this petition.
Hearing none Mr. Bosiger asked Mr. Caldwell to read the next petition.
Mr. Caldwell read petition number 98-338 of John R. Cole for a conditional use permit
for home occupation (Sporting good sales) within main dwelling. Property is Tax Map
Parcel #88 (4) 6 & 7 and is located on Route 733 between Route 727 and Dead-end.
Property is zoned R-1.
Mr. Bosiger called for comments on this petition.
Mr. John R. Cole came forward and gave his address as Route 5 Box 956, Appomattox,
Virginia. Mr. Cole continued that he has had a Federal Firearms License for some time
and that due to his family moving he has to have zoning approval at the new location
