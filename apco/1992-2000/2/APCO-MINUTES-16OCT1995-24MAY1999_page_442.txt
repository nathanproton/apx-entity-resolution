Mr. Bosiger called the public hearing to order and explained the purpose of the hearing
was to receive comments on a zoning petition. Mr. Bosiger then stated the procedure for
addressing comments and called on the Clerk to the Planning Commission, Bill Caldwell
to read the only petition for consideration.
Mr. Caldwell read petition number 98-345 of Donald Lewis for a variance to paragraph
170-80A of the Appomattox County Zoning Ordinance pertaining to front yard setbacks
of dwelling. Property is part of Tax Map No. 19-1-5 and is located on Route 686
between Route 663 and Route 615.
Mr. Bosiger called for comments on this petition.
Hearing none, and there being no other petitions, Mr. Bosiger declared the public hearing
closed at 7:04 P.M.
Garet K. Bosiger, Chairman
A regular meeting of the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors was held on July 20,
1998 after the public hearing, in the Courthouse, Courthouse Square, North Court Street,
Appomattox, Virginia.
