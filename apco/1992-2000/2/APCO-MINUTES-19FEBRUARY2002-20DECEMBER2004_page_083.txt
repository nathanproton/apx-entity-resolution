proposed ordinances and proposed amendments to the Appomattox County Code. Anyone
wishing to comment should approach the microphone, give thier name and address for the
record, address the Board, and not the audience. The Board of Supervisors will not respond to
questions at this time but will note any questions raised during the public hearing and give
consideration to these in making its final decision.
