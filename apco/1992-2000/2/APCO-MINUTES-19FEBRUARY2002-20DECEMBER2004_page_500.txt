Old Elementary School:
A. CVCC Satellite Campus: Meeting with Todd Christianson has been
confirmed for April 6, 2004. There is great progress being made to
keep the existing structure intact without it becoming an eyesore for
the County and Town. Along these lines plans are being made to
establish a CVCC Satellite Campus and the Carver-Price Museum,
along with other entities that will make good use of this facility.
We are pursuing grants in two (2) different areas. DHCD/CDBG
grants. We need to progress forward in order to receive these
grants.
Mr. Carter recognized and thanked Blair Smith for his time and efforts
in helping with this project.
