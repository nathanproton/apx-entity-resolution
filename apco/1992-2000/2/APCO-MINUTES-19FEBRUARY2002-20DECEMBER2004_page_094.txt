At 5:00 p.m. on Tuesday, June 11, 2002 a Special meeting of the Appomattox County Board of
Supervisors was held at the Carver Price Cultural Center/Appomattox Assembly, Inc on Patricia
Anne Lane, Appomattox, VA. The purpose of this meeting being a planned Economic
Development Summit.
PRESENT: Samuel E. Carter, Chairman - Courthouse District
Russell H. Moore - Piney Mountain District
Frank H. Carwile - Wreck Island District
ABSENT: Jesse L. Hancock - Falling River District
Jerry G. Kendrick - Appomattox River District
ALSO PRESENT: — Andy Carroll - County Administrator
John Spencer - Assistant County Administrator
Brandon Stidham - County Planner
At 5:20 p.m following dinner, Mr. Carter called the meeting to order.
Mr. Bill Slagle, Chairman of the Industrial Development Authority of Appomattox County
welcomed everyone present, stated the purpose of the meeting, and introduced the speakers.
Ms. Shannon Heady, Virginia Economic Development Partnership of Farmville gave a
presentation on Why Economic Development? And How the Commonwealth Markets Virginia.
Mr. John Spencer gave a Marketing Update on Region 2000 as Ms. Kim Butler of Region 2000
was unable to be present.
Mr. Spencer also presented a report on IDA activities for the County.
A presentation on The Local Perspective - How Does Appomattox Stack Up?, What it Takes to
be Competitive in Today’s Marketplace and the Role of the IDA and Other Community Partners
was given by Shannon Heady and Neal Barber, Va Economic Development Partnership of
Richmond.
