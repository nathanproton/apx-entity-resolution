Mr. Carter then read the following :
Proclamation Commending Jesse Hancock on Service as
Appomattox County Board of Supervisor
Hwereas, Jesse Hancock, was duly elected by the citizens of the Falling River District to
serve as their representative on the Board of Supervisors since January 1, 2000; and
Whereas, Mr. Hancock has ably and passionately represented the citizens of his district
and Appomattox County as demonstrated by the countless hours dedicated to performing
the public’s business; and
Whereas, Mr. Hancock served as Vice-chair of the Appomattox County Board of
Supervisors in 2001 and 2002 including all the duties attached with those positions; and
Whereas, He served the citizens of the County with great distinction;
Now Hereby Be It Proclaimed, that the citizens of Appomattox County do hereby
express gratitude for the leadership and selfless service provided by Jesse Hancock
during his tenure on the Board of Supervisors and wish him well in all of his future
endeavors
December 15, 2003
Samuel E. Carter, Chairman
