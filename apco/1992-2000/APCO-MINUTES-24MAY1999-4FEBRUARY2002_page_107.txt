Mr. Bosiger called the public hearing to order at 7:05 P.M. stated the purpose of the
hearing was to receive public comment on several zoning petitions and gave the
procedure for addressing comments.
Mr. Bosiger then called on Mr. Caldwell to read the first petition.
Mr. Caldwell read petition number 99-396 of Robert C. Stephens for a variance to
paragraph 170-80-A of Appomattox County Zoning Ordinance pertaining to side yard
setback. Property is Tax Map No. 76-A-163 located on Business 460 between Route 635
and Route 460 and is zoned A-1.
Mr. Bosiger called for comments on this petition.
Hearing none, Mr. Bosiger asked Mr. Caldwell to read the next petition.
Mr. Caldwell read petition number 99-397 of William A. and Arlene P. Stratton to rezone
property from A-1 to M-1 with Conditional Use Permit for manufacturing musical
instruments. Property is Tax Map No. 76-A-158 located on Route 635 between Business
460 and Route 1101.
Mr. Bosiger called for comments on this petition.
Mr. Mario Luongo came forward and gave his address as Route 2 Box 187. Mr. Luongo
stated that he has seen Mr. Bryans work and that this business would be a credit to the
community.
