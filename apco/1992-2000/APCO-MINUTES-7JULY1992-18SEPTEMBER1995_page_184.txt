The regularly scheduled monthly meeting of the Appomattox County Board
of Supervisors was held in the Board’s meeting room, Courthouse Square,
Appomattox, Virginia, Monday, May 10, 1993 at 7:00 P.M.
Present:
W. E. Jamerson, Courthouse District (late)
Dennis W. Torrence, Appomattox River District
Nina B. Beasley, Falling River District
Frank H. Carwile, Wreck Island District
Russell H. Moore, Piney Mt. District
Also present:
Gordon L. Rice, County Administrator
Thomas W. Lawson, County Attorney
Mr. Torrence, Vice Chairman, presiding called the meeting to order at
7:00 P.M. and asked Mr. Carwile to give the invocation.
Mr. Torrence then called on Mr. Don Austin, Assistant Resident
Engineer, Virginia Department of Transportation. Mr. Austin
distributed to the Board copies of a proposed Resolution concerning the
priority list for the 93/94 Secondary Road Allocation. Mr. Torrence
asked if anyone had any questions for Mr. Austin. Discussion followed
concerning projects in progress at the present time. Mr. Rice informed
Mr. Austin of a request from Mr. D. S. Nash to reduce his road project
bond for Woodchuck Drive located in Hunting Ridge Subdivision to
$5,000.00. Mr. Austin informed the Board that the project was
sufficiently complete to approve the reduction in Mr. Nash’s bond. (Mr.
Jamerson arrived). Mr. Carwile made a motion seconded by Mrs. Beasley
to reduce Mr. Nash’s road bond for Woodchuck Drive to $5,000.00.
Vote: Mr. Jamerson, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr.
Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.
Mr. Jamerson thanked and excused Mr. Austin and called Mrs. Janet
Belter, Treasurer. Mrs. Belter presented financial reports for the
month of April 1993 to Board members. A discussion of the County’s
financial condition followed. Mrs. Belter requested permission to
attend the Treasurer’s Convention in June costing approximately $200.00
which is reimbursed by the State. Mr. Torrence made a motion seconded
by Mr. Carwile to approve Mrs. Belter’s’ request for $200.00 to attend
the Treasurer’s Convention. Vote: Mr. Jamerson, aye; Mrs. Beasley,
aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.
Mr. Jamerson thanked and excused Mrs. Belter and called Dr. Jack
Thomas, Superintendent of Schools to appear before the Board. Dr.
Thomas asked for an appropriation of $960,968.82 for June, $439,300.00
for July, and $400,300.00 for August 1993, for school expenses. Motion
by Mr. Torrence seconded by Mr. Moore to appropriate for June 1993
$960,968.82 and Encumber July’s appropriation of $439,300.00 and August
appropriation of $400,300.00. Vote: Mr. Jamerson, aye; Mrs. Beasley,
aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.
