Mr. Thompson stated that they send brochures to hotels only by request. They do have some
brochures in hotels in the Lynchburg and Farmville areas. They focus their efforts on Visitors
Centers, sending out approximately 50,000 a year. The problems areas seem to be in developing

encouraging them to come in. A couple of them came in and did a study but were not interested
