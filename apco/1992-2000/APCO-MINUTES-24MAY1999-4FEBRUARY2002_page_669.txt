8. Revenue Maximization: This is a program that allows localities to work with the Virginia
Department of Social Services to access additional federal funds to provide prevention
services for youth. In order to participate the Board needs to: 1) Authorize the County
Administrator to sign an Agreement between the County of Appomattox, Virginia and The
Virginia Department of Social Services regarding Local Government Expenditures in
Support of Federal Programs Administered by the Virginia Department of Social Services;
and, 2) Designate the local CPMT Committee (Community Policy Management Team) as
the group to develop a strategy for using these funds for preventative purposes and to
implement the strategy.
Mr. Carter stated this was a program that was very much needed in our County and he is in favor
of endorsing it.
Mr. Carter made a motion that Appomattox County participate in the Revenue Maximization
program and to authorize the County Administrator to sign the Agreement between the County of
Appomattox and the Virginia Department of Social Services regarding local government
expenditures in support of federal programs administered by Virginia Department of Social
Services and designating the local CPMT Committee as the group to develop a strategy for using
these funds for preventative purposes and implementing the strategy. Motion seconded by Mr.
Carwile. VOTE: Mr. Kendrick, aye; Mr. Carter, aye; Mr. Hancock, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; and
Mr. Moore, aye.
