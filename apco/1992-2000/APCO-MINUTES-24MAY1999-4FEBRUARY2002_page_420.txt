At 6:00 p.m. on November 20, 2000, the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors held it regularmeeting at the Appomattox County Courthouse, Courthouse Square, Court Street, Appomattox, VA.
PRESENT: Russell H. Moore, Chairman - Piney Mountain District
Jerry Kendrick - Appomattox River District
Jesse Hancock - Falling River District
Frank H. Carwile - Wreck Island District
ABSENT: Samuel Carter - Courthouse District
ALSO PRESENT: Andy Carroll - County Administrator
At 6:02 P.M Mr. Moore called the meeting to order and gave the invocation.
Mr. Moore explained that a closed session was necessary under Code Section 2.1-344(A)7 - legalissues and Code Section 2. 1-344(A)1 - personnel matters.
At 6:04 P.M. Mr. Carwile made a motion seconded by Mr. Hancock to enter into closed session todiscuss a legal and/or personnel matter. VOTE: Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Kendrick, aye; Mr. Hancock,aye; and Mr. Carwile, aye.
