Mr. Caldwell read petition 92-171 of Paul and Faye Bugg to
rezone the property of Edna Anderson Estate from R-1 to R-2
to permit the construction of a single family dwelling with
basement apartment and a four (4) unit apartment building.
The property is located on lLee-Grant Avenue between Maple
Street and Ferguson Street.
Mr. Torrence asked to hear from the petitioner. There being
no response, Mr. Torrence asked to hear from those opposing
the petition. Mr. James C. Sefter, adjacent property
owner,spoke in opposition. Mr. Sefter wondered why have
zoning if zones were changed any time property owners
desired. Mr. Sefter also questioned if this change would be

defined as a quite low density residential area and that was

R-2 was defined as medium to high density housing and felt
this would change the character of the neighborhood. Mr.
Sefter continued by pointing out apartments on this property
would increase traffic and noise and create potential for
other problems like those recently experienced at other
apartment buildings located in the Town. Mr. Sefter
concluded by pointing out there were very few areas within
the Town zoned R-1 and he felt those left should be
preserved. Also, Mr. Sefter informed the Board he knows of
others opposing this rezoning application.
