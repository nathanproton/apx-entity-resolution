Mrs. Ferguson asked the Board of Supervisors if they would like to pass
a resolution to send to VA Power requesting a service person be placed
in Appomattox County. Mrs. Ferguson continued by explaining that the
Town of Appomattox had already passed such a resolution.
Mr. Carwile made a motion seconded by Mr. Paulette to adopt the
following resolution:
RESOLUTION
WHEREAS, our local citizens are experiencing difficulty communicating
to the proper person either in the Farmville office or other offices of
Virginia Power during storms and emergencies; and
WHEREAS, Virginia Power revenues have increased over the years and
Virginia Power has diligently improved the service to our community; and
WHEREAS, it is of the utmost urgency to our citizens that a service
person be readily accessible to properly service the Virginia Power
consumers in our area; and
WHEREAS, the County of Appomattox is ever seeking avenues to assist our
citizens; and
NOW THEREFORE, be it resolved the County of Appomattox Board of
Supervisors respectfully requests that a Service Representative be
assigned full time to Appomattox County and the Town of Appomattox and
be required to live in the County.
VOTE: Mr. Torrence, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr
Moore, aye; and Mr. Paulette, aye.
