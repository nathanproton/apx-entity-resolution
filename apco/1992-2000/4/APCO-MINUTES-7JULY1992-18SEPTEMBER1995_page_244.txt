Mr. Jamerson next called Mr. Charlie Peterson, Solid Waste
Administrator. Mr. Peterson began by presenting the Board with the
gate activity report for July and annually. After discussion on the
gate activity report Mr. Peterson reported on the construction of Cell
"I", The underdrain has been installed and is working well. The road
has not been completed but construction is on schedule. Mr. Peterson
continued to discuss Cell "I" and the underdrain system. Mr. Peterson
explained that Hurt & Proffitt has suggested the County monitor the
water table daily, on the underdrain system. Mr. Peterson has
proceeded with this testing using a borrowed "watertable indicator"
from Hurt & Proffitt. Since this will be an on going testing, Mr.
Peterson requests an appropriation in the amount of $400.00 to purchase
the needed equipment. Mr. Carwile made a motion seconded by Mr.
Torrence to appropriate $400.00 to purchase a "watertable indicator"
for use at the landfill. Vote: Mr. Jamerson, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye;
Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; and Mr. Torrence, aye.
Mr. Peterson next discussed the results of the surface water testing
that has been done at the landfill. Mr. Peterson says we are below
State standards in all areas of contaminants. Mr. Peterson suggests
that the County proceed with these tests as agreed to previously by the
Board. The Board told Mr. Peterson to proceed with this testing
program.
Mr. Peterson explained Certification required for landfill operators

aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye and Mr.
Torrence, aye.
