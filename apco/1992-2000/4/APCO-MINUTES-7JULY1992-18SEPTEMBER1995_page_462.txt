Chairman
A special meeting of the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors was
held at 6:30 P.M. July 12, 1994 in the Board of Supervisor’s meeting
room adjacent to the Circuit Court Clerk’s Office. The meeting was
requested by Supervisors Torrence and Paulette to consider the
following items: 1) Landfill - Add on to closure contract; 2) Juvenile
& Domestic Relations Court - Fax Machine; 3) Nash Container Site: 4)
Delinquent Tax list.
Mr. Torrence presiding called the meeting to order at 6:30 P.M.
Present:
Mr. Dennis W. Torrence, Chairman - Appomattox River District
Mrs. Nina B. Beasley - Falling River District
Mr. Frank H. Carwile - Wreck Island District
Mr. Russell H. Moore - Piney Mountain District
Mr. Philip E. Paulette - Courthouse District
Also Present:
Mrs. Aileen T. Ferguson - County Administrator

written waiver of the notice and Chairman Torrence directed the County
