A recessed meeting and budget work session was held April 18, 1994 at
7:00 P.M. in the Board of Supervisors meeting room, Courthouse Square,
North Court Street, Appomattox, Virginia.
Present:
Dennis W. Torrence, Chairman - Appomattox River District
Russell H. Moore - Piney Mountain District
Philip E. "Gus" Paulette - Courthouse District
Absent:
Nina B. Beasley - Falling River District
Frank H. Carwile - Wreck Island District
Also Present:
Aileen T. Ferguson, County Administrator
Mr. Torrence, Chairman, presiding called the meeting to order at 7:02
P.M.
Mrs. Ferguson presented a request from the National Historical Park for
dumpster service to be provided by the County, discussion followed. No
action was taken on this matter.
Budget discussion and review followed on the following department
requests: 1301 Electoral Board/Officials; 1302 Registrar; 2101 Circuit
Court; 2102 General District Court; 2105 Juvenile/Domestic Relations
Court; 2106 Circuit Court Clerk; 2109 Court Services Unit; 2201
Commonwealth Attorney.
The meeting was adjourned at 9:00 P.M. until April 25, 1994 at 7:00 P.M
