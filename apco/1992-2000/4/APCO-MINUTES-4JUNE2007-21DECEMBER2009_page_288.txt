On Monday, June 2, 2008 at 6:30 p.m. the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors held its
regular scheduled meeting at the Appomattox Community Center, located at 220 Park Lane,
Appomattox, Virginia.
Present: William H. Craft - Appomattox River District
Samuel E. Carter — Courthouse District
Thomas H. Conrad ~ Falling River District
Russell H. Moore ~ Piney Mountain District
Gary W. Tanner — Wreck Island District
Also Present: Aileen T. Ferguson- County Administrator
John K. Spencer — Assistant County Administrator
J.G. Overstreet — County Attorney
Vicky Phelps — Administrative Assistant
Chairman Craft called the regular scheduled meeting to order at 6:35 p.m. and called on Mr.
Carter to deliver the invocation. Everyone joined in the Pledge of Allegiance to the American
Flag.
Chairman Craft welcomed everyone to the meeting and then called for setting of the agenda.
With no changes to the agenda, Chairman Craft proceeded to the Public Comment Session,
hearing none; Chairman Craft presented Administrative Iteri #1 — Social Services FY2009
Budget Approval.
Chairman Craft called for a motion. Mr. Tanner made a motion to approve the FY2009 Social
Services Budget in the amount of $1,157,259.86 with local funds in the amount of $305,777.14.
Mr. Carter seconded Mr. Tanner’s motion. Chairman Craft called for a roll call vote: Mr. Carter,
aye; Mr. Conrad, aye; Mr. Craft, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Tanner.
Mr. Tanner asks Mr. Craft on behalf of the Board to thank Social Services for their cooperation
during this difficult budget process.
