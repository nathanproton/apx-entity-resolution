RESOLUTION OF THE BOARD OF SUPERVISORS OF
THE COUNTY OF APPOMATTOX, VIRGINIA APPROVING A PLAN OF
FINANCNING WITH THE ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY OF
APPOMATTOX COUNTY, VIRGINIA, FOR THE ISSUANCE OF LEASE REVENUE
AND REFUNDING BONDS
WHEREAS, the Board of Supervisors (the “Board of Supervisors”) of the County of
Appomattox, Virginia (the County”) has directed Davenport & Company LLC (“Davenport”)
and Sands, Anderson, Marks & Miller, a Professional Corporation, the County’s Bond Counsel
(together with Davenport, the “Consultants”) to evaluate financing options and recommend a
plan of financing (the “Plan of Financing”) to finance (i) the refunding of certain County lease
obligations, including: the County’s outstanding School Capital Lease Purchase Obligation,
Series 2001, its Public Facility Lease Revenue Note, Series 20034, its Public Facility Lease
Revenue Refunding Note, Series 2003B, its Public Facilities Lease Revenue Bond, Series 2005
and its Public Facilities Lease Revenue Bond, Series 2006 (together, the “Outstanding
Bonds”) and (ii) to fund certain utility improvements for the County (the “Project”); and
WHEREAS, the Consultants have considered the Plan of Financing and have
recommended that the Board of Supervisors on behalf of the County select the financing set forth
in the indication of interest response received from Sun Trust Bank (the “Selected Option”)
wherein Davenport will purchase the Bonds (as defined below) to be repaid by a financing lease
between the County and the Authority to be structured to conform to the Selected Option; and
WHEREAS, the Board of Supervisors has reviewed each of the indications of interest
and the recommendation from the Consultants and has determined that the Selected Option is the
most beneficial financing method and desires to accept the Plan of Financing with such Selected
Option which refunds the Outstanding Bonds with a refunding portion of the Bonds at the most
flexible fixed interest rate in accordance with the Selected Option, maturing in approximately 14
years, and the new money portion of the Bonds at the most flexible fixed interest rate for the
time period specified in the Selected Option, maturing in approximately 22 years; and
WHEREAS, the Board of Supervisors will request the Economic Development
Authority of Appomattox County, Virginia (the “Authority”) to issue, offer and sell its (i) lease
revenue refunding bond in the principal amount of approximately $11,400,000 (the “2009A.
Bond”) pursuant to the Selected Option to refund the Outstanding Bonds and (ii) lease revenue
bond in the principal amount of approximately $3,500,000 (the “2009B Bond”, together with
the 2009A Bond, the “Bonds”) all pursuant to the Plan of Financing, with the leasing by the
Authority of the County’s Carver Price Educational Complex, Courthouse Building and
Administration Building (the “Leased Property”) from the County and, in turn, lease the
