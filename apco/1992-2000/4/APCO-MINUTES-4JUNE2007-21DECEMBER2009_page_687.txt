Chairman Conrad presented Administrative Item #4 — Resolution upon Retirement and stated
that Mr. Moore has decided to retire as of December 31, 2009 and staff is recommending the
Board to adopt the following resolution acknowledging his faithful and dedicated service to
Appomattox County citizens. Chairman Conrad called for discussion.
RESOLUTION COMMEND ATION
MR. RUSSELL H. MOORE FOR SERVICE ON THE
APPOMATTOX COUNTY BOARD OF SUPERVISORS
WHEREAS, Mr. Russell H. Moore, was elected to the Appomattox
County Board of Supervisors November 1991; and
WHEREAS, During his years of service, he represented the concerns and
needs of Appomattox County citizens in an effective and compassionate
manner; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Moore served Eighteen years on the Appomattox
County Board of Supervisors; and
WHEREAS, He freely gave his time, talents and expertise to help make
the lives of those he served better;
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED That the Appomattox County
Board of Supervisors on this 7 day of December, 2009, does hereby
commend and thank Mr. Russell H. Moore for his years of service and
achievement as a member of the Appomattox County Board of
Supervisors; and
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED That Appomattox County Board of
Supervisors sincerely thanks Mr. Russell H. Moore and wishes him much
happiness andfulfillment in his further pursuits.
Mr. Tanner stated that he admired Mr. Moore for what he has done for the County and then made
a motion to adopt the resolution acknowledging Mr. Moore for his faithful and dedicated service
to Appomattox County citizens. Mr. Carter seconded Mr. Tanner’s motion. Chairman Conrad
