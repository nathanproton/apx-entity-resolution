On Monday, August 31, 2009 at 1:30 p.m. the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors held a
special called meeting in the County Administration Conference room located on 153A Morton
Lane, Appomattox, Virginia 24522
Present: Mr. Thomas H. Conrad — Chairman — Falling River District
Mr. William H. Craft -Appomattox River District
Mr. Gary W. Tanner — Wreck Island District
Absent: Mr. Russell H. Moore — Piney Mountain District
Mr. Samuel E. Carter — Courthouse District
Also Present: Aileen T. Ferguson — County Administrator
J.G. Overstreet — County Attorney
John Spencer — Assistant County Administrator
Johnnie Roark — Planner
Earl Dickerson — Hurt & Proffitt
Chairman Conrad called the special meeting of the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors to
order at 1:30 p.m. and stated the purpose of this special called meeting would be to enter into
closed session in accordance to Section 15.2-2907(D) Annexation; 15.2-2711(A)7 — Legal
Council for Negotiation; 15.2-2711(A)6 - Investment.
Mr. Tanner made a motion to enter into closed session at 1:33 p.m. in accordance to Section
15.2-2907(D) Annexation; 15.2-2711(A)7—Legal Council for Negotiation; 15.2-2711(A)6 -
Investment. Mr. Craft seconded Mr. Tanner’s motion. Hearing no discussion, Chairman Conrad
called for a vote: Mr. Conrad, aye; Mr. Craft, aye; Mr. Tanner, aye.
Mr. Craft made a motion to enter into regular session at 3:00 p.m. Mr. Tanner seconded Mr.
Craft’s motion. Hearing no discussion, Chairman Conrad called for a vote: Mr. Conrad, aye; Mr.
Craft, aye; Mr. Tanner, aye.
