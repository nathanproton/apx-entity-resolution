A special recessed meeting and budget work session was called by the
Appomattox County Board of Supervisors March 22, 1993 at 7:12 P.M. in
the Board’s meeting room adjacent to the Circuit Court Clerk’s Office,
Courthouse Square, North Court Street, Appomattox, Virginia.
Present:
W. E. Jamerson, Chairman, Courthouse District
Dennis W. Torrence, Vice Chairman, Appomattox River District
Nina B. Beasley, Falling River District
Frank H. Carwile, Wreck Island District
Russell H. Moore, Piney Mountain District
Also Present:
Gordon L. Rice, County Administrator
Mr. Jamerson, presiding, called the meeting to order at 7:00 P.M. and
asked the Board to consider several requests for FY93 budget line item
transfers. After review and discussion, Mr. Carwile made a motion
seconded by Mrs. Beasley to approve the following line item transfers:
Recreation - $200.00 from 7102-3006 and $75.00 from 7102-5801 to
7102-5203 to pay telephone bills
General Properties - $500.00 from 4202-5103 to 4302-5407 to clean
carpets in General District Court Offices and
Health Department
Vote: Mr. Jamerson, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr.
Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.
Mr. Jamerson next asked the Board to consider several request for
appropriations from unappropriated surplus. After review Mr. Carwile
made a motion seconded by Mrs. Beasley to appropriate:
Registrar - $87.00 to 1302-5201 for postage
County Administrator - $2,042.68 to 1201-5401 to cover additional
expenses due to new computer system
Vote: Mr. Jamerson, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr.
Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.
