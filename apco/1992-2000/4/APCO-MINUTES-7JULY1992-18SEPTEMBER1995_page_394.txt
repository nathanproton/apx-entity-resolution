Mr. Torrence, next presented a request from Bruce S. Drinkard for cow
killed by stray dogs. Mr. Paulette made a motion seconded by Mrs.
Beasley to pay Bruce S. Drinkard $294.00 for a cow killed by stray dogs
and also to make a supplemental appropriation of $134.00 to 3501-5802
to cover this claim. Vote: Mr. Torrence, aye; Ms. Beasley, aye; Mr.
Moore, aye; and Mr. Paulette, aye.
Mrs. Ferguson presented a request from the Commissioner of Revenue for
a supplemental appropriation to pay for programming services. Mrs.
Beasley made a motion seconded by Mr. Moore to appropriate $634.71 asia
supplemental appropriation to 1209-7002 for programming services.
Vote: Mr. Torrence, aye; Ms. Beasley, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; and Mr.
Paulette, aye.
A request from the Clerk of Circuit Court was presented next for
$524.00 for Compensation of Jurors. Mr. Paulette made a motion
seconded by Mrs. Bealey to appropriate $524.00 as a supplemental
appropriation to 2101-1007 as Compensation for Jurors.
Mr. Williams of Court Services Unit has requested an additional
appropriation to cover juvenile detention. Mr. Moore made a motion
seconded by Mr. Paulette to approve a supplemental appropriation of
$952.00 to Court Services FY94 budget for court ordered juvenile
detention. Vote: Mr. Torrence, aye; Ms. Beasley, aye; Mr. Moore, aye
and Mr. Paulette, aye.
General Properties has requested a supplemental appropriation to
replace funds reimbursed by the insurance company for storm damage to
the Health Department. Mr. Moore made a motion seconded by Mrs.
Beasley to approve a supplemental appropriation to 4302-5407 in the
amount of $1,099.93. Vote: Mr. Torrence, aye; Ms. Beasley, aye; Mr.
Moore, aye; and Mr. Paulette, aye.
General Properties is also requesting a supplemental appropriation to
cover expenses for the Health Department renovation. Mr. Paulette made
a motion seconded by Mrs. Beasley to approve $1,683.40 as a
supplemental appropriation to 4302-3002 and $2,134.63 to 4302-5407, for
Health Department renovations. Vote: Mr. Torrence, aye; Ms. Beasley,
aye; Mr. Moore, aye; and Mr. Paulette, aye.
Mrs. Ferguson requested a supplemental appropriation to cover expenses
for the evacuation shelter. Mr. Paulette made a motion seconded by
Mrs. Beasley to approve a supplemental appropriation to Emergency
Services FY94 budget of $40.11 to cover expenses associated with the
evacuation shelter. Vote: Mr. Torrence, aye; Ms. Beasley, aye; Mr.
Moore, aye; and Mr. Paulette, aye.
