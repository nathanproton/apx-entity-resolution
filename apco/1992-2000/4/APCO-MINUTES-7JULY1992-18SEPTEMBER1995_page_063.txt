The Board next considered two (2) requests to attend
conferences and a request to pay for police training. After
discussion Mr. Torrence made a motion seconded by Mr. Moore
to pay $326.00 expenses for Bill Caldwell to attend the
annual Virginia Building and Code Officials Association
Conference in Hampton November 15-17, 1992; to pay $370.00
expenses for Brian Armontrout to attend the Virginia Library
Association Conference in Richmond November 12-14, 1992; and
to pay $2684.00 training expenses for twenty-two police
officers in FY93. The $2,684.00 is to come from
unappropriated surplus. Vote: Mr. Jamerson, aye; Mrs.
Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence,
aye.
Mr. Rice next informed the Board of seven (7) employees that
have elected to sign up for BC/BS medical insurance coverage
through the County’s group policy. This will cost $7,875.00.
The Board next considered Mrs. Beasley’s reappointment to the
Community Services Board. Her current term expires December
31, 1992 and the length of the new term will be for three (3)
years. Mr. Carwile made a motion seconded by Mr. Moore to
appoint Mrs. Beasley to the Central Virginia Community
Services’ Board for a term of three (3) years effective
January 1, 1993 to December 31, 1995. Vote: Mr. Jamerson,
aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr.
Torrence, aye.
