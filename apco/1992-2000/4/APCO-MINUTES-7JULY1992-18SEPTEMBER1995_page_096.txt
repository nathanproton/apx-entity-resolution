On November 16 and 17, 1992, I, Gordon L. Rice, as County
Administrator conducted a telephone poll asking if the Board
wished to donate $200.00 as a Christmas gift for members of
the Appomattox Volunteer Fire Department. Mr. Dick Martin
requested the donation and explained the money would be
combined with donations of others and distributed to the
firemen at a Christmas dinner given in their honor December
2, 1992. Mr. Martin also explained the County had
contributed a similar amount last year.
Vote: Mr. Jamerson - Aye
Mrs. Beasley - Aye
Mr. Moore - Aye
Mr. Carwile - Aye
Mr. Torrence - Nay
Also, Supervisor Carwile asked me to check with other
Supervisors to determine if they objected to Mr. Carwile
audio taping Board Meetings. Mr. Carwile said the tapes were
intended for his reference only because he had difficulty
hearing at times.
Mrs. Beasley - no objection
Mr. Moore - no objection
Mr. Jamerson - object LO)
Mr. Torrence - object
