Mr. Conner stated that the primary reason that Appomattox County has been overlooked by
prospects is due to the infrastructure not being in place in the Industrial Park.
Mr. Conner stated that the EDA focused on what we should be doing to move forward and
looked at other programs to see how they were working and what the EDA felt were similar
localities to Appomattox County. Those factors include demographic and comparable
environment which are similar roads no interstate and mostly rural. The reason this was done
was because there has been a lot of concern of what results will be achieved in hiring a full time
economic development coordinator. Mr. Conner stated that if you look at the facts that have
been presented you will find that every County that has a full time economic development
coordinator has added industry and jobs to those counties in the last couple of years. Mr. Conner
stated that a lot of jobs have been provided in similar counties with an economic development
coordinator.
