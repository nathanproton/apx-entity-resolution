A Special Meeting of the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors and
budget work session was held April 22, 1993 at the Courthouse,
Courthouse Square, North Court Street, Appomattox, Virginia.
Present:
W. E. "Bill" Jamerson, Courthouse District
Dennis W. Torrence, Appomattox River District
Nina B. Beasley, Falling River District
Frank H. Carwile, Wreck Island District
Russell H. Moore, Piney Mountain District
Also Present:
Gordon L. Rice, County Administrator
Mr. Jamerson, Chairman, presiding called the meeting to order at 7:46
P.M. shortly after the close of the Public Hearing.
Work on the proposed FY94 budget continued. After discussion, Mrs.
Beasley made a motion seconded by Mr. Carwile to permit the Sheriff to
purchase two (2) new cars costing $30,371.00 paying for them with
unneeded money in his FY93’s budget. Vote: Mr. Jamerson, aye; Mrs.
Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.
After discussion, Mr. Torrence made a motion seconded by Mrs. Beasley
to pay for the new roll-off truck needed to collect solid waste
transfer bins and 10 new 40 c.y. containers using money from the
Landfill Bond proceeds.
