Chairman Craft next called on Ms. Valarie Daughtery to come and appear before the Board.
Chairman Craft explained that Ms. Daughtery had requested to meet with the Board to give an
update on the happenings at the Appomattox Courthouse Theatre.
Ms. Daughtery began by thanking the board on behalf of the Courthouse Theatre Board of
Directors for their continued use of the old courthouse for the theater. Ms. Daughtery explained

music and drama.

Mr. Carter thanked Ms. Daughtery for providing this service to the citizens of Appomattox
County.
Ms. Daughtery also stated that they have a Civil War Melodrama in the upcoming productions.
Ms. Daughtery stated that they have had great support from citizens and businesses in and around
the County.
