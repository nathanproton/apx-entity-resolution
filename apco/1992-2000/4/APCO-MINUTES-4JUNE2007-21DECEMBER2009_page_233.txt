Chairman Craft next presented Administrative Item #7a - Use of vehicles on Courtland
Field. Chairman Craft explained that a request had been made by the Lions Club to use
Courtland Field and also be able to have vehicles on the field for their annual event.
Mr. Conrad stated that the Lions Club Festival brought in over $30,000 last year and they
are hoping that it will grow and be even bigger this year. Mr. Conrad explained that the
money brought in is dispensed to civic organizations throughout the County, and addition
it is also used by this organization in repairing eye glasses, hearing aids, and obtaining
see in eye dogs as needed. Mr. Conrad feels certain that the Lions Club would adhere to
anything the Board would ask of them to do as far as keeping the field in good condition.
Mr. Tanner made a motion to allow the Lions Club to have vehicles on the Courtland
Field during the Lions Ciub Festival with the understanding that they repair any damage
they cause to the field. Mr. Conrad seconded Mr. Tanner’s motion. Vote: Mr. Carter,
aye; Mr. Conrad, aye; Mr. Craft, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Tanner, aye.
