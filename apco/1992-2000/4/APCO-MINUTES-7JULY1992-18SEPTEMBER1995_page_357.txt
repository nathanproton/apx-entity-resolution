A joint meeting of the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors and the
Appomattox Town Countil was held February 5, 1994 at 9:00 A.M. at the
Spout Spring Ruritan Club located on Route 460, Spout Spring, Virginia.
Present (Appomattox County Board of Supervisors):
Dennis Torrence - Appomattox River District
Frank Carwile - Wreck Island District

The meeting was called to order by Mayor Spiggle at 9:05 A.M. Mr.
Spiggle welcomed everyone and explained that Mr. Dennis Gragg was ther
representing Central Virginia Planning District Commission and to off
any information that may be helpful during discussion. Mr. Spiggle
began by bringing the group up to date concerning studies that have
been done addressing water and sewer for the Town and County. Mr.
Spiggle continued by saying that he did not feel that any further
studies were necessary.
A lengthy discussion followed with all agreeing that there is a need
extend water and sewer facilities beyond the present Town boundaries.
The group also decided to form a Committee made up of all the members
of the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors and all members of the
Appomattox Town Council, Mayor Ronnie Spiggle, Aileen Ferguson and
Wayne Lewis. The committee will work toward obtaining a site for an
industrial park, provide all utilities to the site, and work towards
future needs and concerns.
The meeting was adjourned at 11:00 A.M.
