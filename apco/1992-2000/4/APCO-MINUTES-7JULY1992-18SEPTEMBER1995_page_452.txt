Mr. Torrence called for nominations for School Board from the Wreck
Island District.
Mr. Carwile nominated Mr. Sanmy Scott for a term to expire December
1995.
Mr. Torrence asked for other nominations. Hearing none, the
nominations were closed.
Mr. Torrence called for a vote on appointing Sammy Scott to the School
Board representing the Wreck Island District to fill a term from July
1, 1994 - December 31, 1995. Vote: Mr. Torrence, aye; Mrs. Beasley,
aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; and Mr. Paulette, aye.
Mr. Torrence next asked for nominations for School Board from the Piney
Mountain District.
Mr. Moore explained that he had contacted Mrs. Ora McCoy, whose term
expires June 30, 1994, and she declined reappointment.
Mr. Moore then nominated Mr. Charles Booker for the School Board from
Piney Mountain District.
Mr. Torrence called for other nominations.
Hearing none, Mr. Torrence called for a vote on appointing Mr. Charles
Booker to the School Board representing the Piney Mountain District for
a term beginning July 1, 1994 to December 31, 1997. Vote: Mr.
Torrence, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; and
Mr. Paulette, aye.
Mrs. Ferguson gave the Board information concerning the Health
Departments FY95 budget reduction. Mr. Keesee had informed Mrs.
Ferguson that with the Board’s cut of $5,000.00 this would cause State
funding to also be reduced, with the total reduction being $16,000.00.
Mr. Keesee would like for the $5,000.00 to be reinstated. No action
was taken.
Mrs. Ferguson presented a request for transfer of funds, from the
Sheriff, from the Jail to the Sheriff’s Department.
Mr. Carwile made a motion seconded by Mr. Moore to approve a transfer
of $9,369.00 from 3301-8004 to 3102-1003. Vote: Mr. Torrence, aye;
Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; and Mr. Paulette,
aye.
Mrs. Ferguson next asked the Board to encumber funds from the FY94
budget to pay for three (3) cars for the Sheriff’s Department.
Mr. Carwile made a motion seconded by Mr. Moore to encumber $43,000.00
from FY94 funds to pay for three (3) cars for the Sheriff’s
Department. Vote: Mr. Torrence, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile,
aye; Mr. Moore, aye; and Mr. Paulette, aye.
