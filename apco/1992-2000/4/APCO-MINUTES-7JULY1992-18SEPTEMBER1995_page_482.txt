Mr. Moore, Vice-Chairman, presiding, called the meeting to order at
7:30 P.M. and stated the purpose of the public hearing.
Mr. Moore then asked Bill Caldwell, Clerk, Appomattox County Planning
Commission to read the first petition.
Mr. Caldwell read petition number 94-226 of Keith and Amy Martin for a
variance to Appomattox Town Zoning Ordinance, Section 74-145 and
74-147, pertaining to front and rear yard setback regulations.
Property is located on Cawthorne Street between Evergreen Avenue and
Dead End. Property is owned by Lorene M. Morris and is zoned R-1.
Mr. Moore asked if anyone wished to comment on this petition.
Hearing no comments, Mr. Moore asked Mr. Caldwell to read the next
petition.
Mr. Caldwell read petition number 94-227 of Anita Wells McCormick and
Denise G. Drinkard for a Conditional Use Permit for home occupation to
allow addition to mobile home to be used for Tanning and Hair Salon.
Property is zoned A-1 and is located on Route 611 between Route 26 and
Route 663.
\
Mr. Moore called for comments on this petition.
Hearing none, Mr. Moore stated that this property adjoins his property
and that he had no objection to this Conditional Use being granted.
Hearing no other comments, and being no further petitions, the meeting
was adjourned at 7:36 P.M. a) ;
