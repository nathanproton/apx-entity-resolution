Mr. Rice informed the Board of two (2) recent letters from
the Landfill Engineers. A lengthy discussion followed. By
consensus the Board directed Mr. Rice to contact Hurt and
Proffitt by letter and telephone and ask them to continue
with the plans as fast as possible.
The Board next considered the policy regarding County
employees soliciting private funds for improvements to County
facilities and/or programs and agreed by consensus that any
such action should have prior Board approval. The Board
directed Mr. Rice to so inform County employees.
The Board next discussed the definition of retirees as it
applies to the extended medical coverage. It was decided by
consensus that defeated Board members were not a retiree and
therefore not eligible for coverage beyond COBRA. It was
also decided Mrs. Ford, the recently retired Registrar, was
eligible for coverage until she reaches 65 years of age.
The status of the Industrial Development Authority was
discussed at length. Mr. Rice reported there was some
confusion about who was on the Authority and explained its
intent. After reviewing the minutes of several ancient Board
meetings, it was decided by consensus to continue the
Authority with the following members:
Doris Nash
Floyd Williams
Bruce Cowan
Ben Marshall
Herbert Fleshman
Sonny Tolley
