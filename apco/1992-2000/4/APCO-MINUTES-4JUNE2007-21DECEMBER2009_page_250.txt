Chairman Craft and Chairman Almond called the joint meeting to order at 7:00 p.m.
Chairman Craft explained the purpose of the public hearing and called on Mr. Roark to
give his presentation of proposed petitions.
Mr. Roark presented petition RZ-08001 of Judith Walker, who is requesting to rezone
two parcels. The first parcel to be rezoned from A-1 to B-1 is located on the North side of
Route 460. The second parcel to be rezoned from R-1 to B-1 is located on the South side
of Route 460. The parcels to be rezoned are parcels 61(A)56 and 61(A)55 and the total
amount of acreage for both parcels is 10.16 acres. Mr. Roark explained that applicant is
not sure what she wants to do with these parcels after they are rezoned.
Chairman Craft called for comments on this petition.
Ms. Judith Walker — 3710 Richmond Highway, Spout Spring, Virginia, addressed the
Board and stated that she is requesting this rezoning with no specific plans in mind. Ms.
Walker stated that she has a business as a beautician and her business is growing and she
anticipates either starting another business in a different location or looking at expansion.
Chairman Craft called for other comments.
Ms. Faye Franklin came forward and stated that her property adjoins Ms. Walker’s
property and wants to know how this is going to affect adjoining property.
Chairman Craft explained to Ms. Franklin that she would have an opportunity to have her
questions answered in the Planning Commission meeting.
Chairman Craft called for other comments.
