A special meeting of the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors was
held on October 31, 1994 at 7:00 P.M. in the Board of Supervisors
meeting room, North Court Street, Courthouse Square, Appomattox,
Virginia.
Present:
Dennis W. Torrence, Chairman - Appomattox River District
Nina B. Beasley - Falling River District
Frank H. Carwile - Wreck Island District
Russell H. Moore - Piney Mountain District
Philip E. "Gus" Paulette - Courthouse District
Also Present:
Aileen T. Ferguson, County Administrator
Thomas W. Lawson, County Attorney
Mr. Torrence called the meeting to order at 7:00 P.M. and asked
Supervisor Moore to give the invocation.
Mr. Torrence began by stating the purpose of the meeting was to discuss
the regional jail resolution and service agreement and asked Mr.
Lawson, County Attorney, if all was in order.
Mr. Lawson stated that the notices had been delivered and all appeared
to be in order.
Mr. Carwile asked if Mr. Torrence was going to call for a decision on
this issue tonight.
Mr. Torrence stated that he was.
Mr. Carwile then stated that the notice stated "discuss" and the
definition of discuss did not include voting.
After discussion, concerning the intent of the notice, Mrs. Ferguson
read a portion of the Code Section concerning Special Meetings, which
stated, "No matter not specified in the notice shall be considered at
such meeting, unless all the members of the board were present".
