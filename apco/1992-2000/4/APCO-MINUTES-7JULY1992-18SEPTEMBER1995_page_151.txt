Mr. Jamerson, presiding, called the meeting to order at 6:00 P.M. and
announced for the record that bids for construction work at the
Landfill had been accepted last Friday March 26, 1993. The County
received bids from four (4) contractors - Allegheny, Counts & Dobyns,
D. S. Nash, and Watts.
Mr. Earl Dickerson representing Hurt & Proffitt, the County’s
contracted Engineers, was called on by Mr. Jamerson. Mr. Dickerson
reviewed the project and the bids which included seven (7) options:
Option "A" - a two (2) feet thick clay liner
Option "B" - a one and one half (1 1/2) feet thick clay liner
Option "C" —- a one (1) foot thick clay liner
Option "D" - a 60 mil thick synthetic liner
Option "E" — leachate collection lines for area "E" and "F"
