The VPI & SU District Extension Service served dinner at 5:00 p.m.
During dinner, Mr. Bruce Jones welcomed everyone in attendance. Mr. Jones explained that the
Appomattox Extension Office had lost one employee, Wanda Evans with the Expanded Food,
Nutrition and Education Program, which is funded with USDA Funds, due to budget cuts.
Mr. Jones introduced each of the Extension employees and explained their titles of work with the
Appomattox Extension Office and then stated that a power point presentation had been put
together to give updates on the different program areas.
Ms. Tillotson came forward to give a brief update on the 4-H Program.
Ms. Tillotson briefed the Board on some activities that she is involved with such as In School
Programs, After School Clubs, Special Interest Clubs, Interstate Exchange Club, Summer 4H
Camp, Health Rocks, Youth Mile, Public Speaking, and Share the Fun which is a talent show.
Ms. Tillotson stated that during the In School Programs she works with every child in grades 3 -
8. Ms. Tillotson stated that the After School Clubs are working with cooking, sewing.
