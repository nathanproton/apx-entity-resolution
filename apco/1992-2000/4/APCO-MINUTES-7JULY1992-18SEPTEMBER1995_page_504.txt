Chairman
A regular meeting of the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors was
held on October 3, 1994 at 7:00 P.M. in the Board of Supervisors
meeting room, Courthouse Square, North Court Street, Appomattox,
Virginia.
Present:
Dennis W. Torrence, Chairman - Appomattox River District
Nina B. Beasley - Falling River District
Frank H. Carwile - Wreck Island District
Russell H. Moore - Piney Mountain District
Philip E. "Gus" Paulette - Courthouse District
Also Present:
Aileen T. Ferguson - County Administrator
Thomas W. Lawson - County Attorney
Mr. Torrence, Chairman, presiding, called the meeting to order at 7:04
P.M. and asked Mr. Moore to give the invocation.
Mr. Torrence asked Ms. Sandra Lindsey, Virginia Department of
Transportation, Resident Engineer, to appear before the Board.
Ms. Lindsay began by addressing concerns expressed by Mr. Carwile at a
previous meeting.
Mr. Torrence then questioned speed limit signs on 460 By-pass near the
Ballpark.
Ms. Lindsay stated that this matter was being taken care of.
Mr. Moore asked Ms. Lindsay about the unevenness of grass cut on the
shoulders of Route 26.
Ms. Lindsay explained that there was a problem with the type mower
being used and this had been corrected.
