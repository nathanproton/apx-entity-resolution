Chairman Conrad next presented Administrative Item#5- Joint Meeting with EDA and Planning
Commission and stated as the Board will remember at a previous meeting it had been asked that
a meeting with the EDA and Planning Commission be put on the agenda in May for the Board’s
consideration. Chairman Conrad called for discussion.
Chairman Conrad stated that Mr. Slagle had contacted him and he had been able to contact
Congressman Perriello and he is willing to come to Appomattox and meet with the Board and
EDA on Wednesday, May 27", 2009 at 4:00 p.m. Chairman Conrad stated that he feels it would
be in the Boards best interest to have this meeting before we set a date for this joint meeting that
had been requested. Chairman Conrad stated that Mr. Slagle is asking who the Board feels
would need to be a part of this meeting with Congressman Perriello. Chairman Conrad stated
that we should invite the Town of Appomattox and the Town of Pamplin. Chairman Conrad
explained that Congressman Perriello would be meeting to discuss program funding that can be
utilized by our County and what help he would be able to offer.
Chairman Conrad stated that this meeting would be held as a special called meeting and it was
the consensus of the Board for Chairman Conrad to contact Mr. Slagle. A location will be
determined and the Board will be notified of this location at their next meeting.
