A recessed meeting and budget work session was held on May 16, 1994 at
7:00 P.M. in the Board of Supervisor’s meeting room, adjacent to the
Circuit Court Clerk’s Office, Courthouse Square, North Court Street,
Appomattox, Virginia.
Present:
Dennis W. Torrence, Chairman - Appomattox River District
Nina B. Beasley - Falling River District
Frank H. Carwile - Wreck Island District
Russell H. Moore - Piney Mountain District
Philip E. "Gus" Paulette - Courthouse District
Also present:
Aileen T. Ferguson - County Administrator
Mr. Torrence, Chairman, presiding, called the meeting to order at 7:02
P.M. and asked Mr. Carwile to give the invocation.
Mr. Torrence welcomed Sheriff Richardson and Chief Jailer Godsey and
asked if they would like to give an overview of their proposed budgets
Sheriff Richardson began by explaining that in department 3301, Jail,
the major increase was $48,000.00 for inmate housing.
Discussion followed as to the reason for the increase in inmate housing
cost, medical cost for inmates, etc.
Next, the Board continued budget discussion on departments 2107; 3102;
3104; and 3105. Sheriff Richardson answered questions and explained
cost associated with his budget request.
Sheriff Richardson, also, requested a 4 percent increase for his
employees, as the School Board is proposing.
Being nothing further for Sheriff Richardson, Mr. Torrence thanked him
for his time.
Mr. Torrence reviewed with the Board a letter from Mrs. Belter,
Treasurer, giving the status of FY94 funds.
Review of other departments followed, with the decision to remove any
equipment from each department, such as, paper shredders, fax machines
etc. Also, convention and education expenses were removed from
individual department budgets.
The Board discussed adding $5000.00 to postage for the Treasurer to
allow her to mail decal information.
By consensus the Board agreed to include in the FY94 budget:
1. 1213 - Deduct $1000.00 from equipment for paper shredder
2. 2102 - Deduct $1200.00 for Fax Machine & additional $400.00
for telephone line for fax was deducted from the
budget.
