Mr. Wesley Williams, Chairman of the Appomattox County School Board, called the School
Board meeting to order at 6:00PM.
Thomas Conrad, Chairman, Appomattox County Board of Supervisors, called the Board of
Supervisors to order at 6:01PM.
Dr. Dorinda Grasty, Superintendent, welcomed everyone to the meeting and began with a power
point presentation.
The Power Point presentation began with student demographic data.
Dr. Grasty explained that the graduating class of 2009 consisted of 148 total students with 93
students receiving advanced diplomas; 46-students receiving standard diplomas; 8 students
receiving modified standard diplomas and one student who received their GED. In this class
upon graduation 57-students planned to go to a four year college; 46-students planned to go to a
two year college; 14-students planned to attend a trade school; six students planned to enlist in
the military; two students planned to enter into the workforce; two students will be attending the
Woodrow Wilson Rehab Center and eleven students were undecided.
