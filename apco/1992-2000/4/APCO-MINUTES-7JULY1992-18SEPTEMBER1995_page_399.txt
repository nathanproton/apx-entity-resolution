A recessed meeting and budget work session was held April 12, 1994 at
7:00 P.M. in the Board of Supervisors Meeting Room, Courthouse Square,
North Court Street, Appomattox, Virginia.
Present:
Dennis W. Torrence, Chairman - Appomattox River District
Nina B. Beasley - Falling River District
Russell H. Moore - Piney Mountain District
Philip E. "Gus" Paulette - Courthouse District
Absent:
Frank H. Carwile - Wreck Island District
Also present:
Aileen T. Ferguson, County Administrator
Mr. Torrence, Chairman, presiding, called the meeting to order at 7:00
P.M.
Mr. Torrence requested that a date be set for the next budget work
session. After discussion and by consensus of the Board, April 18,
1994 at 7:00 P.M. was set for the next budget work session.
Mrs. Ferguson presented a request from Sheriff Richardson from the
Asset/Forfeiture Account. Mrs. Beasley made a motion seconded by Mr.
Paulette to appropriate $171.78 from the Asset/Forfeiture Account.
Vote: Mr. Torrence, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; and Mr.
Paulette, aye.
Mrs. Ferguson next presented a request from Mr. Frank Stacy, Old
Dominion Appraisal Company for partial payment on his contract. After
discussion, the Board by consensus, denied the request and decided to
stay with the terms of the original contract.
Budget discussion followed with review of budget requests for
departments 1101 Board of Supervisors; 1201 County Administrator; 1204
Legal Services; 1206 Legislative Audit; 1209 Commissioner of Revenue
and 1213 Treasurer.
After review of the above budgets Mr. Paulette requested a list of the
supplemental appropriations approved for each department since the
budget was approved.
Being no further business the meeting was adjourned at 9:00 until April
18, 1994 at 7:00 P.M.
