Thomas H. Conrad, Chairman
On Monday, July 7, 2009 at 6:30 p.m. the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors held their
regular scheduled meeting at the Appomattox County Administration Building, located at 153A
Morton Lane, Appomattox ‘Virginia.
PRESENT: William H. Craft — Appomattox River District
Thomas H. Conrad — Falling River District
Russell H. Moore — Piney Mountain District
Gary W. Tanner — Wreck Island District
Samuel E. Carter - Appomattox Courthouse District
ALSO PRESENT: = Aileen T. Ferguson - County Administrator
John K. Spencer — Assistant County Administrator
J.G. Overstreet - County Attorney
Vicky Phelps — Administrative Assistant
Johnnie Roark — Planner
Chairman Conrad called the regular scheduled meeting of the Appomattox County Board of
Supervisors to order at 6:30 p.m. Mr. Tanner delivered the invocation which was then followed
by the Pledge of Allegiance to the American Flag.
Chairman Conrad called for the setting of the agenda. Hearing no additions to the agenda,
Chairman Conrad called for Public Comment and explained the procedure for addressing the
Board during this time.
Having no public comment, Chairman Conrad presented Administrative Item #1 — Agreement
with Campbell County Utilities and Service Authority and explained that Mr. Roark has
presented them with a draft agreement between CCUSA and the County for the purchase of
water and the Board had several items of concern that they wanted addressed. Mr. Conrad
explained that CCUSA had responded. Chairman Conrad stated if the Board is comfortable with
the response he would need a motion to enter into an agreement with CCUSA.
Mr. Tanner made a motion to enter into the agreement with CCUSA as amended. (A copy of the
agreement can be found in the Boardfile for July 2009) Mr. Moore seconded Mr. Tanner’s
motion. Chairman Conrad called for discussion, hearing none, he called for a vote: Mr. Carter,
aye; Mr. Conrad, aye; Mr. Craft, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Tanner, aye.
