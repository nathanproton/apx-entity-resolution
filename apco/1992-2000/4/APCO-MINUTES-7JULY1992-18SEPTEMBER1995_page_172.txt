By consensus the Board agreed to include in the FY94 budget:
1. salary increases of 3% effective December 1, 1993.
2. increased health insurance contribution to $129.05 per month
enrolled employee.
3. County Attorney’s salary at $12,000.00.
4. level funding for the Fire Department
5. $20,000.00 for renovations to bring County Office Building in
compliance with ADA requirements and to repair parking lots.
6. the Mental Health budget increasie as proposed.
7. contributions to:
(a) Appomattox Youth Association - $5,000.00
(b) Pamplin Recreation Center - $1,500.00
(c) American Legion Post 104 - $1,000.00
(d) Appomattox Railroad Festival - $1,500.00
(e) Arts Council - $1,000.00
(f) Community of Candles - $150.00
(g) ACHS After Prom - $250.00
8. salary increase for Liberian of 3% effective 12/1/93.
9. $10,000.00 contribution to the Chamber of Commerce plus
$5,000.00 after accreditation
10. $1,000.00 for Leaders of Tomorrow program and $2,400.00 for
telephone service at VPI Extension Office.
11. Funding for Central Virginia Area Agency on Aging of $3,500.00,
12. Funding for Region 2000 at $9,224.00
13. Funding for Robert E. Lee Soil & Water Conservation District
of $3,500.00
14. Funding for Central Virginia Planning District Commission of
$3,320.00.
