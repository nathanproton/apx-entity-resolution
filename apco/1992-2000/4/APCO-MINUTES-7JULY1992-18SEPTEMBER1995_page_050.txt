Mayor Spiggle of the Town of Appomattox called the County
Administrator September 18, 1992 and asked the County to
share expenses for a luncheon for the Railroad Festival’s
parade Grand Marshall October 10, 1992. The estimated cost
for the County’s share was $250.00. The County Administrator
polled the members of the Board of Supervisors by telephone
September 18 and 19, 1992. The result of the poll was:
W. E. Jamerson, Courthouse Dist. - aye
Dennis W. Torrence, Appomattox River Dist. - aye
Frank H. Carwile, Wreck Island Dist. - aye
Nina B. Beasley, Falling River Dist. - nay
Russell H. Moore, Piney Mt. Dist. - abstain
