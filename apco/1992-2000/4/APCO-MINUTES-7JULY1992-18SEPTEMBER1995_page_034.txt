Mr. Lawson asked to be and was recognized. Mr. Lawson
explained he recently had to have his computer serviced and
asked the County to reimburse him for the cost which was
$500.00. Mr. Lawson further explained this computer was his
personal property, but felt since he used the computer for
County business, the County should bear the cost of the
upkeep. After discussion, Mr. Carwile made a motion seconded
by Mrs. Beasley to pay $500.00 to reimburse Mr. Lawson for
the expense of the computer repairs. Vote: Mr. Jamerson,
aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr.
Torrence, aye.
The Board next considered and reviewed several ordinances and
the comments made on each by the consultant which has been
retained to update the County Code. No action was taken.
Mr. Moore made a motion at 9:20 P.M. seconded by Mrs. Beasley
to adjourn. Vote: Mr. Jamerson, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr.
Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Torrerce, aye.
