Mr. Jamerson, presiding, called the meeting to order at 6:00 P.M. and
recognized Mr. Dickerson representing Hurt & Proffitt, Inc. the
contracted Landfill Engineer. Mr. Dickerson presented each Board
member a tabulation of the four (4) bids received for the proposed
Landfill construction project discussed at the meeting last night April
29, 1993. The tabulation included summations of various combinations
of options.
After discussion and review, Mr. Torrence made a motion to award a
contract to include options A, E, and G. The motion died for lack of a
second.
Mr. Moore next made a motion seconded by Mr. Carwile to award a
contract which includes options C, E, and G. Vote: Mr. Jamerson, aye;
Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.
Option C is a 12" clay liner for the new trench. Option E is for
leachate collection lines for area E and F - the older portion of the
existing landfill which is to be capped and closed under this
contract. Option G is for a 60 mil thick synthetic liner for the
leachate collection pond.
