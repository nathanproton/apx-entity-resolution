The Board next considered the engineering proposals to
conduct a feasibility study for a regional landfill to serve
Appomattox, Buckingham, and Nelson Counties. A lengthy
discussion followed. Mr. Torrence made a motion to agree to
the study if the other jurisdictions participated. The
motion died for lack of a second.
Mr. Jamerson next asked the Board to consider a request from
the Central Piedmont Private Industry Council to renew their
lease to June 30, 1993. After some discussion, the Board
agreed by consensus to extend the lease to June 30, 1993 at
$285.00 per month and asked Mr. Rice to so notify the
Council.
