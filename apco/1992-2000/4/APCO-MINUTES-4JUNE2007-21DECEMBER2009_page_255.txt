On Monday, May 5, 2008 at 6:30 p.m. the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors
held their regular scheduled meeting at the Appomattox County Administration Building
located at 153A Morton Lane, Appomattox, Virginia.
Present: William H. Craft —- Appomattox River District
Samuel E. Carter - Courthouse District
Thomas H. Conrad — Falling River District
Russell H. Moore — Piney Mountain District
Gary W. Tanner — Wreck Island District
Also Present: Aileen T. Ferguson — County Administrator
John K. Spencer ~ Assistant County Administrator
Johnnie Roark — Planner
Vicky Phelps — Administrative Assistant
Absent: J.G. Overstreet — County Attorney
Chairman Craft called the meeting to order at 6:30 p.m. and called on Mr. Tanner to
deliver the invocation. Everyone joined in the Pledge of Allegiance to the American Flag.
Chairman Craft welcomed everyone to the meeting and then called for setting of the
agenda.
With no changes to the agenda, Chairman Craft proceeded to the Public Comment
Session. Hearing none, Chairman Craft moved on to the next item setting a date to hold a
public hearing on a proposed land use assessment ordinance.
Mr. Conrad stated that the Board needed someone to come and speak with the Board on
land use to give the Board a better understanding of what can be done. Mr. Conrad
suggested Mr. Tom Morelli from the Department of Taxation come and discuss this with
the Board. Mr. Conrad stated that this should be done prior to the Board holding a public
hearing.
Ms. Ferguson stated that she would give Mr. Morelli a call to see if he could attend the
May 19", Board meeting.
