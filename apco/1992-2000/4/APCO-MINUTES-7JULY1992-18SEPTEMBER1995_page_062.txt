Mr. Rice informed the Board Mr. Barry Sauls has examined the
boxwoods in front of the Courthouse and feels they have grown
too large for the root system to support. Mr. Sauls suggests
they be cut back or removed and replaced with smaller bushes.
After discussing the situation, Mr. Carwile made a motion
seconded by Mrs. Beasley to ask Mr. Sauls and his high school
horticulture class to help replace the boxwood bushes the
costs of the trees are to be borned by the County. Vote:
Mr. Jamerson, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr.
Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.
