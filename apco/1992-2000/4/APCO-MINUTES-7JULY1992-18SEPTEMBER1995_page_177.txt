Mr. Torrence asked to hear from anyone else favoring the petition.
Hearing no response, Mr. Torrence asked to hear from anyone opposed to

identified herself. She felt the additional traffic generated would
become a problem especially considering the close proximity of the High
School and Nursing Home. Ms. Chenault also was concerned about the

the need for an apartment complex that might lead to more guns, knives,
and drugs in the neighborhood; and asked Mr. Jamerson to reconsider.

Johnson agreed with the previous negative comments and also pointed out
the additional apartments would detract from the High School.
