Mr. Tanner made a motion to approve the Consent Agenda as submitted. Mr. Carter seconded
Mr. Tanner’s motion. Chairman Conrad called for discussion. Hearing none, Chairman Conrad
called for a Roll Call Vote: Mr. Carter, aye; Mr. Craft, aye; Mr. Conrad, aye; Mr. Moore, aye;
Mr. Tanner, aye.
Chairman Conrad presented the Informational Items and called for discussion or comments.
Hearing none, Chairman Conrad explained that a closed session is needed in accordance with
Section 2.2-3711(A)(7) Code of Virginia.
Mr. Overstreet explained that the purpose of this closed session is a follow up from the last
meeting regarding the water system and a draft letter written by Mr. Roark and himself, which he
would now present to the Board and have them read, if the Board members have no objection to
the letter being sent, the Board could authorize the Chairman to sign and send the letter out, and
a closed session would not be needed.
Mr. Tanner stated that this was a well written letter.
Chairman Conrad stated that he did not have a problem with sending the letter.
It was the consensus of the Board to have the Chairman sign and send out the presented letter.
Mr. Overstreet stated that this letter would now become material to be disclosed under the
Freedom of Information Act and stated that he would give the press a copy and ask him to please
hold until we are able to get this letter to Mr. Harvey, Town Mayor.
Chairman Conrad then called for Supervisor Concerns, hearing no Supervisor Concerns,
Chairman Conrad reminded the Board of the budget work session scheduled for April 21, 2009
at 6:00 p.m. at the County Administration Conference Room, and then reminded the Board of
other upcoming meetings scheduled for the month of May.
Hearing no further comments, Mr. Craft made a motion to adjourn the meeting at 6:48 P.M. Mr.
Carter seconded Mr. Craft’s motion. Hearing no discussion, Chairman Conrad called for a vote:
Mr. Carter, aye; Mr. Craft, aye; Mr. Conrad, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Tanner, aye.
