On Wednesday, April 21, 2009 at 6:00 p.m. the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors held a
special called meeting in the County Administration Conference room located on 153A Morton
Lane, Appomattox, Virginia 24522
Present: Mr. Thomas H. Conrad — Chairman ~ Falling River District
Mr. Samuel E. Carter — Courthouse District
Mr. William H. Craft -Appomattox River District
Mr. Russell H. Moore — Piney Mountain District (arrived at 6:30 p.m.)
Mr. Gary W. Tanner — Wreck Island District
Also Present: Aileen T. Ferguson — County Administrator
Vicky Phelps ~ Administrative Assistant
Mr. Johnnie Roark — Planner
Mr. John Spencer — Assistant County Administrator
Chairman Conrad called the special meeting of the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors to
order at 6:00 p.m. and turned the meeting over to County Administrator, Aileen Ferguson.
Ms, Ferguson began the budget work session with the proposed budget for the Utility
Department and stated that she had asked Mr. Roark to put together a budget for presentation.
Ms. Ferguson called on Mr. Roark to come and give the details of the Utility budget to the
Board.
Mr. Roark came forward and stated that he had come up with the figures for the utility budget
from the Preliminary Engineering Report, and the contract with Hurt & Proffitt for professional
services and basically used a forth of the year totals since we will not be up and running until late
FY2010. Mr. Roark stated that a vehicle and debt service had been included in this budget.
