Mr. Torrence called the meeting to order and on behalf of the Board of
Supervisors and Town Council, thanked the Industrial Site Review
Committee, for the work they had done. Mr. Torrence then asked the
Committee to review their findings and recommendations.
Mr. Paulette began by thanking the citizen members of the Committee for
all the time they committed to this project.
Mr. Paulette proceeded by explaining the background work that was done
by the Committee and presented a handout with the Committee’s
recommendation and a handout of the Committee’s review of other tracts
pros and cons. (Handouts on file in the August 1994 Board file.)
Mr. Paulette proceeded by stating that the Committee feels that an
Industrial Development Authority should be formed to proceed with a
more orderly development of an industrial development site.
A lengthy discussion followed with questions concerning the purpose of
an Industrial Development Authority, the cost of developing a site, etc
After discussion, it was agreed to schedule another joint meeting, the
second Monday of December, 1994, to review cost in acquiring land and
developing property and see if it would be feasible to continue with
this project.
