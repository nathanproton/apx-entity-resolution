After discussion, Mr. Carwile amended his motion to have tickets

The Board agreed to discuss procedure concerning scale tickets at the
next meeting.
Mr. Torrence next asked Mr. Peterson, Solid Waste Administrator to
appear before the Board.
Mr. Peterson presented the Board with bids for the Oakville and Vera
container sites. Mr. Peterson explained that the old Vera School will
have to be pushed down and then burned. Mr. Peterson also explained,
that the bids do not include the cost of gravel.
After discussion, Mr. Moore made a motion seconded by Mr. Paulette to
accept the bid on the Vera site of $18,957.00 and the Oakville site at
$19,957.00, from W. Frank Moore, Inc. Vote: Mr. Torrence, aye; Mrs.
Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; and Mr. Paulette, aye.
Mr. Carwile asked Mrs. Ferguson to check with BFI to see why they use
the small garbage containers rather than the roll off bins.
Mr. Peterson reviewed a memo that he had prepared for the Board giving
the background on the County Disposal System. After the review Mr.
Peterson requested a work session with the Board on the landfill
operation.
After discussion, the Board by consensus, agreed to meet on August 15,
1994 at 7:00 P.M. to discuss the landfill operation.
Mr. Torrence thanked and excused Mr. Peterson and called for a motion
to go into executive session with Bill and Ellen Jamerson to discuss a
legal matter.
At 8:20 P.M. Mrs. Beasley made a motion seconded by Mr. Paulette to
enter into Executive Session to discuss legal matters. VOTE: Mr.
Torrence, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; and
Mr. Paulette, aye.
On a motion by Mrs. Beasley seconded by Mr. Moore, it was resolved the
Board of Supervisors return to regular session at 9:18 P.M. VOTE: Mr.
Torrence, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; and
Mr. Paulette, aye.
