William H. Craft, Chairman
On Monday, July 21, 2008 at 6:30 p.m. the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors held their
regular scheduled meeting at the Appomattox Community Center, located on 220 Community
Lane, Appomattox, Virginia.
Present: William H. Craft - Appomattox River District
Samuel E. Carter — Courthouse District
Thomas H. Conrad — Falling River District
Russell H. Moore — Piney Mountain District
Gary W. Tanner — Wreck Island District
Also Present: Aileen T. Ferguson — County Administrator
John K. Spencer — Assistant County Administrator
J. G. Overstreet — County Attorney
Johnnie Roark — Planner
Vicky Phelps - Administrative Assistant
Chairman Craft called the meeting to order at 6:30 p.m. and called on Mr. Carter to deliver the
invocation. Everyone joined in the Pledge of Allegiance to the American Flag.
Chairman Craft welcomed everyone to the meeting and called for the setting of the agenda.
There were no additional items for the agenda.
Chairman Craft next called for citizen public comments.
Having no public comments, Chairman Craft presented Administrative Item #1 — Central
Virginia Community Services Board.
Chairman Craft stated that the Central Virginia Community Services Board is running in a deficit
this year and at the end of the year they were two thirds of a million dollars over the budgeted
amount, which is why the 28 day substance abuse program was cut. The substance abuse
program cost about one million dollars a year to administer and only brings in about $10,000 in
return. This was done to save other programs that were felt were more beneficial to all citizens.
Mr. Carter made a motion to approve the Central Virginia Community Services FY2009
Performance Contract as presented. Mr. Tanner seconded Mr. Carter’s motion. Chairman Craft
called for further discussion, hearing none, Chairman Craft called for a vote: Mr. Carter, aye; Mr.
Conrad, aye; Mr. Craft, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Tanner, aye.
