Next, Mr. Carwile addressed the Board concerning an excessive
noise ordinance and furnished a copy of Lynchburg’s ordinance
for reference. Mr. Carwile felt there was difficulty
controlling offensive and excessive noise in the County.
After discussing this issue, the Board by consensus asked Mr.
Rice to check with the Sheriff and County Attorney concerning
this problem and report back to the Board.

The regularly scheduled monthly meeting of the Appomattox
County Board of Supervisors was held in the Board’s Meeting
Room, Courthouse Square, Appomattox, Virginia, Monday,
November 9, 1992 at 7:00 P.M.
Present:
W. E. Jamerson, Chairman, Courthouse District
Dennis Torrence, Vice Chairman, Appomattox River Dist.
Nina Beasley, Falling River District
Frank Carwile, Wreck Island District
Russell Moore, Piney Mountain District
Gordon L. Rice, County Administrator
Mr. Jamerson, presiding, called the meeting to order at 7:00
P.M. The invocation was given by Supervisor Torrence.
