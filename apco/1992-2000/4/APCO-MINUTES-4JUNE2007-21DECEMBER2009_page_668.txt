Dr. Grasty explained that a new challenge they are facing this is being the Flu preparations and
then explained that the flu mist had been administered to 800 students in September, the HINI
vaccine was administered to 900 students in October; they are currently monitoring the absentee
rate daily; additional cleaning is taking place along with Nurses providing hygiene clinics to
students.
Dr. Grasty next discussed the CIP Projects that has been completed over the past two years. The
Appomattox Middle School Roof, trim and gutters at $55,000; the Appomattox County High
School football and practice fields at $350,000; the Appomattox County High School baseball
field at $215,000; the Appomattox Middle School football field at $12,000; and the two modular
classroom at the high school costing $96,000. Also, other capital improvements over two years
included the High School parking lot $13,000; Maintenance — two used trucks $14,000;
Maintenance-two trucks with service bodies $47,000; Maintenance-one used dump truck
$15,120; Transportation — two school buses $92,000; Food Services at Appomattox Primary
School — steamer $12,000; Scoreboards - $40,000; Video security systems - $130,000; Gym
Lighting- $7,000.
Dr. Grasty next explained the American Recovery and Reinvestment Act of 2009 (ARRA) and
stated that they would be applying for ARRA school construction bonds for energy efficiency
improvements to existing buildings or for the installation of renewable energy equipment on
school grounds. Dr. Grasty explained that the application is due on November 11, 2009. Some
items for the ARRA Energy Efficiency Bonds will be boilers for the Primary and Middle School;
occupancy sensors for lighting for all schools; air conditioning for the High School auditorium
and gas heating units for the High School.
Dr. Grasty explained the (PPEA) Public-Private Education Facilities and Infrastructure Act. She
stated that this is grants the School Board have authority to create as a public-private partnership
for development of projects in order to provide the projects in a timely or costly affective
fashion. She stated that more information will be coming on this by November 2, 2009.
