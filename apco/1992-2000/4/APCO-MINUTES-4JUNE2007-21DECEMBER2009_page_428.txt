Resolution
Emergency Operations Plan
WHEREAS the Board of Supervisors of Appomattox County, Virginia recognizes the need to
prepare for, respond to, and recover from natural and manmade disasters; and
WHEREAS Appomattox County has a responsibility to provide for the safety and well being of
its citizens and visitors; and
WHEREAS Appomattox County has established and appointed a Director, Deputy Director and
Coordinator of Emergency Management.
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the Board of Supervisors of Appomattox County,
Virginia, this Emergency Operations Plan as revised is officially adopted, and
IT IS FURTHER RESOLVED AND ORDERED that the Director of Emergency Management,
or his/her designee, are tasked and authorized to maintain and revise as necessary this document
over the next four (4) year period or until such time be ordered to come before this Board.
