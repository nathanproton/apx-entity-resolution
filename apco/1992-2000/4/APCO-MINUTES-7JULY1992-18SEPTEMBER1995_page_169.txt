Mr. Rice next informed the Board of the status of the Summer Youth
Employment Grant application after which the Board continued their work
on the proposed FY94 County budget.
The Board by consensus asked Mr. Rice to request the Sheriff revise his
proposed FY94 budget using the following suggestions:
1. include only Compensation Board approved positions.
2. limit salary increases to 3% effective 12/93.
3. limit new car purchase to three (3) and review FY93 budget to
see if two (2) cars can be purchased from FY93 funds.
4. delete purchase of computers since the Compensation Board has
recently approved reimbursement in FY93.
5. delete courtroom metal detectors; since purchase of these has
been included in the General Properties budget.
The Board next discussed the need to conduct a study to determine the
cost and requirements for additional jail space. After discussion, Mr.
Torrence made a motion seconded by Mr. Carwile to seek a proposal from
Architectural Partners of Lynchburg to prepare a feasibility study of
expanded jail space including cost estimates and to appropriate
$5,000.00 from unappropriated surplus to pay for the study. Vote: Mr.
Jamerson, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr.
Torrence, aye.
At 10:13 P.M. Mr. Jamerson with agreement: of the other Board members
