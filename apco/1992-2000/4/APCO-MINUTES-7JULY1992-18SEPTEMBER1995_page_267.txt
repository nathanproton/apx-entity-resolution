HEREAS, The Lynchburg Neighborhood Development Foundation (LNDF) ,

purchase counseling, and
WHEREAS, no financial obligation is being requested from any local
government or the Central Virginia Planning District Commission,
BE IT THEREFORE RESOLVED that the Appomattox County Board of
Supervisors does hereby adopt this RESOLUTION SUPPORTING the
regional loan application being submitted by the Lynchburg
Neighborhood Development Foundation, under the terms outlined
above.
