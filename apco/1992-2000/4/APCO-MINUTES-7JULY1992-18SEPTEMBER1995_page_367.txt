LOCAL EMERGENCY RESOLUTION
WHEREAS, the Board of Supervisors of the County of Appomattox does
hereby find:
1. That due to the heavy rains, freezing rain and sleet the
County of Appomattox and its citizens have sustained
considerable loss and damage to property; and
2. That due to the rain, freezing rain and sleet a
condition of extreme peril necessitates the proclamation
of the existence of an emergency;
NOW, THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY PROCLAIMED that an emergency now exists
throughout the County; and
TT IS FURTHER PROCLAIMED AND ORDERED that during the existence of said
emergency the powers, functions, and duties of the Director
of Emergency Services and the Local Emergency Planning
Committee of the County of Appomattox shall be those
prescribed by State Law and the ordinances, resolutions, and
approved plans of the County of Appomattox in order to
mitigate the effects of said emergency.
Vote: Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Paulette, aye; and Mr.
Torrence, aye.
Mr. Paulette made a motion to adjourn seconded by Mr. Moore. Vote: Mr
Moore, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Paulette, aye; and Mr. Torrence, aye
