On Monday, June 18, 2007 the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors and the
Appomattox County Planning Commission held a joint Public Hearing at 6:15 p.m. to
receive comments on the Proposed Amendment to the Telecommunications Facilities
Section of the Zoning Ordinance.

At 6:15 p.m. Mr. Carter opened the Public Hearing and stated the purpose of the Public
Hearing was to receive comments on the Proposed amendments to Article XX —
Telecommunications Facility Ordinance.
Mr. Carter then turned the meeting over to Mr. Roark.
Mr. Roark commented that these changes are necessary to synch up the Planned
Industrial Park section with the Telecommunications section. These changes will
increase opportunities for technology infrastructure (possibility of wireless broadband
extension) to be placed in the park and giving the County another tool by which to
market the park. Mr. Roark then read the following:
