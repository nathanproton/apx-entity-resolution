do the field work and then hearings must be held and the book finalized.
Mr. Mays said that Appomattox was growing at a rate of approximately
300 parcels per year and that there were approximately 8,500 parcels
now. Mr. Mays estimated the reassessment would cost approximately
$10.00 per parcel.
