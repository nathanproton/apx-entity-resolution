The regular meeting of the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors was
held on February 6, 1995 at 7:00 P.M. in the conference room of the
School Board Office, North Court Street, Appomattox, Virginia.
Present:
Philip E. "Gus" Paulette, Chairman - Courthouse District
Nina B. Beasley - Falling River District
Frank H. Carwile - Wreck Island District
Russell H. Moore - Piney Mountain District
Dennis W. Torrence - Appomattox River District
Also Present:
Aileen T. Ferguson - County Administrator
Thomas W. Lawson - County Attorney
Mr. Paulette called the meeting to order at 7:00 P.M. and asked Mr.
Torrence to give the invocation.
Mr. Paulette next called for additions or corrections to the minutes o
the recessed meeting of January 9, 1995 and the regular meeting of
January 3, 1995.
Mr. Carwile stated that in the January 3, 1995 minutes a word on page
needed to be corrected from "elevate" to "alleviate".
Hearing no other corrections, Mr. Paulette stated that the minutes
stand approved as submitted.
Mr. Paulette then called Ms. Sandra Lindsay, Virginia Department of
Transportation, Resident Engineer, to appear before the Board.
Ms. Lindsay began by saying she would like to schedule April 3, 1995 a
6:30 P.M. for the annual Secondary Road public hearing, if the Board
had no objection.
The Board set April 3, 1995 at 6:30 P.M. for their Annual Secondary
Road Public Hearing.
Ms. Lindsay next discussed the previous request for a traffic study at
the shopping centers. She requested the Chairman and the County
Administrator meet with Virginia Department of Transportation on
February 15, 1995 to discuss this problem.
Ms. Lindsay asked if anyone had concerns for her.
Mr. Torrence asked the status of the improvements to Route 701.
Ms. Lindsay stated there has been no further progress.
Being nothing further for Ms. Lindsay, Mr. Paulette thanked her for he!
report.
Mr. Paulette asked for Supervisor concerns.
