On Monday, June 4, 2001 at 6:00 P.M. the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors held a public
hearing at the Appomattox County Courthouse, Courthouse Square, Court Street, Appomattox, VA
24522.
PRESENT: _ Jerry G. Kendrick, Chairman - Appomattox River District
Samuel Carter - Courthouse District
Jesse Hancock - Falling River District
Frank H. Carwile - Wreck Island District
Russell H. Moore - Piney Mountain District
ALSO PRESENT:
Andy Carroll - County Administrator
At 6:05 P.M. Mr. Kendrick opened the Public Hearing and welcomed those in attendance. Mr.
Kendrick stated the public hearing was to consider the Fiscal Year 2002 Budget. Any one who
wished to speak for or against the Budget was to come forward, and give their name and address for
the record. This is not the proper forum for discussion or any arguments regarding the Budget.
There were no comments regarding the proposed County Budget for FY2002.
At 6:07 Mr. Kendrick closed the Public Hearing.
