Mr. Jamerson next’ called Mr. Bill Caldwell, Planning
Commission Clerk. Mr. Caldwell reported the Planning
Commission and Board of Zoning Appeals recommends approval of
Petition 92-163, 92-164, 92-165, 92-166, and 92-168. Mr.
Torrence made a motion seconded by Mrs. Beasley to approve
Petition 92-163 of Mr. Sammy Kress, Jr. The petitioner is
seeking a Conditional Use Permit to allow a home occupation
tanning booth in an accessory building to his main dwelling
on property owned and occupied by him that is zoned A-1 and
located on Route 647 between Route 648 and Route 606. Vote:
Mr. Jamerson, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr.
Moore,aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.
Mr. Moore made a motion seconded by Mr. Torrence to approve
Petition 92-164 of Mr. Richard James Purcell, Jr. The
petitioner is seeking a Conditional Use Permit to allow a
home occupation auto repair shop in an accessory building to
his main dwelling on property owned and occupied by him that
is zoned A-1 and located on Route 650 between Route 648 and
Route 606. Vote: Mr. Jamerson, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr.
Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.
Mr. Torrence made a motion seconded by Mr. Moore to approve
Petition 92-165 with proffers of Ms. Wanda Daley to allow the
installation of the second mobile home on her property that
is zoned R-1 and located on Route 727 between Route 604 and
Route 733. Vote: Mr. Jamerson, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr.
Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence, aye.
Mr. Jamerson questioned how Conditional Use Permits are
passed on from owner (s) to owner (Ss) if they were not
recorded on deeds and suggested they be required to be
recorded if they indeed were to go with the transfer of
property. A discussion followed. Mr. Rice and Mr. Lawson
were asked to verify if Conditional Uses passed from owner to
owner at sale of property.
Mr. Moore made a motion seconded by Mr. Torrence to approve
Petition 92-166 with proffers of Mr. Charles Hunter Phillips.
The petitioner is seeking a Conditional Zoning Permit which
would allow him to install a mobile home on his lot after
demolishing his existing log structure home. Mr. Phillips’
property is zoned R-1 and is’ located on Route 691 between
Route 719 and Route T691. Vote: Mr. Jamerson, aye; Mrs.
Beasley, aye; Mr. Carwile, aye; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence,
aye.
After discussion, Mrs. Beasley made a motion seconded by Mr.
Moore to approve Petition 92-168 of the Town of Appomattox
for a Conditional Use Permit to allow a 250’ high tower to be
constructed on the Town’s’ property located approximately 0.5
miles beyond Fairview Hill Subdivision at the Town’s water
tank site. Mr. Jamerson, aye; Mrs. Beasley, aye; Mr.
Carwile, nay; Mr. Moore, aye; Mr. Torrence, abstain.
