Mr. Moore, Vice-Chairman, presiding, called the meeting to order at
7:30 P.M.
Mr. Moore asked Bill Caldwell to read the first petition for comment.
Mr. Caldwell read petition number 94-218 of George Woodson and
Appomattox County to rezone property from R-1 to R-2 located on the
east side of Route 669 between Route 608 and Route 608 and being the
property divided by Eddie Gunter and others.
Mr. Moore asked if anyone wished to make comments for or against this
petition.
Mr. James Harmon came forward and give his address at Route 2, Concord,
Virginia. Mr. Harmon stated that he is against rezoning this property.
Mr. R. W. Childs, Concord, Virginia was recognized next and expressed
that he is opposed to rezoning this property from R-1 to R-2.
Ms. Annie Hunter, Route 2, Concord, Virginia spoke and expressed that
she is opposed to rezoning from R-1 to R-2.
Mr. Vincent Capobianco, Route 2, Concord, Virginia opposed the rezoning
