Having been duly advertised, the Public Hearing was called to order at
7:00 P.M. with Mr. Torrence, Chairman, pesiding.
Mr. Torrence stated the purpose of the hearing was to receive comments
on the County’s participation in Phase II of the ISTEA grant
application for the Driving Tour of the Route of Lee’s Retreat project
Mrs. Ferguson explained that ISTEA stood for the Intermodal Surface
Transportation Efficiency Act. Under the Act the Federal Government
has established funds for enhancement projects along highways.
Mrs. Ferguson continued by explaining that the maximum matching funds
required from Appomattox County for Phase II of this project would be
$8,300.00.
Mr. Torrence then asked for comments regarding this project.
