Mr. Paulette called the public hearing to order at 7:00 p.m. and stated
the purpose of the public hearing was to receive comments on proposed
zoning petitions.
Mr. Paulette then stated that petition number 96-289 of Grover and Nina
Beasley had been withdrawn.
Mr. Paulette asked Mr. Caldwell, Clerk to the Planning Commission to
read the first petition.
Mr. Caldwell read petition number 96-290 of Jeff L. Crawford for a
variance to Paragraph 170-81 of Appomattox County Zoning Ordinance
pertaining to location of Accessory Building. Property is Tax Map
Parcel #46(A)52 and located on Route 667 between Route 668 and Route
608. Property is zoned A-1.
Mr. Paulette asked if anyone wished to comment on this petition.
