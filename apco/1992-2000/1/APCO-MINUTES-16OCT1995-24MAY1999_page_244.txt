Room, Lynchburg, Va.
Present:
Garet K. Bosiger - Falling River District
Russell H. Moore, Vice-Chairman - Piney Mountain District
Frank H. Carwile - Wreck Island District
Absent:
Philip E. Paulette - Courthouse District

Also Present:
Aileen T. Ferguson - County Administrator

Mr. Earl Dickerson, Hurt & Proffitt, Inc., narrator of the meeting,
