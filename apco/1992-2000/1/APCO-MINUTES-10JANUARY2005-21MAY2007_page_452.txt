Mr. Moore made a motion, seconded by Mr. Craft, to approve amendments to Chapter
44 — Animals, Appomattox County Code, to update license tax amounts and add
language concerning the adoption of dogs from animal shelter.
o Section 44-2(A) adds separate tax for spayed and neutered dogs ($4.00) and increases
the tax for all other dogs from $4.00 to $6.00.
Section 44-2 (C) adds tax for kennels of thirty (30) dogs ($35.00)
Section 44-2 (D) adds tax for kennels of forty (40) dogs ($45.00)
Section 44-2 (E) amends the tax for kennels of fifty (50) dogs ($50.00)

$5.00 per day, deletes not to exceed five dollars ($5.00), amends tax for claiming an

first day, and deletes not to exceed ten dollars ($10.00)
