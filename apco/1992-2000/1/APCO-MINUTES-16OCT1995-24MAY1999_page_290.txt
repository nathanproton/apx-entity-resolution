Mr. Moore asked if anyone had any business to bring forth.
Mr. Bosiger asked if Mrs. Ferguson would contact Mrs. Joyce Jamerson to
check on the results of the violence survey requested by the Board.
Mr. Carwile stated he was very concerned about the drug situation in
the County and would like to suggest that Sheriff Staples set up an
informant fund that would encourage citizens to come forward with drug
related information.
After discussion, Mrs. Ferguson was to discuss this matter with Sheriff
Staples and report back to the Board.
Mr. Carwile also stated he had received numerous complaints about dump
trucks and tractor trailers on Route 611 and Route 608. Mr. Carwile
continued that he was going to look into what could be done to solve
this problem and would report to the Board at a later date.
