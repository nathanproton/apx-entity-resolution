At 7:00 p.m. on Monday, August 15, 2005, the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors
and the Appomattox Town Council held a Public Hearing in Council Chambers of the
Appomattox Town Office, located on Linden Street, Appomattox, VA.

Mr. Carter opened the public hearing and stated that the purpose of the public hearing
was to receive comments on the proposed updated Zoning Map for the Town of
Appomattox, which the Planning Commission intends to recommend for adoption, and
the Board of Supervisors and Town Council intends to adopt.
