At 7:00 p.m. Mr. Carter called the meeting to order and welcomed everyone in
attendance. Ms. Jones called the School Board to order.
Dr. Krug stated that Ms. Martin was not able to be present as her husband was ill and
was being admitted to the hospital. He thanked the Board of Supervisors and the School
Board for being able to work together, it is really wonderful and things have been very
productive.
Dr. Krug first discussed the school facility needs. He thanked the Board of Supervisors
for the use of a portion of the Carver-Price facility for the maintenance shop; the Home
Economics Cottage which they are using for technology personnel and plan to use for
more office space in 2005-06; they are also using some space for storage for the
auction.
