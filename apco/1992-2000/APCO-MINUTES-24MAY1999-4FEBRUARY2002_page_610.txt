Mr. Kendrick called the meeting to order and read the following statement.
The notice of the intention of the Board of Supervisors of Appomattox County, Va. to propose
for passage a resolution authorizing the issuance of up to $9,350,000, Appomattox County
General Obligation School Bonds for school purposes. This is for the new Appomattox
Elementary School now under construction.
The public hearing is the forum to state whether you are for or against this resolution. We would
prefer that you not discuss this with one another at this time.
Mr. Kendrick called for comments for this resolution.
Hearing none, Mr. Kendrick called for comments against this resolution.
Hearing none Mr. Kendrick declared the public hearing closed at 7:14 p.m.
